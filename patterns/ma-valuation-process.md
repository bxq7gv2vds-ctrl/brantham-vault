# Processus de Valuation M&A en 5 Étapes

## 🎯 Étape 1: Screening Initial & Initial Assessment (Jours 1-3)

### **Objectif**: Filtrer rapidement les targets potentiels

### **Screening Checklist**
- [ ] **Size criteria**: Revenue >$2M, EBITDA >$500K
- [ ] **Growth trajectory**: >15% YoY growth
- [ ] **Market fit**: Clear product-market fit
- [ ] **Team alignment**: Core team intact
- [ ] **Tech stack**: Modern, scalable architecture

### **Key Questions**
1. **Market position**: Leader dans sa niche?
2. **Revenue quality**: Recurring >20%?
3. **Exit viability**: Clear path to ROI?
4. **Strategic fit**: Alignement avec objectives?

### **Template d'Évaluation Rapide**
```markdown
## Quick Score Evaluation

| Criteria | Score (1-5) | Weight | Weighted |
|----------|-------------|--------|----------|
| Market Position | X | 20% | X.X |
| Growth Trajectory | X | 25% | X.X |
| Revenue Quality | X | 20% | X.X |
| Team Strength | X | 15% | X.X |
| Strategic Fit | X | 20% | X.X |
| **Total** | | **100%** | **X.X/5** |

**Decision**: [ ] Green Light (>3.5) [ ] Yellow Light (2.5-3.5) [ ] Red Light (<2.5)
```

## 🔍 Étape 2: Due Diligence Structurée (Jours 4-14)

### **Objectif**: Validation profonde des hypothèses initiales

### **Deep DD Checklist**
#### **Financial Due Diligence**
- [ ] 3 years P&L statements
- [ ] Cash flow analysis
- [ ] Debt covenant analysis
- [ ] Customer concentration (<20%)
- [ ] Working capital optimization

#### **Legal Due Diligence**
- [ ] Contracts review (key clients)
- [ ] IP assets audit
- [ ] Litigation status
- [ ] Regulatory compliance
- [ ] Employment contracts

#### **Tech Due Diligence**
- [ ] Architecture assessment
- [ ] Security posture
- [ ] Scalability analysis
- [ ] Integration complexity
- [ ] Maintenance costs

### **Risk Matrix Assessment**
| Risk Type | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|-----------|-------------|--------|-------------|
| **Revenue Risk** | Low | High | Diversify client base |
| **Tech Risk** | Medium | Medium | Migrate to cloud |
| **Regulatory Risk** | Medium | High | Compliance overhaul |
| **Team Risk** | Low | High | Retention plan |
| **Market Risk** | Low | Medium | Growth strategy |

## 💰 Étape 3: Valuation Multiple Approaches (Jours 15-21)

### **Objectif**: Établir une fourchette de valeur objective

### **Valuation Methods**
#### **1. Market-Based Valuation**
```markdown
**Comparable Analysis**
- Multiple Selection:
  - Revenue Multiple: 3-5x (SaaS)
  - EBITDA Multiple: 8-12x (Mature)
  - Revenue Multiple: 1-2x (Transactionnel)

**Peer Analysis**
- Public comps: X, Y, Z
- Private comps: A, B, C
- **Average multiple**: X.X
```

#### **2. DCF Analysis**
```markdown
**Assumptions**
- Revenue growth: [ ] 20% [ ] 25% [ ] 30%
- Margin expansion: [ ] 5% [ ] 10% [ ] 15%
- Discount rate: [ ] 12% [ ] 15% [ ] 18%
- Terminal multiple: X.X

**DCF Calculation**
- Year 1 Revenue: $X
- Year 5 Revenue: $Y
- Terminal Value: $Z
- Present Value: $TOTAL
```

#### **3. Precedent Transactions**
```markdown
**Transaction Analysis**
- Similar deals in sector:
  1. Deal 1: $ valuation, multiple X.X
  2. Deal 2: $ valuation, multiple X.X
  3. Deal 3: $ valuation, multiple X.X

**Benchmark**: Multiple range X.X - X.X
```

| Method | Base Case | Upside | Downside | Recommendation |
|--------|-----------|--------|----------|---------------|

## 📊 Étape 5: Final Valuation & Decision Making (Jours 29-35)

### **Objectif**: Validation finale et décision d'investissement

### **Final Valuation Summary**
```markdown
**Base Case Valuation: $5.5M**
- Market approach: $5.0M (0.9x weight)
- DCF approach: $6.0M (0.6x weight)  
- Precedent approach: $5.5M (0.5x weight)

**Adjusted Valuation: $5.3M**
- Growth premium: +$0.5M
- Risk discount: -$0.2M
- Synergy add-back: +$0.3M
- Control premium: +$0.4M

**Final Offer Range: $4.8M - $6.0M**
```
