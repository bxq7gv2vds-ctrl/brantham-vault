## 🔍 Analyse Synergies Détaillées

### **SaaS Sector - Synergies Type**
```markdown
**Revenue Synergies**
- Cross-selling: $200K-800K (25-40% of target revenue)
- Upselling: $150K-600K (15-30% of target revenue)
- Product bundling: $100K-400K (10-20% of target revenue)
- International expansion: $50K-200K (5-10% of target revenue)

**Cost Synergies**
- Sales team: $300K-1M (15-25% of target opex)
- Marketing: $200K-800K (15-25% of target opex)
- Technology: $100K-400K (10-15% of target tech costs)
- Admin: $50K-200K (10-15% of target admin costs)

**Timeline Synergies**
- Month 1-3: Quick wins (Legal, Financial)
- Month 4-6: Revenue synergies start
- Month 7-12: Cost synergies realized
```

```markdown
**Cost Synergies**
```

### **Synergy Implementation Timeline**
```markdown
**Phase 1: Quick Wins (0-3 months)**
- Legal integration

- Communication plan

**Phase 2: Revenue Synergies (4-12 months)**

- Product bundling
- Market expansion
```
