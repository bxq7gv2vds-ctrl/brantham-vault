# M&A Master Overview (MOC)

> Cartographie complète des pratiques M&A chez Brantham Partners. Méthodologie, outils, templates et best practices pour toutes les opérations d'acquisition.

## Domaines M&A

### 1. Deal Sourcing & Prospection
- **Stratégie**: Distressed M&A via LinkedIn, partnership networks, investment banks
- **Cibles**: PME/ETI <50M€, secteurs industrial & tech services
- **Outils**: Pappers API, scoring distressed, LinkedIn Sales Navigator

### 2. Due Diligence
- **Financial DD**: Template standardisé, BFR analysis, 13-week cash flow
- **Legal DD**: Procédures collectives, vérifications contentieux, compliance
- **Operational DD**: Supply chain, customers, team assessment
- **Commercial DD**: Market positioning, competitive analysis

### 3. Deal Structuring
- **Valuation**: Multiple approach, DCF adjusted for distressed, comparable transactions
- **Term Sheet**: Standard template with earn-out provisions, representations & warranties
- **Legal Structure**: SAS acquisition, SPV pour distressed deals

### 4. Post-Acquisition Integration
- **100-day plan**: Quick wins, integration roadmap, cultural alignment
- **Financial integration**: Consolidation reporting, cash flow optimization
- **Operational integration**: Systems merge, team rationalization

## Workflows

### Deal Flow Standard
```
Lead Qualification → DD Initiale → Term Sheet → Due Diligence Complète → Négociation → Closing → Integration
     (1 jour)        (3-5 jours)   (1-2 semaines)   (2-4 semaines)    (1-2 semaines)  (2-3 semaines)  (3-6 mois)
```

### Distressed Deal Special Flow
```
Signal Detection → Mandataire Contact → Urgent DD → Term Sheet Distressed → Closing Rapid → Stabilisation
      (24h)            (24-48h)       (3-7 jours)     (3-5 jours)       (1-2 semaines)  (1-3 mois)
```
