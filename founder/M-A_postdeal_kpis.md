## Governance: Deal Steering Committee

**Cadence:** Weekly (Days 1-30) → Bi-weekly (Days 31-100) → Monthly (Post-100)

**Attendees:** CFO, CTO, COO, target CEO/President, HR Lead, Legal

**Agenda fixed:**
1. Financial snapshot (5 min)
2. Top 3 risks / issues (10 min)
3. Synergy tracker (5 min)
4. Personnel/retention updates (5 min)
5. Decisions log (5 min)

---

## Red Flags Requiring Immediate Escalation

- Any customer >10% revenue churn unexpectedly
- Unplanned headcount loss of founder/CTO
- Discovery of material compliance issue
- System outage >4 hours
- Synergies realized <50% of plan by Day 60
