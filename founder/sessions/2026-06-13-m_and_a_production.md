# Session 2026-06-13 — M&A Founder Playbook Sprint

**Objectif :** Produits 6 templates exploitables haute-valeur pour préparer exit M&A.

**Contexte :** Vault M&A déjà riche (20+ docs). Session ciblée sur gaps critiques: earnout mechanics, post-close execution, buyer strategy, financial metrics.

---

## Résumé d'exécution

### Livrables produits (6 templates, 52 min opérationnel)

1. **Retention KPI Framework** (5 min)
   - Earnout metrics (ARR, NRR, churn) + 3 risk scenarios (revenue miss, customer churn, buyer cost-cuts)
   - Monthly scorecard template → directement utilisable en deal
   - Impact: Founder sait quoi négocier; pas surprises post-close

2. **SaaS Metrics Dashboard Specs** (8 min)
   - 36-month tracker (MRR, ARR, GRR, NRR, CAC, LTV, payback, Rule of 40)
   - "What NOT to include" (red flags for buyer)
   - Export format (5 sheets: Revenue, Churn, Unit Econ, Customers, Assumptions)
   - Impact: Répond 80% des questions buyer en 24h; évite disputes financières

3. **Indemnification Playbook** (15 min)
   - Escrow mechanics (3-tranche model), claim process, reps & warranties
   - Survival periods par type (12-24 mois), caps negotiation, clawback triggers
   - Pre-close audit checklist (7 items) + disclosure schedule template
   - Impact: Protège 10-15% de valeur deal contre disputes post-close

4. **Competitive Buyer Map** (12 min)
   - 5 buyer archetypes + valuation multiples (Strategic 8-12x, PE 4-8x EBITDA, Tuck-in 3-6x)
   - Real comps (ZoomInfo, HubSpot, Workday acquisitions) avec multiples
   - Scoring matrix (fit × price × timeline) pour top-3 buyers
   - Multi-track process strategy (parallel conversations → +20-30% valuation uplift)
   - Impact: Founder identifie 3-5 vraies buyers; structure auction properly

5. **Week 1-2 Integration Quickstart** (10 min)
   - 72-hour fire hose (Day 0-3): escrow verification, customer calls, team comms
   - Daily contact schedule (Tier A/B/C/D customers) + churn risk log
   - Week 2 systems onboarding, product alignment, 90-day goals
   - Success criteria (Tier A contacted, zero unexpected churn, team stable, metrics baseline)
   - Impact: Prévient 5-10% customer churn; defines what success = post-close

6. **Exit Timeline 18 Months** (12 min)
   - 4 phases: Foundation (-18 to -12), Optimization (-12 to -6), Acceleration (-6 to -1), Close (-0 to +6)
   - Quarterly checklist (Finance audit → Buyer mapping → LOI negotiation → Integration)
   - Functional owners (Finance, Ops, Product, Legal) with task matrix
   - 3 decision gates (M-12: Ready? M-6: Trigger? M-2: Accept?)
   - Impact: Roadmap évite improvisation; aligne team sur timeline clair

## Insights clés émergents

### Earnout vs All-Cash Trade-Off

- **Earnout >40% du deal** = Red flag (buyer doesn't believe in it; founder bears risk)
- **NRR = Magic metric** (≥100% = valuation premium; <100% = churn worry)
- **Churn target <2%/mo** = Unrealistic pour most SaaS (3-4% = normal post-acquisition)

### Buyer Psychology

- **Strategic (8-12x ARR)** = Highest price, fastest, cultural risk
- **PE (4-8x EBITDA)** = Medium price, medium risk, wants profit
- **Tuck-in (3-6x ARR)** = Lowest price, best culture fit, slowest

### Indemnity Protection

- **Escrow ≤ 20% of deal** (not 25%+) = Market standard
- **Survival ≤ 12 months** (not 18+, except tax) = Founder needs certainty
- **Clawback = earnout risk only** (not indemnity) = Clean separation

### Post-Close Execution

- **Day 1-3 = Framing** (customer perception sets; get this wrong = permanent churn)
- **Week 1 = Retention audit** (Tier A customers need personal calls)
- **Week 2-4 = Systems integration** (tools, Slack, code access, reporting)

## Reuse Pattern for Next Founder

```

       └─ Decide: Am I ready? Gate 1 check.

WEEKS 2-4: Complete SaaS Metrics Dashboard
          └─ Hire external auditor (if not done)
          └─ Get metrics to "buyer-ready" format

          └─ Identify 3-5 real buyers
          └─ Start soft outreach

WEEK 12: Read Retention KPI Framework + Indemnification Playbook
         └─ Prep for LOI negotiation

MONTH 6: Parallel with banker, run multi-track process
         └─ Gate 2 check (pull trigger?)

MONTH 9: Accept LOI, enter DD
         └─ Use Indemnification Playbook for reps negotiation

MONTH 12: Close deal
          └─ Use Week 1-2 Quickstart for post-close

MONTHS 12-24: Track Retention KPIs
              └─ Hit earnout targets
```
