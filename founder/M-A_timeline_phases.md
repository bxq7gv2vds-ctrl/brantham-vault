# M&A Timeline — Phases et Jalons Critiques

## Phase 1 : Sourcing & Screening (2-4 semaines)
- Identification cibles (IB, brokers, inbound)
- Preliminary NDA si nécessaire
- 1-page teaser (financials anonymisés)
- **Jalon:** Signature LOI (Letter of Intent)

---

## Phase 2 : LOI to Closing (8-12 semaines)

### Semaines 1-2: Initial Data Room
- Accès documents financiers/légaux
- Équipe interne + external advisors mobilisés

### Semaines 3-5: Active Due Diligence
- Financial audit, legal review
- Tech/ops/commercial diligence
- Buyer models (valuation refinement)

### Semaines 6-8: Definitive Agreements
- Drafting SPA (Stock/Asset Purchase Agreement)
- Négociation reps & warranties, indemnifications
- Financing committed (si relevant)

### Semaines 9-12: Final Closeout
- Regulatory approvals (si secteur régulé)
- Material adverse change (MAC) testing
- Stockholder/board vote
- Closing (funds wired, keys handed over)

---

## Phase 3 : Post-Close (100 jours critiques)
- Day 0: Announcement
- Days 1-30: Integration planning finalization, comms
- Days 31-60: System cutover, financial consolidation
- Days 61-100: Performance stabilization, synergy quick-wins

---

## Jalons critiques à ne pas rater
| Étape | Délai critique | Dépend de |
|-------|---|---|
| LOI signature | J+15 screening | Seller commitment |
| Data room ouverture | J+25 | Seller legal counsel |
| DD report draft | J+50 | Advisors availability |
| SPA signée | J+70 | Buyer legal review |
| Closing | J+90 | Regulatory/financing |
