# Strategic Framework

## Mission
Brantham Partners: sourcing and intermediation of M&A opportunities in French distressed companies (procedures collectives).

## Vision
Devenir le systeme nerveux du M&A distressed en France. Chaque PME viable en procedure collective a un repreneur identifie et contacte en 48h.

## Current Priorities (ordered)
1. Close first deal via Brantham pipeline (MLD deadline 17/03, or next high-score)
2. Fill pipeline to 5+ deals actifs (seuil score 65+)
3. Complete agent automation (Director -> full pipeline zero intervention)
4. Integrate swarm predictions into scoring ensemble (quant 70% + swarm 30%)
5. SEO machine: 10+ articles, 3-5 cas d'etude

## What We Are NOT Doing
- Weather Alpha (hobby, not priority)
- New projects before Brantham generates revenue
- Over-engineering memory/infrastructure
- External CRM (dashboard IS the CRM)
- SaaS/API before first revenue (intermediation first)

## Key Metrics
| Metric | Current | Target Q2 2026 | Target Q4 2026 |
|--------|---------|----------------|----------------|
| Deals in pipeline | 1 (MLD) | 5+ | 15+ |
| Deals closed | 0 | 1+ | 5+ |
| Revenue | 0 EUR | First fee | 300K+ EUR |
| Website pages | 19 | 40+ | 100+ |
| BODACC coverage | 2023-2026 | 2015-2026 | 2010-2026 |
| Swarm predictions | MVP (3 agents) | Production (25 agents) | v2 calibrated |

## Active Bets
- Automated agent pipeline reduces deal processing time from days to hours
- SEO on M&A distressed niche has low competition and high intent
- BODACC data + scoring algorithm creates information asymmetry
- Swarm intelligence (local LLM consensus) adds predictive edge on deal outcomes
- Speed-to-market (48h detection→contact) is an unbeatable moat
