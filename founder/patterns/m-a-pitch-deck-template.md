# Pitch Deck M&A Template

## Structure Complète

### 1. Cover Slide (1 slide)
- **Titre**: [Nom de la cible] - Acquisition Opportunity
- **Slogan**: [Phrase d'accroche 1-ligne]
- **Montant recherché**: [X€]
- **Votre logo + contact**

### 2. Executive Summary (1 slide)
- **Opportunity**: What/Why/When
- **Target company**: 2-liner description
- **Investment thesis**: Why this fits our strategy
- **Key metrics**: €€€ timeline
- **Ask**: What we need

### 3. Market Opportunity (2-3 slides)
- **Market size**: TAM/SAM/SOM
- **Growth drivers**: Why now
- **Competitive landscape**: Positionnement
- **Trends**: What's changing

### 4. Target Company Profile (2-3 slides)
- **Business overview**: Model, revenue, customers
- **Financials**: Revenue, EBITDA, margins, growth
- **Management team**: Key people, experience
- **Key metrics**: LTV/CAC, retention, etc.

### 5. Acquisition Rationale (1-2 slides)
- **Strategic fit**: How we complement each other
- **Synergies**: Cost/revenue projections
- **Market positioning**: Combined strengths
- **Competitive advantage**: What we gain

### 6. Financial Projections (2 slides)
- **Historical performance**: 3-5 years
- **Projected results**: 3-5 years post-acquisition
- **Synergy breakdown**: Where value comes from
- **Key assumptions**: What drives numbers

### 7. Deal Structure (1 slide)
- **Valuation methodology**: How we arrived at price
- **Offer terms**: Cash/stock considerations
- **Timeline**: Key milestones
- **Contingencies**: What's negotiable

### 8. Implementation Plan (1-2 slides)
- **Integration milestones**: 90/180/365 days
- **Key priorities**: What to focus on first
- **Resource allocation**: Budget, timeline, team
- **Risk mitigation**: How we'll handle challenges

### 9. Team & Track Record (1 slide)
- **Your team**: Relevant M&A experience
- **Previous deals**: Similar transactions
- **Advisors**: Legal, financial partners
- **Track record**: What you've delivered

### 10. Appendix (as needed)
- **Detailed financials**: Spreadsheets
- **Market research**: Data sources
- **Competitive analysis**: Detailed breakdown
- **Legal docs**: Excerpts if relevant

## Design Guidelines
- **Max 20 slides total**
- **One idea per slide**
- **Visual data over text**
- **Consistent branding**
- **End with clear next step**

## Content Tips
- Lead with the opportunity, not details
- Focus on strategic rationale
- Quantify synergies clearly
- Be transparent about risks
- Show urgency without pressure
