## HOW TO USE EACH FILE

### 1. Email Templates (`email_templates_m_and_a.md`)

**Format:** 3 email templates (cold outreach, objection, closing) + tips  
**Use case:** When you need to reach out to buyers or negotiate price  
**Action:** Copy-paste templates, personalize, send  
**Success metric:** 15%+ reply rate on outreach; counter-offer accepted

---

### 2. Buyer Tracking Sheet (`buyer_tracking_sheet.md`)

**Format:** Excel-ready table + scoring legend + red flags  
**Use case:** Manage active buyer pipeline (20+ prospects)  
**Action:** Copy to Google Sheets/Excel; update weekly  
**Success metric:** 5+ buyers scoring 75+ (competitive tension)

---

### 3. Financial Normalization (`financial_normalization_template.md`)

**Format:** 3 EBITDA scenarios + add-back framework + working capital formula  
**Use case:** Justify your valuation when buyer questions your price  
**Action:** Build conservative/base/aggressive EBITDA bridges; present to buyer  
**Success metric:** Buyer accepts ≥80% of adjustments; credible valuation

---

### 4. Competitive Response Playbook (`competitive_response_playbook.md`)

**Format:** 3-step protocol (align → prepare → respond) + sample response letter + tactics by buyer type  
**Use case:** When buyer submits LOI 20–30% below expectations  
**Action:** Follow 3-step framework; send response letter; deploy tactical moves  
**Success metric:** Move from opening offer to fair market value (+20–30%) in 2–3 rounds

---

### 5. Pre-Close Checklist 100 (`preclosing_checklist_100.md`)

**Format:** 12 sections × 100 items (legal, financial, HR, tech, ops, comms)  
**Use case:** Orchestrate final 21 days before closing  
**Action:** Assign owners per section; daily standups; track progress  
**Success metric:** Zero last-minute surprises; close on-time; funds received

---

### 6. Founder Transition Guide (`founder_transition_guide.md`)

**Format:** 10-point personal playbook + role decision matrix + transition covenant template  
**Use case:** Negotiate your post-acquisition role + maximize earnout  
**Action:** Choose Co-CEO vs Advisor vs Exit; negotiate in SPA; execute 100-day plan  
**Success metric:** Earnout ≥80% paid; team retained 95%; personal satisfaction 8/10

---

### 7. Customer Win-Back Kit (`customer_winback_kit.md`)

**Format:** 3 emails (D0, D7, D30) + FAQ + retention playbook by risk level  
**Use case:** Prevent customer churn post-close  
**Action:** Draft emails D90; send D0–D30; track churn weekly  
**Success metric:** Churn <2% (first 30 days); NPS stable or improving

**Use case:** Map integration risks; protect earnout; execute post-close

**Success metric:** Earnout achievable; team retained; zero critical escalations

## WHEN TO SKIP WHICH FILE

- **Skip Email Templates if:** You have your own outreach strategy (but read for benchmarking)
- **Skip Buyer Tracking if:** You have <5 qualified buyers (use once you hit 5+)
- **Skip Financial Normalization if:** Buyer accepts your price upfront (unlikely, but possible)
- **Skip Competitive Response if:** Your opening valuation is accepted (celebrate, but unlikely)
- **Skip Pre-Close Checklist if:** Deal falls through before D70 (but save for next deal)
- **Skip Founder Transition if:** You're doing a clean exit (no post-close role)
- **Skip Customer Win-Back if:** You have no material customer churn risk (<10% revenue concentration)
- **Skip Integration Risk if:** Keep-Separate model (no product integration planned)
