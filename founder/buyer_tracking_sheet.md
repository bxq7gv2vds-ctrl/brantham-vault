## SCORING LEGEND

| Score | Definition | Action |
|-------|---|---|
| 90–100 | **Hot buyer** — Urgent close signals, strong alignment | Daily contact, expedite closing |
| 75–89 | **Warm buyer** — DD underway, likely to close | Weekly check-ins, resolve blockers |
| 60–74 | **Lukewarm** — Interested, slow-moving | Bi-weekly follow-up, maintain optionality |
| 40–59 | **Cold** — Hesitant, multiple objections | Monthly touch, expand outreach |
| <40 | **Walk away** — Not serious, misaligned | Archive, don't waste time |

---

## STATUS DEFINITIONS

1. **Outreach** — Initial email sent, no NDA
2. **NDA Signed** — Can share teaser
3. **Teaser Sent** — Awaiting feedback/interest
4. **In DD** — Active data room access
5. **LOI Draft** — Negotiating term sheet
6. **LOI Signed** — Moving to closing
7. **Stalled** — No contact >2 weeks
8. **Dead** — Confirmed no interest / budget issue / passed

## RED FLAGS TO DETECT

| Red Flag | Risk | Action |
|---|---|---|
| No reply to 2 emails | Low intent | Archive after 10 days |
| DD takes >4 weeks | Slow decision-maker | Pressure with deadline |
| Multiple "final offers" | Indecision | Force binary choice ("yes/no by X") |
| CEO absent from calls | Org not aligned | Request CEO call or walk |
| Asking for exclusive | Deal risk | Refuse; counter "non-exclusive until LOI" |
| Wants aggressive earnout (>50%) | Low confidence | Counter with higher cash, lower earnout |
| Board delays >3 weeks | Org dysfunction | Flag as risk for post-close integration |

## STRATEGY MATRIX

**High Score + Long Timeline** → Pressure with other offers  
**High Score + Near Deadline** → Fast-track closing  
**Low Score + Multiple Buyers** → Use as negotiation lever  
**Stalled >2 weeks** → Re-engage with new data (earnings, product launch)
