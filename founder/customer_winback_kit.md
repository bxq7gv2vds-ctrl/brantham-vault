# Customer Win-Back Kit — Post-Close Communications

**When:** Immediately post-close (D0 onward)  
**Goal:** Prevent customer churn, address concerns, ensure loyalty through integration  
**Timeline:** Email 1 (D0), Email 2 (D7), Email 3 (D30)

---

## FRAMEWORK: 3-Email Sequence

### EMAIL 1: ANNOUNCEMENT (Send on Closing Day or D1)

**Subject:** [YourCo] is now part of [Buyer] — What's Next for You

**Audience:** All customers (email to entire customer base)

---

Dear [Customer Name],

Great news: [YourCo] is now part of [Buyer]! This is an exciting step for us and for you.

#### What This Means for You

**Your service is unchanged.** Your contract terms, pricing, and support team remain the same for the next [X months]. You'll continue to get the product you love with new superpowers from [Buyer].

**New capabilities coming:**
- Integration with [Buyer product #1] (available in [X weeks])
- [Buyer infrastructure] backing your data (more reliable, more secure)
- Access to [Buyer ecosystem] partners
- [Team, support availability], etc.

#### What to Expect Over the Next 30 Days

1. **This week:** You'll get access to [Buyer's support portal] (in addition to your current support)
2. **Week 2:** We'll host a [product roadmap] call with early customers (optional)
3. **Week 4:** New product features rollout (details to come)

#### Any Questions?

I know acquisitions can feel uncertain. We're here to reassure you:

- **Your data is safe** ([Buyer has SOC 2 Type II, GDPR-compliant infrastructure])
- **Your contracts are honored** (pricing locked in through [month/year])
- **Your success matters** (I'm personally invested in your account thriving)

Please reply to this email or book a call with [support person]: [link]

Looking forward to growing together.

Best regards,  
[Founder Name]  
[Title]  
[Contact]

## BONUS: FAQ for Customer Concerns

**Q: Will my pricing increase?**  
A: No, we're honoring all existing contracts through their renewal date. After renewal, pricing may adjust per normal business terms, but we'll give 90 days notice.

**Q: Is my data safe with [Buyer]?**  
A: Yes. [Buyer] is SOC 2 Type II certified, GDPR-compliant, and maintains separate databases for each customer. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

**Q: What if I'm not happy with [Buyer]'s changes?**  
A: We hear you. The core product remains the same. If you're unhappy within [90 days], we'll discuss a separation plan or transition assistance. No penalties.

**Q: Will [YourCo] team stay?**  
A: Yes, [Founder + key team members] are staying for at least 2 years post-close to ensure seamless integration. We're personally invested in your success.

**Q: What happens to my support team?**  
A: Your support team members are now part of [Buyer's] success organization. You'll have the same points of contact + escalation path to [Founder/Product Lead].

**Q: Will you continue shipping features?**  
A: Absolutely. We're shipping [X] features per month (up from [Y] before). New capabilities are coming to [roadmap areas].

**Q: What if I want to leave?**  
A: We hope you won't, but we understand. Standard contract termination applies (if any). We'll provide 30-day migration assistance if needed.

**Q: How do I report bugs or request features?**  
A: Same as before — [support link]. Response times are actually faster now (thanks to [Buyer] infrastructure).

**Q: Is this a conflict of interest for [Buyer]?**  
A: No. [Buyer] acquired [YourCo] to extend their capabilities to your vertical, not replace you. We're complementary, not competitive.

**Q: Will API keys / integrations break?**  
A: No, we're maintaining backward compatibility. We'll announce any changes 60 days in advance.

**Q: What about [specific concern]?**  
A: Let's talk in person. Book a call: [link]

## SAMPLE RETENTION PLAYBOOK (By Risk Level)

### HIGH-RISK CUSTOMERS (Top 3 accounts; up to 50% of revenue)

- [ ] Founder personal outreach (within 48h of close)
- [ ] Product roadmap call (week 1)
- [ ] Quarterly business reviews (locked in post-close)
- [ ] Early access to new features (beta program)
- [ ] Preferred support (direct access to founder/product lead)

### MEDIUM-RISK CUSTOMERS (Accounts 4–10; 20% of revenue)

- [ ] Founder email + follow-up call (week 1)
- [ ] Monthly product updates (email)
- [ ] Semi-annual business review
- [ ] Feature request prioritization (product backlog)

### LOW-RISK CUSTOMERS (Accounts 11+; 30% of revenue)

- [ ] Email announcement (send to all)
- [ ] Support ticket responsiveness (standard SLA)
- [ ] NPS survey (week 2–4)
- [ ] Win-back email if churn signals detected

## FOUNDER TALKING POINTS (If Customer Calls)

**"Why [Buyer]?"**  
"[Buyer's] infrastructure and network unlock [3-4 specific benefits]. We're stronger together, and you benefit immediately."

**"Will anything change?"**  
"Your contract terms stay the same. We're adding capabilities, not removing anything."

**"Can I leave?"**  
"We hope not, but standard contract terms apply. We'll work with you on any transition if needed."

**"What about pricing?"**  
"Locked until renewal. After that, we'll honor growth-based pricing if you want to expand usage."

**"Team changes?"**  
"I'm staying, your success team is staying. Integration happens at backend infrastructure level, not on your end."
