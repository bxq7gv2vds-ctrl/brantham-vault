# Integration Risk Matrix — Post-Acquisition

## Catégories & Mitigations

| Risque | Probabilité | Impact | Mitigation |
|--------|---|---|---|
| **Fuite clients clés** | Haute | Critique | • Contrats retention 12m avant close<br/>• Visites management<br/>• Garanties prix/service |
| **Key person departure** | Média | Critique | • Accord deferred comp (12-24m earnout)<br/>• Reporting à leadership <br/>• Retention bonus au close |
| **Choc culturel** | Média | Élevé | • Workshop intégration 30j avant<br/>• Leader bipartite au steering committee<br/>• Respect autonomie locale |
| **Double-counting synergies** | Média | Élevé | • Validation external advisor<br/>• Sequencing dependencies<br/>• Conservative 50% haircut |
| **Système IT incompatibles** | Média | Élevé | • Migration plan détaillée pre-close<br/>• Data audit (size, quality)<br/>• Dual-run 1-2 mois |
| **Compliance/audit findings** | Faible | Moyen | • Remediation escrow si material<br/>• Post-close insurance <br/>• Indemnification cap |
| **Leverage trop élevé** | Faible-Média | Critique | • Refinancing commitment pré-close<br/>• Covenant buffers (1.5x min)<br/>• Synergy realization KPI |

## Early Warning Signals (100 premiers jours)

| Signal | Fréquence check | Action |
|--------|---|---|
| Customer churn vs baseline | Hebdo | Si >5% hors-plan → exec meeting |
| Headcount attrition | Hebdo | Retention bonus review si >1% |
| System downtime | Jour | Escalade IT director si >2h |
| Synergy realization YTD | Bi-weekly | Replan si <70% trajectory |
| Employee NPS | J30, J60, J90 | Pulse si score baisses >10pts |

## Contingency Budget
- **10-15% du deal price** en cash holdback
- Escrow period: 12-18 mois
- Split common: 50% liability cap, 75% uncapped reps
