- Eliminate duplicate finance/HR/IT leadership
- **Example:** Seller CFO $300k + Buyer CFO overallocation → Save $200-300k/year
- **Timing:** Days 45-90
- **Confidence:** HIGH
- [ ] Org chart reconciliation
- [ ] Severance planning (if redundant roles)
- [ ] Estimate annual savings: $________

### 2. Procurement / Vendor Optimization
- Leverage combined scale with suppliers (software, services, raw materials)
- **Example:** Salesforce seat consolidation 50 + 30 → 70, negotiate volume discount
- **Timing:** Days 30-60
- **Confidence:** HIGH
- [ ] Vendor audit (top 20 spend)
- [ ] RFP for consolidated contracts
- [ ] Estimate annual savings: $________

### 3. Real Estate / Facilities
- Consolidate offices (if geographically close)
- **Example:** 2 leases $500k/year → 1 lease $600k, save net $400k
- **Timing:** Post-100 days (renegotiate leases)

### 4. Technology Stack Rationalization

- **Example:** $2 SaaS tools × $50k/year = $100k saved, 1-time migration $200k
- **Timing:** Days 60-120
- **Confidence:** MEDIUM (execution risk)

- [ ] Consolidation roadmap
- [ ] Estimate annual savings: $________
- [ ] **One-time cost to migrate:** $________

### 5. Manufacturing / Supply Chain (if applicable)
- Consolidate production facilities, warehouses
- Shared logistics optimization
- **Confidence:** MEDIUM-LOW (capital heavy)
- [ ] Estimate annual savings: $________

## Golden Rules

1. **50% haircut rule:** If claiming $2M synergies, assume $1M will realize (execution risk)
2. **Attribution:** Only count synergies unique to this deal, not standalone initiatives
3. **Timing:** Be conservative on ramp—expect 30% Y1, 70-80% Y2
4. **Milestones:** Tie synergy realization to steering committee KPIs (monthly tracking)
5. **Avoid:** Double-counting (e.g., same cost reduction claimed in cost + revenue)

---

## Due Diligence Validation

**Pre-LOI:** Present synergy thesis to seller for credibility.  
**Pre-Close:** Finalize synergy plan, get CFO/COO alignment on execution.  
**Post-Close:** Track monthly vs. plan, escalate variance >20%.
