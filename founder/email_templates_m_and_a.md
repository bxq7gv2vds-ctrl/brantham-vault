# M&A Email Templates — Production Ready

## Template 1: OUTREACH (Cold Buyer)

**Subject:** [YourCo] - Strategic opportunity for [Buyer vertical]

---

Dear [Buyer CEO/BD],

We're [YourCo], and we've built the leading [product category] for [vertical].

Last year we helped [key metric]: 2,000+ teams, $[X]M ARR, [NPS score]. We're now exploring strategic partnerships with companies like [Buyer], where we see natural product synergies.

Would you be open to a brief call? I'll send a confidential overview after NDA.

Best,  
[Founder name]

---

**Send date:** Monday 9am (not Friday)  
**Personalization:** Mention 1 recent buyer acquisition/product launch  
**Follow-up:** Resend after 5 days if no reply  
**Success rate:** 12-18% reply (top buyers: 25-35%)

---

## Template 2: OBJECTION HANDLING (Price)

**Subject:** RE: [YourCo] — On valuation

---

Hi [Buyer name],

Thanks for the offer at $[X]M. I appreciate the clarity.

Given our growth trajectory ([% YoY], path to [milestone]), we believe the fair range is $[X] to $[Y]M, which reflects:

- **Revenue:** $[X]M ARR, +[Y]% YoY (Top 5% in [category])
- **Retention:** [X]% net dollar; customers stay for [Y] years
- **Synergies:** We see $[Z]M cost-of-goods synergy ([details])
- **Market:** [Comparable recent exit] at [multiple]x revenue

Assuming DD confirms our metrics, could we reset expectations to [$X–$Y]M? That gives you the margin you need and values us fairly.

Let me know your thoughts.

Best,  
[Founder]

---

**Tone:** Analytical, not emotional  
**Length:** Under 150 words  
**Attachment:** 1-pager with 3 comps + synergy math  
**Goal:** Create optionality (reopen conversation, not walk away)

---

## Template 3: CLOSING (Deal Signal)

**Subject:** [YourCo] closing — next steps

---

Dear [Board/Buyer team],

We're pleased to move into closing. To keep us on track for [target close date], we'll need:

**By [DATE]:**
- Final signed SPA + exhibits
- EBC/proof of funds confirmation
- Vendor/customer consents list

**By [DATE+5]:**
- LC/escrow account details
- Closing checklist completion
- Tax clearance certificates

**On [CLOSE DATE]:**
- Wire transfer
- Instrument delivery
- Post-close onboarding kickoff

Please confirm feasibility and assign owner per category.

Best regards,  
[Founder]

---

**Tone:** Professional, deadline-driven  
**Audience:** Buyer CFO, Legal, CEO  
**Goal:** Lock commitments (no drift)  
**When to send:** After SPA signed (D90 typically)

---

## BONUS: Earnout Kickoff Email

**Subject:** [YourCo] earnout program launch

---

Hi [Buyer CEO],

Great to be part of [Buyer]. Per our SPA, earnout payout is based on:

- **Metric 1:** Customer retention ≥ [X]%
- **Metric 2:** ARR growth ≥ [Y]%
- **Measurement:** Quarterly review (next: [date])

I'm attaching the measurement protocol [PDF] so we're aligned on audit/reporting.

Let's sync on KPIs weekly in [channel].

Cheers,  
[Founder]

---

**Tone:** Collaborative, transparent  
**Attachment:** Earnout spec from SPA  
**Cadence:** Weekly sync (first 90 days), then monthly

---

## TIPS

1. **Outreach:** Generic = 0% reply. Specific (1-2 recent exits/products) = 15%+
2. **Objections:** Never say "no." Counter with data + optionality.
3. **Closing:** Create external pressure (other bidders, deadline).
4. **Earnout:** Weekly syncs build trust (easier to pay out).

**Success metric:** Response rate ≥ 15% (outreach), counterproposal accepted (objection), close on-time (closing).
