## NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT

**Effective Date:** [DATE]

**Between:**  
**Disclosing Party:** [Target Company Name], a [State] corporation ("**Seller**")  
**Receiving Party:** [Buyer Company Name], a [State] corporation ("**Buyer**")

---

### 1. Purpose
Buyer and Seller are exploring a potential business transaction. Seller will disclose to Buyer certain confidential financial, operational, and strategic information (the "**Confidential Information**") to facilitate this evaluation.

---

### 2. Definition of Confidential Information
"Confidential Information" means all non-public information relating to Seller's business, including but not limited to:
- Financial statements, projections, tax returns
- Customer lists, contracts, pricing
- Product roadmap, technical specifications
- Personnel information, compensation
- Strategic plans and market analyses

**Excludes:**
- Information already public or independently developed
- Information received from third party without restriction
- Information required to be disclosed by law (with prior notice to Seller where possible)

---

### 3. Obligations
Buyer will:
- Restrict access to Confidential Information to employees, advisors (bankers, lawyers, accountants) with a need to know
- Use Confidential Information **solely** to evaluate the transaction
- Maintain strict confidentiality using reasonable care (not less than standard used for own confidential information)
- Return or destroy all Confidential Information upon request or termination

---

### 4. No License or Obligation to Purchase
Disclosure grants no license or rights to Confidential Information. Neither party is obligated to proceed with any transaction.

---

### 5. Term
This NDA applies:
- **During evaluation:** From date above until transaction closes or 12 months, whichever is earlier
- **Post-closing:** Confidentiality survives indefinitely
- **If no deal:** Confidentiality obligations survive 3 years

---

### 6. Permitted Disclosures
Buyer may disclose Confidential Information if required by:
- Court order or regulatory demand (with 5 days' prior notice to Seller to seek protective order)
- Stock exchange filing or securities law (with prior notice to Seller to redact)

---

### 7. Remedies
Buyer acknowledges that breach would cause irreparable harm. Seller is entitled to injunctive relief in addition to damages.

---

### 8. Governing Law
This Agreement is governed by the laws of [State], without regard to conflict of laws.

---

### 9. Miscellaneous
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire understanding; supersedes all prior discussions
- **Amendment:** Only by written consent of both parties
- **Severability:** If any provision is unenforceable, remainder remains in force
- **Assignment:** Neither party may assign without written consent
