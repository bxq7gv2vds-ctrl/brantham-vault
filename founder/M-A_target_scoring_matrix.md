# Target Scoring Matrix — Pondération et Ranking

| Critère | Poids | 1★ | 2★ | 3★ | 4★ | 5★ |
|---------|-------|-----|-----|--------|--------|---------|
| **Croissance revenue** | 20% | <5%/an | 5-10% | 10-20% | 20-40% | >40%/an |
| **Rentabilité (EBITDA %)** | 15% | <0% (loss) | 0-5% | 5-15% | 15-25% | >25% |
| **Concentration client** | 15% | >50% top 1 | 30-50% | 20-30% | 10-20% | <10% |
| **Market position** | 15% | #5+ | #3-4 | #2 | #1 | #1 + narrow moat |
| **Synergie potentielle** | 15% | 0-5% | 5-10% | 10-20% | 20-35% | >35% |
| **Valuation (vs EBITDA)** | 10% | >12x | 10-12x | 8-10x | 6-8x | <6x |
| **Risque intégration** | 10% | Très élevé | Élevé | Modéré | Faible | Minimal |

## Scoring Exemple

**Cible A (SaaS B2B):**
- Croissance: 35%/an → 5★ (20 pts)
- EBITDA: 18% → 4★ (15 pts)
- Clients: 8% top 1 → 5★ (15 pts)
- Marché: #2 → 4★ (12 pts)
- Synergies: 25% → 4★ (12 pts)
- Valuation: 8.5x → 4★ (4 pts)
- Risque: Modéré → 3★ (3 pts)

**Total: 81/100** → Achat recommandé

---

**Decision rule:**
- ≥80 pts: Go
- 70-79 pts: Due diligence approfondie
- 60-69 pts: Passe ou restructure termes
- <60 pts: Passe
