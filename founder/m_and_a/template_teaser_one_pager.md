# Teaser One-Pager Template

**Purpose:** Send via email after initial warm intro. No identity yet. Tests buyer interest before detailed NDA.

**Format:** 0.5 page max, unbranded, no logo, text only.

---

## TEMPLATE

```
CONFIDENTIAL - FOR DISCUSSION PURPOSES ONLY

ACQUISITION PARTNERSHIP OPPORTUNITY

PROFILE:
- SaaS company, Series [X] funded / bootstrapped
- $[X]M ARR, [Y]% YoY growth
- [Z] customers in [market segment]
- Founded [year], team of [size]

THE PROBLEM WE SOLVE:
Your [customer segment] needs [pain point]. 
Current solutions [why they fail]. 
We built [solution] to [outcome].

TRACTION:
- [Customer Name 1] (enterprise customer, $[amount]/year)
- [Customer Name 2] (growth segment, $[amount]/year)
- [Customer Name 3] (sample, $[amount]/year)
- NRR: [X]%, Churn: [Y]%/month
- Gross margin: [Z]%

WHY NOW:
- [Market signal 1: customer demand / competitive pressure]
- [Market signal 2: window closing in 12 months]
- [Market signal 3: team availability]

NEXT STEP:
If interesting, happy to share a detailed confidential 
information memorandum. Requires quick NDA (5 min).

---
Contact: [Your name]
[Email]
[Phone]
```

---

## CUSTOMIZATION CHECKLIST

- [ ] Do NOT name your company (test genuine interest)
- [ ] Do NOT show logo or branding
- [ ] Do NOT include deck or links
- [ ] Do NOT list all customers (top 3 only)
- [ ] Do NOT give exact revenue (round to nearest $500K)
- [ ] Do NOT mention competitor names
- [ ] Do NOT ask for meeting (ask if interested first)

---

## SEND INSTRUCTIONS

**Timing:** Send 24 hours after warm intro call / discussion.

**Channel:** Email only (trackable).

**Subject line:**
```
Confidential - Acquisition Partnership Discussion
```

**Body:**
```
Hi [Name],

Following up on [Introducer]'s call yesterday — 
here's the teaser for the opportunity we discussed. 

If it looks interesting, happy to dig deeper.

[Paste teaser text]
```

**Expect response:** 3-7 days. If no response, assume "no" and move to next buyer.

---

## RED FLAGS IN RESPONSE

- **"Interesting but we're not buying right now"** → Move on (low signal)
- **"Love it, but need to understand more"** → Send NDA + full financials (hot signal)
- **"Can we call tomorrow?"** → They want to learn more (medium signal → schedule call)
- **"Forwarding to [internal stakeholder]"** → Good signal if stakeholder has budget
