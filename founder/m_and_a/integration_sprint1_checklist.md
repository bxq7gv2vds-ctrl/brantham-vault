# Integration Sprint 1 — Days 1-30

**Role** : Founder owns this sprint. Buyer's integration manager supports.

---

## Day 1 (Closing Day)

- [ ] All bank wires cleared, funds received?
- [ ] Announce to team (all-hands): "We've been acquired by [Company]. Here's what happens next."
- [ ] Announce to customers: "Exciting news! We've joined [Buyer]. Service uninterrupted. New contact: [name]."
- [ ] Email key customers (top 10): Personal note from founder. Emphasize: continuity, new features coming, dedicated support.
- [ ] Buyer's CEO meets your team (kickoff call, culture intro).

---

## Days 2-5: Customer Introductions

- [ ] Founder introduces buyer's account executives to top 10 customers (video calls).
- [ ] Prepare "customer success handoff" doc: 1-pager per customer (usage, risks, opportunities).
- [ ] Buyer assigns dedicated account manager to top 3 customers.
- [ ] Zero churn target: All introduced customers = committed to stay.

---

## Days 6-15: Technical Integration Planning

- [ ] CTO/Engineering: Auth systems merge? (SSO, user provisioning)
- [ ] Data migration plan: Timeline, testing, rollback procedure.
- [ ] API integration: Buyer's product ↔ Your product? (if applicable)
- [ ] Confirm: No overlapping customers? Pricing conflict?
- [ ] Uptime SLA: Agreed? Communicated to customers? Monitored?

---

## Days 16-30: Early Wins & Momentum

- [ ] Release 1 new feature jointly (buyer + your team). Shows momentum to customers.
- [ ] NPS survey (quick 2-question): Are customers happy post-acquisition?
- [ ] Identify 3 "quick wins" for integration (bundled offers, shared feature, cost savings).
- [ ] Weekly syncs (Founder + Buyer integration lead): Issues, blockers, progress.
- [ ] Retention report: Zero churn? Any at-risk customers flagged?

---

## Success Metrics (Sprint 1)

| Metric | Target | Owner |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Customer churn | 0% | Founder |
| NPS change | Flat or +2pts | Founder |
| New feature launch | 1+ | Engineering |
| Team satisfaction | No departures | HR/Founder |
| Data migration | 100% complete | CTO |

**If any metric misses** : Escalate to integration lead, adjust playbook.

---

## Red Flags (Trigger Remediation)

- Customer asks about data privacy (post-acquisition).
  → **Action** : Reassurance email + Data Processing Agreement signed within 48h.

- Employee considering leaving.
  → **Action** : Retention offer + role clarity conversation within 24h.

- Customer receives error / product breaks.
  → **Action** : Fix within 4h, customer apology within 8h.

- Buyer missed promised integration deadline.
  → **Action** : Escalate, adjust timeline, communicate to team.

---

## Founder's Time Commitment

- **Day 1-5** : 40h (customer intros, team comms)
- **Day 6-15** : 20h (planning, tech calls, removal of blockers)
- **Day 16-30** : 15h (weekly syncs, early wins, churn monitoring)
- **Total Month 1** : ~75h (full-time for 2 weeks, then 15h/week for week 3-4)

**After Day 30** : Transition to 10-15h/week for months 2-3 (earnout KPIs + integration supervision).

---

## Handoff Check (Day 31)

- [ ] All customers introduced and assigned account managers?
- [ ] Zero churn confirmed?
- [ ] Data migrated + tested?
- [ ] Team onboarded to buyer's systems (tools, processes)?
- [ ] Earnout KPI dashboard live + tracking?
- [ ] Founder role clarified for next 12 months?

**If all ✓** : Integration sprint 1 = success. Move to Sprint 2 (long-term integration).
