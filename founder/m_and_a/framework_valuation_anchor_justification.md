# Valuation Anchor Justification — Defend Your Ask

**Use this framework to defend your price before first LOI. Anchor high; negotiate down.**

---

## THE 3-PILLAR APPROACH

### Pillar 1: DCF (What Are You Worth Fairly?)

**Formula:** PV of 5-year revenue stream + terminal value.

**Quick version:**
- Year 1 revenue: $[ARR] × (1 + growth %)
- Years 2-5: Assume [X]% annual growth (decelerate over time)
- Year 5 terminal value: Revenue × [multiple based on company type]
- Discount by [10-15%] (WACC for SaaS)

**Example:** 
- $2M ARR, 40% growth, assume deceleration to 20% by Year 5
- Year 1: $2.8M | Year 2: $3.4M | Year 3: $4.1M | Year 4: $4.9M | Year 5: $5.9M
- Terminal value (Year 5): $5.9M × 6x = $35.4M
- Discount to present (10% rate): ~$20M

**Your DCF Value:** $____M

---

### Pillar 2: Comps (What Did Similar Companies Sell For?)

**Find 3-5 comparable acquisitions in your space:**

| Company | ARR (at sale) | Price | Multiple | Year |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| [Comp 1] | $XM | $YM | 10x | 2023 |
| [Comp 2] | $XM | $YM | 8x | 2024 |
| [Comp 3] | $XM | $YM | 7x | 2024 |
| **Average** | — | — | **8.3x** | — |

**Your comp-based value:** Your $ARR × 8.3x = $____M

**Where to find comps:**
- Crunchbase, Dealroom, PitchBook (search "acquisitions" + your market)
- Recent press releases (search "[Your Market] acquisition")
- Your investor's portfolio (ask for sample deals)

---

### Pillar 3: Strategic Premium (What's the Synergy Worth?)

**Add 20-50% to comps if you solve a strategic problem:**

| Signal | Premium |
|---|---|
| Fills product gap for buyer (< 12 mo to build) | +20-30% |
| Avoids competitive threat (prevents rival from buying you) | +30-40% |
| Accelerates buyer's growth roadmap (TAM expansion) | +25-35% |
| Defensive (acquires talent to prevent competitor hiring) | +15-25% |

**Example:**
- Comps baseline: 8x ARR = $16M
- Strategic premium: Fills HubSpot's product gap (+30%) = +$4.8M
- Your ask: $16M + $4.8M = **$20.8M**

## HOW TO DEFEND YOUR ASK IN LOI

**Buyer:** "Your ask is too high. Market pays 6x for SaaS."

**You:** "Agree — market average is 6-8x. We're asking 8x because:
- [DCF shows $20M intrinsic value]
- [Comparable companies sold at 8-10x]
- [Strategic synergy worth $X to you specifically (close product gap)]

Our 8x is fair. Happy to discuss structure: [upfront] + [earnout] + [retention bonus]."

## STRUCTURE OPTIONS (When Buyer Balks at Price)

If buyer won't hit your number, offer:

**Option A: Higher Earnout**
- Your ask: $20M upfront
- Buyer: "Best we can do is $15M"
- Compromise: $13M upfront + $7M earnout (24m, revenue-based)
- Your real value: $20M if earnout hits

**Option B: Founder Carry Note**
- Your ask: $20M upfront
- Buyer: "Can't get board approval for $20M cash"
- Compromise: $15M upfront + $5M seller note @ 4% (48-month amortization)
- You get cash flow + security

---

## RED FLAGS: DON'T ACCEPT

- ❌ Buyer offers 50% earnout contingent on buyer's uncontrollable metrics (unfair)
- ❌ No earnout achievement roadmap (buyer can manipulate)
- ❌ Earnout period > 24 months (buyer drags feet, kills earnout)
- ❌ Price < your DCF floor (you lose money)
