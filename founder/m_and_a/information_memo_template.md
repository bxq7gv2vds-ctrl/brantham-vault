## Use Cases

### Soft-Shop (Passive Contact)
- **Who gets this:** 3-5 strategic buyers + 1-2 PE firms you hand-select
- **Timing:** Send after 15-min "exploratory call"
- **Message:** "Quick overview for your evaluation; happy to dive deeper if there's interest"

### Pre-LOI (Active Deal)
- **Who gets this:** Serious buyer who's past initial screening
- **Timing:** During data room setup
- **Updates:** Keep 2-3 versions (initial, post-Q1 results, pre-close updated)

### Board/Investor Alignment
- **Use internally** to confirm positioning with board before going to market
- **Get feedback** on value drivers, message clarity

## Production Checklist

- [ ] Metrics verified with finance (no made-up numbers)
- [ ] Competitive positioning fact-checked (not aspirational)
- [ ] Growth projections are conservative (don't over-promise)
- [ ] Risks disclosed honestly (missing this kills credibility)
- [ ] Appendices ready (customer list, financials, org chart)
- [ ] Design is clean (not fancy; use template or hire designer $2-5k)
- [ ] Proof-read 3x (typos scream "amateur")
- [ ] NDA/confidentiality language included
- [ ] Length is <5 pages (longer = not read)
- [ ] M&A advisor reviewed before sending to buyer
