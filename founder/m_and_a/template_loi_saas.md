## Annotations for Negotiation

### What to Push Back On
1. **Reps cap >1.5%** → Counter: "Market standard is 0.5-1.0%, our clean audit supports 0.5%"
2. **Survival >18m** → Counter: "Standard SaaS is 12-18m; propose 12m for top reps, 18m for indemnification"
3. **Earnout >30%** → Counter: "Control is better; propose 20% max, tied to conservative target"
4. **Founder non-compete >2y** → Counter: "Willing to 1 year in-market, 2y non-compete in strictly non-competing space"
5. **Break-up fee** → Counter: "Ask for specificity: 3% if Buyer terminates, 0% if Seller walks due to material change"

### Red Flags in Buyer's LOI
- [ ] No earnout detail = buyer planning to pay less later
- [ ] MAC clause too broad = buyer has exit option
- [ ] Financing contingency = deal not real
- [ ] Founder non-compete >18m = buyer fears founder restart
- [ ] No timeline = slow buyer = stalling tactic
