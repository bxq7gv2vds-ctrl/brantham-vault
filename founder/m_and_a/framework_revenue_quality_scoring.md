# FRAMEWORK: REVENUE QUALITY SCORING

**Not all revenue is equal. Buyers value recurring, low-churn, expansion revenue more than one-time services. Score your revenue quality.**

---

## THE 10-POINT REVENUE QUALITY SCORECARD

| Item | Poor (0 pts) | Fair (1 pt) | Good (2 pts) | Excellent (3 pts) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Recurrence** | One-time sales | 50% recurring | 80% recurring | **95%+ recurring** |
| **Churn Rate** | >10% MoM | 5–10% MoM | 2–5% MoM | **<2% MoM** |
| **NRR (Net Rev Retention)** | <100% (declining) | 100–105% | 110–120% | **>120%** |
| **CAC Payback** | >24 months | 18–24 months | 12–18 months | **<12 months** |
| **Top 5 Customers** | >50% of ARR | 40–50% of ARR | 30–40% of ARR | **<30% of ARR** |
| **Contract Length** | <1 year (month-to-month) | 1–2 year avg | 2–3 year avg | **3+ year avg** |
| **Gross Margin** | <50% | 50–65% | 65–75% | **75%+** |
| **LTV:CAC Ratio** | <1:1 | 2:1–3:1 | 4:1–5:1 | **6:1+** |
| **Expansion Velocity** | <5% MoM expansion | 5–10% MoM | 10–15% MoM | **15%+ MoM** |
| **Customer Sentiment** | NPS <20 | NPS 20–40 | NPS 40–60 | **NPS 60+** |

---

## SCORING & INTERPRETATION

**Total Score: [0–30 points]**

| Score | Revenue Quality | Typical Multiple | Example Valuation |
|---|---|---|---|
| **0–5** | Poor | 3–4x | $[30]M on $[10]M ARR |
| **6–15** | Fair | 5–6x | $[50–60]M on $[10]M ARR |
| **16–22** | Good | 7–8x | $[70–80]M on $[10]M ARR |
| **23–30** | Excellent | 9–10x | $[90–100]M on $[10]M ARR |

**Higher revenue quality = higher valuation multiple.**

---

## EXAMPLE: YOUR COMPANY SCORES 25 POINTS

| Item | Score | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| 95% recurring revenue | 3 | SaaS; all subscription-based |
| 2% MoM churn | 3 | Best-in-class; stable customers |
| 130% NRR | 3 | Expansion revenue strong |
| 10-month CAC payback | 3 | Efficient growth |
| 25% from top 5 customers | 3 | Well-diversified |
| 3-year avg contracts | 3 | Long commitment |
| 72% gross margin | 2 | Good, not exceptional |
| 5.5:1 LTV:CAC | 2 | Strong, but can improve |
| 12% MoM expansion | 2 | Good growth, room for improvement |
| **NPS 58** | 1 | Good but room for improvement |
| **TOTAL** | **25/30** | **Excellent revenue quality** |

**Justifies 9–10x multiple. $[10]M ARR × 9x = $[90]M valuation.**

---

## HOW TO IMPROVE YOUR REVENUE QUALITY SCORE

**Before selling, focus on:**
1. **Reduce churn** (below 2% MoM) → +3 points potential
2. **Increase NRR** (above 120%) → +3 points potential
3. **Improve margins** (above 75%) → +2 points potential
4. **Raise NPS** (above 60) → +2 points potential

**Improving 4 points = +1x multiple = +10% valuation.**

---

## WHAT BUYERS LOOK FOR

**Buyers prioritize (in order):**
1. **NRR >120%** (expansion = sustainable growth)
2. **Churn <3% MoM** (stable base)
3. **CAC payback <12 months** (profitable growth)
4. **No concentration** (top 5 <30% of ARR)
5. **High gross margin** (>70% = profitable at scale)

**Your job:** Score high on these 5 items, and your valuation will be strong.
