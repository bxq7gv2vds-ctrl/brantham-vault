# SELLERS' REPRESENTATIVE AGREEMENT
**Short Form** (Companion to SPA)

---

## KEY CONCEPT

**Sellers' Rep** = one trusted person authorized to sign documents, answer Qs, and handle disputes on behalf of all shareholders **after closing**.

Why? Because you don't want [Buyer] calling 10 different shareholders for approvals. One person = faster, cleaner.

---

## APPOINTMENT

**The undersigned shareholders of [Seller Company]** appoint **[Founder/Representative Name]** as the **exclusive Sellers' Representative** to:

1. **Sign final documents** (SPA amendments, transition docs, escrow releases)
2. **Represent shareholders** in disputes (indemnification claims, earnout disputes)
3. **Manage escrow** (approve releases of held funds)
4. **Make decisions** binding all shareholders

**Scope of authority**: [Rep] may take any action reasonably necessary to close and integrate the transaction.

---

## LIABILITY & INDEMNIFICATION

[Rep] is **personally liable** to [Buyer] for:
- Breaches of representations & warranties (up to escrow amount)
- Indemnification claims (up to cap)

**All shareholders agree to share liability pro-rata** with [Rep]. If [Rep] pays out $1M indemnification, shareholders reimburse [Rep] proportionally.

---

## COMPENSATION & EXPENSE REIMBURSEMENT

[Rep] is **not compensated** for this role (it's part of being a shareholder).

[Buyer] reimburses reasonable legal fees incurred by [Rep] post-close (e.g., dispute resolution, earnout calculation reviews).

---

## EARNOUT DISPUTES

If earnout calculation is disputed:

1. [Rep] and [Buyer CFO] meet to resolve (within 5 days)
2. If unresolved, hire independent accountant (cost split 50/50)
3. Accountant's determination is final & binding

[Rep] has authority to settle for up to $[X] without shareholder vote.

---

## ESCROW RELEASE

[Rep] approves release of escrowed funds when:
- No pending indemnification claims
- Or all claims are resolved

[Buyer] cannot hold escrow hostage; [Rep] enforces release timeline.

---

## RESIGNATION / REPLACEMENT

[Rep] can resign with [30 days] notice, replaced by vote of [majority / 75%] of shareholders.

---

## TERMINATION

This agreement terminates [3 years] post-close (when escrow period ends).

## PRO TIPS

✓ **Choose someone** who [Buyer] trusts + who's not leaving immediately  
✓ **Give broad authority** to avoid 10 shareholders having to vote on every detail  
✓ **Lock in dispute resolution process** (arbitration, independent accountant, etc.)  
✓ **Set reasonable caps** on [Rep] authority for big decisions (e.g., settle for >$[X]M requires shareholder vote)
