## **4. LOI COUNTER-OFFER EMAIL**

```
Subject: [Company] — Counter to LOI (DRAFT)

Hi [CEO],

Thank you for the LOI. We've reviewed with our advisor.

A few points to address:

VALUATION
• You propose: €1.2M (2.4x ARR)
• We counter: €1.8M (3.6x ARR)
• Our rationale: Peer SaaS B2B at 4-6x, our growth +60% justifies premium

EARNOUT STRUCTURE
• You propose: 100% cash at close
• We counter: 50% cash, 50% earnout over 2 years
• Rationale: Aligns our interest in success post-acquisition

NON-COMPETE
• You propose: 5 years, worldwide, all verticals
• We counter: 2 years, France only, competing salary/admin software excluded
• Rationale: We should be free to invest in other industries

FOUNDER RETENTION
• You propose: Acquisition bonus €50k
• We counter: €150k acceleration + 2-year employment at €XXk/year
• Rationale: Want to stay engaged with integration

TIMELINE
• We propose signing LOI by [+10 days], VDD [+40 days], Close [+65 days]
• That's 65 days total. Doable ?

Can we discuss live over Zoom [date/time] ?

Best,
[Your name]
```

## **6. EARNOUT CALCULATION TEMPLATE**

```
Post-Close Earnout Structure

BASE PAYOUT: €XXXk at signing
EARNOUT: €XXXk over 2 years

YEAR 1 TARGET (Jan - Dec Year+1)
• Earnout trigger: If ARR ≥ €650k
• If hit: €XXXk paid Q1 Year+2
• If miss by <5%: €XXXk × 50% paid
• If miss by >5%: €0 paid

YEAR 2 TARGET (Jan - Dec Year+2)
• Earnout trigger: If ARR ≥ €850k
• If hit: €XXXk paid Q1 Year+3
• If miss by <5%: €XXXk × 50% paid
• If miss by >5%: €0 paid

IMPORTANT CAVEATS
• "ARR" measured same way as pre-closing (same accounting)
• Buyer not allowed to artificially reduce ARR (e.g., by deleting customers unfairly)
• If buyer changes pricing model, we audit together
• If buyer is acquired, earnout paid in full at close

FOUNDER OBLIGATIONS (to earn earnout)
• Stay employed (2 years)
• Participate in integration (80% time = okay)
• Don't compete during period
• Provide accurate revenue reporting
```
