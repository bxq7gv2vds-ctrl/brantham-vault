## B. DETAILED SCORING RUBRIC (1-5 scale per criterion)

### **VALUATION**
- **5:** ≥9x ARR, >70% cash, earnout <20% and buyer-favorable milestones
- **4:** 8-9x ARR, 50-70% cash, earnout 20-35%
- **3:** 7-8x ARR, 40-50% cash, earnout 35-50%
- **2:** 6-7x ARR, <40% cash, earnout >50%, or buyer milestones very hard
- **1:** <6x ARR, <30% cash, >50% earnout with bad gates

### **FINANCIAL STRENGTH**
- **5:** Public co, BBB+ or better, 10+ acquisitions, pays within 30 days
- **4:** Mid-market, decent credit, 5-10 acquisitions, 30-60 day payment
- **3:** Smaller buyer, unknown credit, 1-5 acquisitions, 60-90 day payment
- **2:** Startup/PE-backed, risky credit, 0-1 acquisitions, 90-180 day payment
- **1:** Pre-revenue, no track record, late payments in past

### **STRATEGIC FIT**
- **5:** Perfect product synergy (4-5/5), >50% customer overlap, expand to 3+ new geos
- **4:** Good synergy (3-4/5), 25-50% overlap, 1-2 new geos
- **3:** Decent synergy (2-3/5), 10-25% overlap, 1 new geo
- **2:** Weak synergy (1-2/5), <10% overlap, no new geos
- **1:** No synergy, competing product, integration pain

### **EARNOUT CONFIDENCE**
- **5:** <20% earnout, 1-year, you control metrics, buyer paid 100% in past
- **4:** 20-35% earnout, 2-year, you influence metrics, buyer mostly reliable
- **3:** 35-50% earnout, 2-year, shared control, buyer's track record unclear
- **2:** 50-65% earnout, 3-year, buyer controls definition, missed earnouts before
- **1:** >65% earnout, 3-year, buyer controls metrics, history of disputes

### **CULTURAL / TEAM FIT**
- **5:** Founder + key team staying, standalone, aligned on vision, great culture
- **4:** Founder staying, mostly standalone, some integration, good culture
- **3:** Founder role TBD, integrated, team uncertain, neutral culture
- **2:** Founder advisor role, heavy integration, key team at risk, tough culture
- **1:** Founder leaving post-close, major layoffs, toxic environment

## C. RED FLAGS BY BUYER TYPE

| Buyer Type | Red Flag | Weight | Action |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Strategic acquirer** | "We'll totally integrate your team" + vague on earnout | 🔴 High | Demand specifics on post-close roles + earnout math |
| **Financial buyer (PE)** | "We plan to flip this in 3-5 years" | 🔴 High | Verify earnout logic independent of resale timing |
| **Acqui-hire** | "We're really buying your engineers" | 🟡 Medium | Clarify product roadmap + customer retention plan |
| **Competitor** | Suddenly changes integration plans post-LOI | 🔴 High | Verify they're not trying to shut you down |
| **Startup buyer** | "We'll pay earnout from future funding rounds" | 🔴 Critical | Demand escrow or walk |

## F. HOW TO USE THIS

1. **List all potential buyers** (5-10 minimum)
2. **Research each** (LinkedIn, Crunchbase, Pitchbook, your network)
3. **Fill in scores** (take 5 min per buyer)
4. **Calculate weighted total** (automatic in Excel)
5. **Identify red flags** (call your lawyer)
6. **Rank by score** (but don't ignore gut feel)
7. **Share with advisor** (they'll catch blindspots)
