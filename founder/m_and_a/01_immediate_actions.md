# 5 Actions Immédiates M&A — Jour 1-7

## Action 1: Define BATNA (Jour 1 → 30min)
**What:** Calculate best alternative to selling (raise next round, stay independent, IPO, shutdown).
**Output:** One-line BATNA value (e.g., "$100M Series B post-money").
**Doc:** [[quickstart_90_days]]
**Why:** Everything else is negotiated against this anchor.

---

## Action 2: Build Cap Table Clarity (Jour 2-3 → 2h)
**What:** Current ownership %, stock options (vesting %, strike), convertibles (SAFEs, notes), preferred shares (prefs, anti-dilution).
**Output:** One spreadsheet, shares → $ at valuation X.
**Doc:** [[quickstart_90_days]]
**Why:** You must know who gets paid first, how much they dilute your proceeds.

---

## Action 3: Identify 5-10 Buyers (Jour 3-4 → 4h)
**What:** List acquirers using:
- Your customers ("who do you wish we integrated with?")
- Advisors, board members
- Competitor intelligence (who's raising, M&A active?)
- SEC filings (if public targets)

**Criteria:** complementary product, relevant market, capacity (revenue >$50M), strategic fit.
**Output:** Spreadsheet: name, contact person, synergies.
**Doc:** [[buyer_profiles_signals]]
**Why:** You control the supply of buyers → control price.

---

## Action 4: Prepare Teaser Deck (Jour 4-5 → 6h)
**What:** 5–10 slides:
1. Problem
2. Solution
3. Market size
4. Traction (revenue, growth)
5. Unit economics (CAC, LTV, payback)
6. Why now
7. Why acquire us (synergies)
8. Timeline

**Output:** PDF, teaser-quality not data-room quality.
**Doc:** [[quickstart_90_days]]
**Why:** This is your hook. If the teaser doesn't grab them, no deep dive.

---

## Action 5: Engage M&A Advisor (Jour 5-7 → meeting + 1w)
**Roles:**
- Lawyer: legal structure, reps/warranties, financing
- Banker (optional): buyer sourcing, valuation negotiation
- Accountant: working capital, tax optimization

**Cost:** Lawyer $30–100k retainer + success fee; Banker 0.5–1.5% of deal value.
**Output:** Signed engagement letters, kickoff meeting.
**Doc:** [[quickstart_90_days]]
**Why:** You need professional credibility and deal mechanics expertise.

## Quick Decision Tree: When to Start M&A?

```
Is your BATNA ≥ current valuation?
├─ YES  → Explore M&A (you have optionality)
└─ NO   → Build more → revisit in 6m

Do you have 3+ credible buyers interested?
├─ YES  → Open soft shop (parallel conversations)
└─ NO   → Spend weeks sourcing before teaser

Is your cap table clean (vesting cliffs resolved, options <15%)?
├─ YES  → Move fast (buyers love simplicity)
└─ NO   → Spend 2w cleaning before outreach

Do you have 18+ months of predictable revenue?
├─ YES  → You have leverage (acquirer wants stable unit)
└─ NO   → Growth story is your leverage (execution risk discount)
```

Pick one of Actions 1-5 and start today. Don't wait for perfection.
