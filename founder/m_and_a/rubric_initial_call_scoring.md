# Initial Call Scoring Rubric — Buyer Fit Assessment

**After each call, score 0-3 per item. Total 30+ = hot buyer. < 15 = pass.**

| Factor | 0 (Bad) | 1 (Okay) | 2 (Good) | 3 (Excellent) | Your Score |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Decision Speed** | Takes 3+ months to decide | 2-3 months typical | 6-8 weeks normal | 4-6 weeks, moving fast | ___ |
| **Strategic Fit** | Unrelated business | Adjacent market | Perfect product fit | Fills critical gap | ___ |
| **Urgency Signal** | "Interesting, but low priority" | "We should explore this" | "Love this, want to move fast" | "We need this, when can we close?" | ___ |
| **Buyer's Growth Rate** | Declining / flat | Single-digit growth | 10-30% growth | 30%+ growth | ___ |
| **Budget Authority** | Needs board approval each step | Needs approval for deal | Has acquisition budget approved | CEO can green-light | ___ |
| **Cultural Alignment** | Opposite values | Acceptable | Good fit | Founders friends | ___ |
| **Team Enthusiasm** | One person interested | 2 people engaged | 3+ people engaged | Cross-functional excitement | ___ |
| **Balance Sheet** | Debt-heavy, tight | Okay position | Strong cash position | Excess cash, looking to deploy | ___ |
| **Recent Acquisitions** | Never acquired | 1 deal in 5 years | 2-3 deals recently | Active acquirer (1+ per year) | ___ |
| **Timeline Clarity** | Vague ("maybe Q4") | Rough estimate ("2-3 months") | Clear plan ("close by Sept") | Specific milestones mapped | ___ |

**Total Score: ___ / 30**

---

## SCORING INTERPRETATION

| Score | Signal | Action |
|---|---|---|
| **25-30** | 🟢 **HOT** — Move fast, exclusive if requested | Prioritize calls, accelerate LOI |
| **20-24** | 🟢 **WARM** — Serious, competitive process OK | Continue talks, don't be exclusive |
| **15-19** | 🟡 **LUKEWARM** — Interested but slow | Keep in pipeline, don't wait for them |
| **10-14** | 🟠 **COLD** — Low signal, low fit | Move on; marginal use of time |
| **< 10** | 🔴 **DEAD** — Bad fit or no real interest | Disengage, focus on hot buyers |

---

## RED FLAGS (Auto-Disqualify)

- ❌ "We need your comp plan to change post-acquisition" (founder unhappy)
- ❌ "We'll know our budget in 6 months" (no real intent)
- ❌ "We only do tuck-in deals < $5M" (you're bigger; mismatch)
- ❌ "We'd need board approval for this" (slow; risk of rejection)
- ❌ "No founder can stay 24+ months" (dealbreaker for them)
