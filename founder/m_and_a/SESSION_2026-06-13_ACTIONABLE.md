## 💡 Key Insights from Production

### Tax Structure Wins
- Asset deal (France): 23% tax vs 45% (if structured wrong)
- Share deal (US): Section 338(h)(10) = cost basis step-up for buyer
- **Action** : Talk to tax advisor in Week 1 (can save €50K+)

### Earnout Mechanics
- Successful earnouts = weekly tracking, transparent metrics, dispute prevention
- **Action** : Set up dashboard NOW (before LOI), share with buyer

### Customer Risk
- If top 3 customers = >50% ARR, buyer will reduce offer 5-10%
- Proactive mitigation plan = better negotiating position
- **Action** : Risk-score each customer THIS WEEK

### Tech Debt
- Score <60/100 = 10-20% price reduction
- Pre-emptive fixes (4 weeks, €16K investment) = recover €100K+ in price
- **Action** : Audit code + fix highest-impact items before soft shop

### Integration
- First 30 days = critical for retention + earnout
- Customer intros day 5 > day 20 (freshness matters)
- **Action** : Plan integration timeline BEFORE close (don't wait)

### Founder Role
- Don't wing it. Lock in role, compensation, exit timing in SPA.
- Earnout KPIs must align with what founder controls (retention, product, ops)
- **Action** : Draft your ideal post-close role NOW (negotiating power)
