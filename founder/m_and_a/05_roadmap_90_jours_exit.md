# Roadmap 90 Jours — De "Je veux vendre" à Exit

## **MOIS 1 : PRÉPARATION (J0-J30)**

### Semaine 1 : Assessment
- [ ] Audit interne (checklist complète)
- [ ] Draft 3-scenario valuation (bear/base/bull)
- [ ] List top 10 acquéreurs potentiels (stratégiques + financiers)
- [ ] Hire M&A advisor (optional mais recommandé si >€1M deal)

### Semaine 2-3 : Nettoyage
- [ ] Fix all red flags du audit (si critical)
- [ ] Prepare 1-page teaser deck
- [ ] Draft NDA template
- [ ] Finalize cap table & cleanliness
- [ ] Get your financials audited (if not done)

### Semaine 4 : Outreach
- [ ] Personal outreach to 3 strategic buyers (CEO to CEO)
- [ ] Contact 2 PE firms (via warm intro if possible)
- [ ] Send teasers (NDA required before sharing)
- [ ] Schedule 5-6 calls Week 1 of Month 2

---

## **MOIS 2 : ATTRACTION (J31-J60)**

### Semaine 5-6 : Management Presentations
- [ ] 5-6 buyer calls (1h each, 1-2 per week)
- [ ] Take feedback, adjust pitch
- [ ] Identify 2-3 most serious buyers
- [ ] Get first LOI term sheet from #1 candidate

### Semaine 7 : LOI Negotiations
- [ ] Receive LOI draft (price, structure, timeline)
- [ ] Counter LOI (price +10%, earnout terms, non-compete)
- [ ] Negotiate back-and-forth (3-5 rounds typical)
- [ ] Goal : Signed LOI by end Week 8

### Semaine 8 : VDD Prep
- [ ] Hire M&A lawyer (if not done)
- [ ] Create data room (Intralinks, Merlock, Google Drive)
- [ ] Upload all documents (financials, contracts, code, IP)
- [ ] Draft Reps & Warranties schedule
- [ ] Prepare customer reference list (top 10)

---

## **MOIS 3 : CLOSING (J61-J90)**

### Semaine 9-10 : VDD Deep Dive
- [ ] Buyer audits financials (3-5 days intensive)
- [ ] Tech due diligence (code review, architecture)
- [ ] Customer calls (buyer talks to 5-8 customers)
- [ ] Employment / Founder agreement drafts
- [ ] Resolve issues as they arise

### Semaine 11 : Final Negotiate & Closing
- [ ] Sign Definitive Agreement (after VDD complete)
- [ ] Final sign-off on Reps & Warranties
- [ ] Secure W&I insurance (indemnity protection)
- [ ] Clear conditions precedent
- [ ] Wire transfer / equity vesting begins

### Semaine 12 : Transition
- [ ] Announce to team (post-close)
- [ ] Hand-over transition (2-3 weeks support)
- [ ] Distribute founder payoff (if earnout structure)
- [ ] Plan next chapter

---

## **Critical Path (Things That Could Delay)**

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|-----------|
| Buyer takes 45 days to respond | +30 days | Parallel track 2 buyers |
| VDD finds big issue (churn 50%) | Price negotiation | Fix metric before exit |
| Founder not available (surgery) | Paused deal | Succession plan for founder role |
| Key customer leaves mid-process | Price hits -20% | Retain top clients before start |
| Lawyer/advisor bottleneck | +20 days | Hire by Week 1 Month 2 |

---

## **Parallel Track (If Buyer Slow)**

- Month 1 : Start talking to buyer #1 + buyer #2 simultaneously
- Month 2 : If buyer #1 is slow (>6 weeks), push buyer #2 forward
- Result : Urgency without desperation

---

## **Sanity Checks at Each Stage**

### End of Month 1 ✓
- ✅ At least 3 buyers talking seriously
- ✅ First management presentations done
- ✅ Valuation deck polished
- ⚠️ If: Only 1 interested → Expand target list

### End of Month 2 ✓
- ✅ LOI signed or very close
- ✅ Data room loaded
- ✅ Buyer VDD about to start
- ⚠️ If: LOI still not signed → Maybe not a good fit, move to buyer #2

### End of Month 3 ✓
- ✅ Deal closed (or 1 week away)
- ✅ Wires transferred
- ✅ Transition plan clear
- ⚠️ If: "Just a few more weeks..." → Probably walking

---

## **Decision Points**

### Should I Walk ?
- [ ] Buyer not respecting LOI timeline (>14 days extra)
- [ ] Price moved down >20% mid-VDD without cause
- [ ] Buyer asking illegal things (tax fraud, customer data breach)
- [ ] You don't trust the buyer (gut feeling, check references)

### When to Push Harder ?
- [ ] 2+ buyers actively bidding (leverage urgency)
- [ ] Buyer saying "very interested" but dragging (set deadline)
- [ ] VDD is stalling (weekly check-in)

### When to Accept ?
- [ ] 80%+ of your original ask
- [ ] Earnout is reasonable (achievable)
- [ ] Buyer is legitimate & stable
- [ ] You're tired & this is good exit
