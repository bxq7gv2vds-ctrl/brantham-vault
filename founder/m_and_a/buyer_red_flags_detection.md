# BUYER RED FLAGS — QUICK DETECTION CHECKLIST

**Purpose:** Identify toxic buyers BEFORE you sign, not after  
**How to use:** Read the list. Check flags as you learn about them. 3+ flags = escalate to advisor.  
**Time:** 5 minutes to diagnose  

---

## QUICK FILTER (Any 1 = serious concern)

| 🔴 KILL-LEVEL RED FLAGS | Evidence | Your action |
|---|---|---|
| **They're a direct competitor** | You sell in same market, same customer type | Demand absolute clarity on product roadmap. If vague = ❌ WALK |
| **Earnout is >65% of deal** | Math says you're financing them | ❌ WALK. You'll never see that money. |
| **They've missed earnouts before** | You can find this via founders they acquired | ❌ WALK. History repeats. |
| **They won't commit to 3rd-party accounting** | They control earnout calculation entirely | ❌ WALK. They'll game the metrics. |
| **They've changed integration plan 2+ times** | You have emails from different phases | They don't know what they're doing. ❌ WALK. |

## RED FLAGS BY CATEGORY

### 💰 FINANCIAL RED FLAGS (Ability to pay)

| Flag | How to check | Severity |
|---|---|---|
| **No public financials** (if public co) | Search SEC Edgar | 🔴 High |
| **Recent credit downgrade** | Ask their CFO directly. Check Moodys/S&P | 🔴 High |
| **Layoffs in last 12 months** | LinkedIn, TechCrunch, their website | 🟡 Medium |
| **Customer concentration >70%** (on THEIR side) | Ask about their top 5 customers | 🟡 Medium |
| **Negative cash flow** (for private buyer) | Ask their accountant | 🔴 High |
| **PE fund with bad track record** | Crunchbase, LinkedIn (founder sentiment) | 🔴 High |
| **They're asking for seller financing** | "Can we pay you over time?" | ❌ KILL-LEVEL |
| **Payment timeline >90 days** | If not escrow, you're bridging them | 🟡 Medium |

**What to do:** Request last 2 years of financials (audited). If they refuse = walk.

---

### 📋 DEAL STRUCTURE RED FLAGS (Terms & conditions)

| Flag | How to check | Severity |
|---|---|---|
| **Earnout metrics are vague** | SPA Exhibit A is fuzzy (e.g., "success-based") | 🔴 High |
| **Earnout is combined company metric** | "Your ARR contribution to our total" | ❌ KILL-LEVEL |
| **Reps & warranties >24 months** | SPA says 24+ months | 🟡 Medium |
| **No reps & warranties insurance** | They won't buy it; self-insure | 🟡 Medium |
| **Indemnification cap is unlimited** | SPA has no dollar cap on your indemnity | 🔴 High |
| **Holdback >20% of deal value** | Money locked up too long | 🟡 Medium |
| **Escrow release is conditional** | "Released only if no claims after year 1" = they can keep it | 🔴 High |
| **No clear integration timeline** | "TBD" or "We'll figure it out" | 🟡 Medium |
| **Material adverse change (MAC) clause is broad** | Any market shift lets them walk | 🔴 High |

**What to do:** Lawyer review. Non-negotiable items = walk.

---

### 👥 TEAM & CULTURE RED FLAGS

| Flag | How to check | Severity |
|---|---|---|
| **High turnover** (their leadership) | LinkedIn: Who left in last 12 months? | 🟡 Medium |
| **Founder-hostile culture** (Glassdoor) | "Micromanagement," "No autonomy" | 🟡 Medium |
| **CTO/CFO left recently** | They lost key technical/financial person | 🔴 High |
| **You don't trust their CFO/COO** | Gut check. They're lying/evasive | 🔴 High |
| **They can't articulate post-close vision** | Vague on product roadmap | 🟡 Medium |
| **They want all your team to stay** | But won't detail their roles | 🔴 High |
| **They want you to leave immediately** | Red flag: Why'd they acquire you then? | 🟡 Medium |

**What to do:** Call 2-3 founders they acquired before. Ask: "How did they treat you post-close?"

---

### 📊 OPERATIONAL RED FLAGS (Business quality)

| Flag | How to check | Severity |
|---|---|---|
| **Customer concentration >60%** (yours) | Your top 3 = X% of ARR | 🟡 Medium |
| **Churn trending up** | Last 3 months > prior 3 months | 🟡 Medium |
| **NPS declining** (if you have it) | Recent survey shows drop | 🟡 Medium |
| **Key customer at risk** | They signaled they might leave | 🔴 High |
| **Tech debt is severe** | Your team can't ship features | 🟡 Medium |
| **Security audit found critical issues** | Unpatched vulnerabilities, no GDPR compliance | 🔴 High |
| **IP cloudy** (open-source, founder assignments) | You don't own all your code cleanly | 🔴 High |
| **Material contract you didn't disclose** | Buyer discovers hidden obligation | ❌ KILL-LEVEL (breach of reps) |

**What to do:** Do self-audit NOW (before DD). Tell buyer what you found + your plan to fix.

---

### 🔍 BUYER BEHAVIOR RED FLAGS (How they act)

| Flag | How to check | Severity |
|---|---|---|
| **Avoids your questions** | You ask about integration, they deflect | 🟡 Medium |
| **Keeps changing terms** | LOI said X, email said Y, call said Z | 🔴 High |
| **Pushes for speed** | "Sign by Friday or deal dies" | 🟡 Medium |
| **Won't do NDA properly** | Wants your data before signing NDA | ❌ KILL-LEVEL |
| **Lowballs late** | Sends LOI 30% below ask after 60 days of discussion | 🔴 High |
| **References are negative** | Founders they acquired speak poorly | 🔴 High |
| **Due diligence is theater** | They don't ask real questions | 🟡 Medium |
| **They're in a rush, you're not** | You have BATNA; they're desperate | 🟢 Good sign |
| **Buyer's counsel is hostile** | Lawyers are adversarial, not collaborative | 🟡 Medium |
| **They won't commit to integration plan** | "We'll decide post-close" | 🟡 Medium |

**What to do:** If 3+ flags = call your advisor. Consider walking.

### ⚖️ LEGAL & COMPLIANCE RED FLAGS

| Flag | How to check | Severity |
|---|---|---|
| **Material litigation pending** (on their side) | Search SEC filings, Bloomberg | 🔴 High |
| **Regulatory issues** (antitrust, securities) | Check press, legal databases | 🔴 High |
| **Prior failed acquisitions** | Crunchbase shows 3+ failed deals | 🟡 Medium |
| **They want unusual indemnity carve-outs** | E.g., "You're liable for future IP claims" | 🔴 High |
| **They won't provide audited financials** | Red flag for solvency | 🔴 High |
| **Insurance is insufficient** | Their reps & warranties insurance has low caps | 🟡 Medium |

**What to do:** Lawyer deep-dive.

## SCORING: HOW MANY RED FLAGS = WALK?

```
0 flags       → ✅ Green light (proceed)
1 flag        → 🟢 OK if minor, escalate if major
2 flags       → 🟡 Yellow flag (get advisor input)
3 flags       → 🟡 Serious concerns (renegotiate or walk)
4+ flags      → ❌ Walk (better deals exist)

+ Any 1 KILL-LEVEL flag → ❌ WALK IMMEDIATELY
```

## FLAG-BY-FLAG REMEDIATION

| Flag | How to fix it | Realistic? |
|---|---|---|
| **Vague earnout metrics** | Demand written definition in SPA Exhibit A | ✅ Yes |
| **Long reps warranty** | Negotiate to 12-18mo (not 24+) | ✅ Yes |
| **High earnout %** | Demand standalone metrics + shorter term | ✅ Maybe |
| **Buyer's integration unclear** | Force 100-day plan commitment (before LOI) | ✅ Yes |
| **Buyer's culture toxic** | You can't fix this. Walk. | ❌ No |
| **Customer concentration** | Get retention letters; adjust earnout for churn | ✅ Maybe |
| **Tech debt** | Disclose, get buyer acknowledgment | ✅ Yes |
| **Buyer changing terms constantly** | Document all versions. Call lawyer. Walk if pattern continues. | ✅ Maybe |

## THE RED FLAG CONVERSATION

If you spot 2+ flags, here's what to say:

```
"I want to make sure we're aligned before LOI. I've noticed a few things 
that would benefit from clarity:

1. [Flag 1] — Can you walk me through how you plan to address this?
2. [Flag 2] — What does this mean for [earnout / integration / etc]?

I'm not worried about any single thing, but together they shift how I 
think about the deal. Want to align before we formalize?"
```

**Why this works:**
- You're not accusatory
- You're giving them a chance to explain
- You're clear it affects your thinking

**If they respond badly = walk**

```
Financial strength:    [Good / OK / Weak]

```
