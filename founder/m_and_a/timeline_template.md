## REALISTIC TIMELINE

**Total Duration**: 14-20 weeks (typically 18 weeks)

| Phase | Duration | Critical Path |
|-------|----------|---|
| Preparation | 2-4 weeks | Don't rush; buyer list should be solid |
| LOI | 2-4 weeks | Usually 3 weeks to sign |
| DD | 6-10 weeks | The longest phase; 70% of deal time |
| PSA + Closing | 3-6 weeks | Relatively fast once DD clear |
| **TOTAL** | **14-20 weeks** | Plan for 18-20 weeks |

---

## KEY DATES TO LOCK IN

When you receive LOI from buyer, lock in these:

1. **DD Phase Start Date** — Data room goes live
2. **DD Phase End Date** — When Qs should stop
3. **PSA Signature Target** — Aim for 2 weeks after DD ends
4. **Closing Date** — Typical: 4-6 weeks after PSA signed
5. **Exclusivity Expiration** — If no closing by this date, you can shop again

---

## RED FLAGS IN TIMELINE

If any of these happen, escalate:

- ❌ DD takes >12 weeks (buyer is not serious or too slow)
- ❌ First buyer Q takes >5 business days after DD starts (buyer delayed)
- ❌ PSA signature >3 weeks after DD ends (legal negotiating too hard)
- ❌ Closing >6 weeks after PSA (buyer financing issue)
- ❌ Buyer keeps changing DD scope (adding new topics) → slows closes

## STAKEHOLDER CHECKLIST (WHO ATTENDS WHAT)

### LOI Negotiation
- You, Your Counsel, Buyer's Counsel

### Data Room Access
- Buyer's: Finance team, Legal team, Tech team, sometimes PE/VC ops

### DD Closing Call (end of DD phase)
- You, Your Counsel, Buyer's Team Lead, Buyer's Counsel, Sometimes buyer's CEO

### PSA Signing Call
- You, Board rep, Your Counsel, Buyer, Buyer's Counsel

### Closing Day Call
- You, Your Counsel, Buyer's Counsel, Title company (if asset sale)
- Buyer NOT always on call (their counsel coordinates)
