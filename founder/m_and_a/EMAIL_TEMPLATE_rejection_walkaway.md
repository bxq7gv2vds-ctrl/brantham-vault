# Email Template: Graceful Rejection / Walk Away

**Subject:** Re: Acquisition proposal

---

Hi [Name],

I appreciate your interest and the time you've invested. After careful review with my team and advisors, we've decided not to move forward.

Here's why we're not the right fit right now:

1. **Valuation gap** — Your proposal is [X]% below our walk-away price based on comparable SaaS multiples. We'd need to see [specific number] to justify the transition risk.

2. **Strategic misalignment** — You're focused on [buyer's priority], but our core expansion opportunity is [different direction]. We'd lose leverage.

3. **Timeline pressure** — Your close timeline is [X weeks], but it doesn't allow for proper due diligence handoff to our team. We'd be at risk of earnout clawback.

That said, if things change on your end (budget update, revised timeline, strategic shift), we're open to revisiting in [X months/year].

Best regards,
[Name]

---

**Notes:**
- Be specific about why (not vague)
- Leave door open (reputational capital)
- Never burn bridges (buyer might return with better offer)
- CC your advisor/lawyer for witness
