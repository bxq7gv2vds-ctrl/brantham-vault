# PRE-CLOSING CHECKLIST — 30 DAYS BEFORE CLOSE

**Final 30 days before closing. Use this checklist to ensure nothing is missed.**

---

## WEEK 1 (30 Days Before Close)

**Legal & Documents:**
- [ ] Final SPA signed by both parties (no more red-lines)
- [ ] Employment agreements finalized (your role, comp, equity)
- [ ] Escrow agreement signed (amounts, timeline, agent confirmed)
- [ ] Indemnification insurance quotes received & policy bound

**Financials:**
- [ ] Final closing statement prepared (working capital adjustment calculated)
- [ ] Purchase price agreed (including any WC adjustment)
- [ ] Tax structuring finalized (C-corp, qualified small biz, etc.)

**Operations:**
- [ ] Key customer calls completed (assure continuity post-close)
- [ ] Key employee retention agreements signed (if applicable)
- [ ] Customer communication plan drafted (Day 1 email)

---

## WEEK 2 (20 Days Before Close)

**Diligence Finalization:**
- [ ] All buyer diligence questions answered
- [ ] No open diligence issues (all items cleared)
- [ ] Reference customers confirmed happy (no surprise churn)

**Cap Table & Equity:**
- [ ] Cap table fully diluted and final
- [ ] All option grants vested or accounted for
- [ ] Any option acceleration documented (if applicable)
- [ ] Shareholder consent obtained (if required by your docs)

**Integration Planning:**
- [ ] 100-day integration plan reviewed with buyer
- [ ] Transition service agreements finalized (if any)
- [ ] Earnout metrics and tracking mechanism confirmed

---

## WEEK 3 (10 Days Before Close)

**Financial Close:**
- [ ] Wire instructions to you confirmed (correct bank account verified)
- [ ] Escrow agent verified and escrow terms final
- [ ] Earnout payment schedule in writing
- [ ] Tax withholding/1099 treatment confirmed

**Board & Approvals:**
- [ ] Board resolution approving sale (signed & sealed)
- [ ] Shareholder approval obtained (if required)
- [ ] Any required third-party consents obtained (if material contracts have change-of-control clauses)

**Closing Preparation:**
- [ ] All closing documents printed & ready for signing
- [ ] Signatory packages assembled (know who signs what)
- [ ] Closing location/logistics confirmed (virtual vs. in-person)

---

## DAY OF CLOSE (Day 0)

**Final Verification:**
- [ ] All documents signed by both parties
- [ ] Purchase price wired to your account (confirm receipt)
- [ ] Company ownership officially transferred
- [ ] Earnout terms confirmed in writing

**Post-Close:**
- [ ] Day 1 all-hands email sent to team
- [ ] Customer announcement prepared (if appropriate)
- [ ] Transition plan communicated to key stakeholders
- [ ] First earnout metrics discussion scheduled with buyer

---

## DEAL CLOSEOUT TASKS (Week 1 Post-Close)

- [ ] All documents archived (secure storage)
- [ ] Earnout tracking mechanism set up (spreadsheets, reporting)
- [ ] Integration kickoff with buyer (100-day plan starts)
- [ ] Tax filings updated (entity changes, ownership)

---

## CRITICAL SUCCESS FACTORS (Don't Skip)

🔴 **Must be done before close:**
1. Wire instructions 100% verified (typos = money sent to wrong account!)
2. Tax structuring finalized (don't discover tax surprise post-close)
3. All key team retention locked in (people leave post-close if not incented)
4. Earnout metrics crystal clear (vague = disputes later)

---

## NEXT STEPS

1. **Print this checklist**
2. **Assign owners** for each item (legal, finance, ops)
3. **Weekly check-in** with buyer & your team
4. **Monitor progress** toward close date
5. **Flag any blockers immediately** (don't wait until last minute)
