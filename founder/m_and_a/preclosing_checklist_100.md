# Pre-Closing Checklist — 100 Items (3 Semaines Avant Close)

**Objectif :** Liste exhaustive des 100 tâches critiques à cocher 3 semaines avant signature de purchase agreement.

**Timeline :** LOI signé → DD finie → Définitif Agreement négocié → **Week -3 starts** → Checklist complete 2 weeks avant close → Week 0 = signature & close.

---

## LEGAL & CORPORATE PREP (20 items)

**Purchase Agreement & Schedule**
- [ ] Final purchase agreement drafted + reviewed by seller counsel
- [ ] All schedules (A: cap table, B: contracts, C: IP, D: employees, E: litigation) complete
- [ ] Definitions section clear (materiality thresholds, MAE definition, etc.)
- [ ] Reps & warranties accurate + survival periods locked
- [ ] Earnout terms finalized (KPI, measurement, dispute resolution)
- [ ] Closing conditions (funding commit letter from buyer, regulatory approvals, etc.)

**Shareholder Approval**
- [ ] Board resolutions authorizing transaction
- [ ] Shareholder meeting/written consent (if needed)
- [ ] Shareholder approvals documented + filed
- [ ] Minority shareholders notified (if any)

**Corporate Records**
- [ ] Stock ledger updated (current as of close date)
- [ ] Certificate of good standing ordered (Delaware or home state)
- [ ] Corporate bylaws/charter filed with state (current)
- [ ] No pending shareholder disputes or derivative suits

---

## FINANCIAL & TAX (15 items)

**Financial Statements & Audit**
- [ ] Latest 3-month financials prepared (as of [DATE])
- [ ] YTD P&L + balance sheet + cash flow statement
- [ ] Tax returns (2024, 2023) filed + copies to buyer's accountant
- [ ] EBITDA reconciliation (add-backs validated independently)
- [ ] Working capital schedule (AR, AP, inventory)
- [ ] Deferred revenue schedule (by customer, maturity date)
- [ ] Accounts payable schedule (vendors, amounts, due dates)

**Tax Clearances & Compliance**
- [ ] Federal + state income tax returns timely filed (no pending audits)
- [ ] Payroll taxes current (no back wages owed, no IRS liens)
- [ ] Sales tax compliance (no back assessments)
- [ ] Estimated tax payments made (if S-Corp or LLC)
- [ ] Tax indemnification insurance obtained (quote + premium)

**Accounting Close & Reconciliation**
- [ ] Trial balance reconciled (GL to subledgers)
- [ ] All journal entries from acquisition prep reversed (if any one-time items)
- [ ] Bank reconciliations complete for all accounts

---

## CONTRACTS & COMMERCIAL (15 items)

**Customer Contracts Audit**
- [ ] All customer contracts (>$10k ACV) reviewed by buyer counsel
- [ ] Change-of-control clauses identified + consent letters obtained (or waived)
- [ ] Multi-year contracts confirmed (renewal dates listed)
- [ ] Escrow hold-backs assigned if top customer terminate post-close
- [ ] Top 10 customers personally visited/confirmed (no hidden churn risk)

**Vendor & Supplier Contracts**
- [ ] All material vendor agreements reviewed (SaaS, hosting, etc.)
- [ ] Pricing escalation clauses identified (any post-close increases?)
- [ ] Cloud infrastructure contracts (AWS, GCP, Stripe, etc.) confirmed current
- [ ] Licensing agreements verified (software, data, IP)

**Employment & Benefits**
- [ ] All employment agreements reviewed + on file
- [ ] Offer letters issued for any new hires post-LOI (buyer approval)
- [ ] Severance obligations documented (if any)

---

## INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY (10 items)

**Patents & Trademarks**
- [ ] Patent portfolio reviewed (granted + pending applications)
- [ ] USPTO status checked (fees paid, office actions handled)
- [ ] Trademark registrations confirmed (US + international)
- [ ] Pending trademark applications tracked

**Source Code & Software**
- [ ] Source code escrow agreement in place (if applicable)
- [ ] Open-source license audit completed (no GPL/AGPL conflicts)
- [ ] Third-party library inventory (dependencies, versions)
- [ ] Code ownership confirmed (no founder personal repos)

**IP Ownership & Assignments**
- [ ] IP assignment agreements from founders to company (on file)
- [ ] IP assignment from contractors/consultants (on file)
- [ ] Assignment from ex-employers (if any) filed
- [ ] University IP licenses (if applicable) current

---

## EMPLOYEES & ORGANIZATION (10 items)

**Employee Records & Compliance**
- [ ] Employee census data (names, titles, salaries, hire dates, vesting)
- [ ] W-4s and I-9 forms on file for all employees
- [ ] Background checks completed (if any required)
- [ ] Non-compete/NDA agreements signed by all
- [ ] Offer letters + acceptance letters on file

**Benefits & Payroll**
- [ ] Employee handbook reviewed + compliant
- [ ] Group health insurance current (no lapses)
- [ ] Retirement plan (401k, SIMPLE IRA) current + compliant
- [ ] Payroll tax withholding correct (no errors flagged)
- [ ] Unemployment insurance account in good standing

**Option Pool & Equity**
- [ ] Option/RSU grant agreements on file
- [ ] Vesting schedule for all grants documented
- [ ] Equity plan amendment (if any option pool increase) approved
- [ ] 409A valuation obtained (if not already recent)

---

## DATA SECURITY & COMPLIANCE (12 items)

**Information Security**
- [ ] Pentest results (last 6 months) current + no critical issues
- [ ] SOC2 Type II report obtained (if applicable)
- [ ] Data breach history disclosed (if any)
- [ ] Cybersecurity insurance policy current
- [ ] Incident response plan documented

**Privacy & Regulatory**
- [ ] GDPR compliance audit completed (if EU customers)
- [ ] CCPA compliance validated (if CA customers)
- [ ] Data retention policy documented + enforced
- [ ] Data processing agreements (DPA) with customers current
- [ ] Privacy policy reviewed + accurate

**Infrastructure & Disaster Recovery**
- [ ] Cloud infrastructure documented (AWS/GCP/Azure account list)
- [ ] Disaster recovery plan tested (RTO <4 hours, RPO <1 hour)
- [ ] Backup strategy verified (daily, 30-day retention minimum)
- [ ] Access controls documented (who has admin, 2FA enforced)

---

## CUSTOMER & REVENUE (10 items)

**Customer Data & Contracts**
- [ ] Customer list with ARR, MRR, start date, renewal date
- [ ] Top 50 customers identified + no churn risk flags
- [ ] Customer concentrations reviewed (top 5 = X% revenue)
- [ ] SLA/support agreements documented (if any)
- [ ] Customer reference calls approved (buyer may call some)

**Recurring Revenue & Churn**
- [ ] Monthly recurring revenue (MRR) calculation verified
- [ ] Churn analysis (monthly/annual) reconciled
- [ ] Net revenue retention (NRR) calculation audited independently
- [ ] Revenue recognition policy reviewed (GAAP compliance)
- [ ] Pipeline/forecast credibility confirmed (not inflated)

---

## OPERATIONS & FACILITIES (5 items)

**Leases & Real Estate**
- [ ] Office lease(s) reviewed (termination clauses, co-tenants)
- [ ] Landlord consent/waiver obtained (if change-of-control required)
- [ ] Other leases (equipment, vehicles) documented

**Insurance**
- [ ] D&O insurance current (coverage limits verified)
- [ ] General liability coverage current
- [ ] Cyber liability insurance current

---

## LITIGATION & REGULATORY (8 items)

**Disputes & Legal**
- [ ] All litigation disclosed (current + resolved past 5 years)
- [ ] Settlement agreements on file (any ongoing obligations?)
- [ ] Regulatory investigations (if any) status communicated
- [ ] Legal holds documented (if discovery obligations pending)

**Regulatory & Compliance**
- [ ] Business licenses current (state, local, federal)
- [ ] Certifications (if applicable) current + verified
- [ ] Export compliance (if applicable) verified
- [ ] Anti-corruption/FCPA compliance certification signed

---

## TRANSACTION LOGISTICS (15 items)

**Funding & Bank Accounts**
- [ ] Buyer's funding commitment letter provided + valid
- [ ] Wire instructions from buyer confirmed (authorized signatory list)
- [ ] Seller's receiving bank account confirmed (20+ characters = correct)
- [ ] Buyer's closing attorney & contact confirmed
- [ ] Seller's closing attorney & contact confirmed

**Documents for Signature**
- [ ] Final purchase agreement + exhibits printed (2 originals + PDFs)
- [ ] Closing statement (proration, adj ustments) finalized
- [ ] Closing checklist (100 items) assigned to responsible party
- [ ] Secretary's certificate (corp authority, resolutions) prepared
- [ ] Officers' certificate (reps true as of close) prepared
- [ ] Earnout SLA signed (if applicable)
- [ ] Employment agreements signed (founder/key team)
- [ ] Non-compete/Non-solicit agreements signed (if required)

**Regulatory Filings**
- [ ] Hart-Scott-Rodino filing (if applicable, threshold $111M)
- [ ] State antitrust clearances obtained (if multi-state)
- [ ] Foreign investment review (CFIUS) if applicable
- [ ] Regulatory approval letters filed

## MANAGEMENT & COMMUNICATION (5 items)

**Leadership Prep**
- [ ] All-hands meeting prepared (talking points, FAQ)
- [ ] Customer communication plan (announcement email ready)
- [ ] Investor thank-you letter drafted
- [ ] Employee Q&A prepared (role changes, benefits, stay/go)

**Post-Close Planning**
- [ ] Integration roadmap (first 100 days) shared with buyer
- [ ] Transition services agreement (60 days) signed
- [ ] Founder employment agreement (retention, earnout) signed

---

## DAY-BEFORE CLOSE CHECKLIST (10 items)

**Final Verification**
- [ ] All documents signed by authorized parties (notarized if needed)
- [ ] Closing attorney has all exhibits + exhibits correct versions
- [ ] Wire amount confirmed ($[X].XX)
- [ ] Wire recipient confirmed (not scam account)
- [ ] Buying attorney confirms no last-minute issues
- [ ] Seller's team available for questions (close day)
- [ ] Earnout tracking mechanism set up (Buyer accountant identified)
- [ ] Post-close communication plan ready (emails to customers/investors)
- [ ] Founder knows: arrive 1 hour early, bring ID, bring phone for wire confirm
- [ ] Finance person ready to confirm wire arrival (notify founder within 5 min)

---

## CLOSE DAY (Day 0) — FINAL CHECKS

**Morning of Close**
- [ ] All parties in signature meeting
- [ ] Purchase agreement signed by all (Buyer, Sellers, Buyer's parent if applicable)
- [ ] Secretary's certificate signed + notarized
- [ ] Officers' certificate signed (reps true as of this date)
- [ ] Closing statement signed (final adjustment reconciliation)
- [ ] Wire authorization signed (with proper limits)

**Funding Transfer**
- [ ] Buyer's legal instructs bank to wire
- [ ] Seller's bank confirms wire receipt (within 2–4 hours)
- [ ] Seller's finance confirms funds visible in account
- [ ] Seller's legal confirms no pending issues from Buyer
- [ ] Seller notifies board/shareholders (transaction closed!)

**Post-Close Day 1**
- [ ] Company name change (if applicable) filed with state
- [ ] Buyer's legal updates cap table with Buyer equity stake (if stock deal)
- [ ] Earnout escrow agent funded (if applicable)
- [ ] All-hands meeting with buyer (founder + new leadership introduced)
- [ ] Customer announcement sent
- [ ] First post-close integration meeting scheduled

---

## Contingency Plans

**If Issue Found Week -2:**
- [ ] Issue severity assessed (<$250k? Walk or renegotiate)
- [ ] Seller's legal notifies Buyer within 24 hours
- [ ] Closing delayed 1 week to resolve (standard)
- [ ] If unresolvable, buyer can terminate (no recourse to seller for typical issues)

**If Issue Found on Close Day:**
- [ ] Buyer can refuse to fund (if material reps violated)
- [ ] Seller has right to sue for specific performance (rare, expensive)
- [ ] More often: both parties agree to delayed close (1–2 weeks) to resolve
- [ ] Deal does NOT close; back to negotiation or walk
