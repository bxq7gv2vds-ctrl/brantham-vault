# Customer Win-Back Kit — Post-Acquisition Messaging

**Objectif :** 3 emails + FAQ pour rassurer customers après acquisition announcement.\n\n**Timeline :** Day 0 = press release; Day 1 = email 1 (reassurance); Days 2–7 = direct outreach (top 20 customers).\n\n---\n\n## EMAIL #1 — ANNOUNCEMENT (Send Day 1)\n\n**Subject:** Exciting News: [COMPANY] Acquired by [BUYER] — Your Product Gets Even Better\n\n```\nDear [CUSTOMER_NAME],\n\nWe're thrilled to announce that [COMPANY] has been acquired by [BUYER].\n\nWhy this matters to you:\n✅ Continued product development (no disruption)\n✅ Better integrations (access to Buyer's ecosystem)\n✅ Enhanced support (Buyer's infrastructure)\n✅ Same product quality (our team stays)\n✅ Same pricing (locked in for [12 months / your contract term])\n\nWhat changes:\n- Your primary contact is still [YOUR_NAME] ([EMAIL/PHONE])\n- Invoicing may be from [BUYER_LEGAL_NAME] (same terms)\n- Data security posture strengthens (Buyer has SOC2, FedRAMP, etc.)\n- Integration roadmap accelerates (new features coming Q[X])\n\nThis is a milestone, not a disruption. You're in great hands.\n\nQuestions? Reply to this email or schedule a call: [CALENDAR_LINK]\n\nBest,\n[FOUNDER_NAME]\nCEO, [COMPANY]\n```\n\n**Strategy:** Lead with benefits (not fear). Same team, same product, better resources.\n\n---\n\n## EMAIL #2 — 48–72 HOURS FOLLOW-UP (Send Day 3)\n\n**Subject Line:** [CUSTOMER_NAME], Let's Talk About Your New Capabilities\n\n```\nHi [CUSTOMER_NAME],\n\nQuick follow-up on our acquisition announcement.\n\nI wanted to share some exciting capabilities now available to you:\n\n1. **[INTEGRATION_A]** — You can now connect [COMPANY] to [BUYER_TOOL] \n   (Previously impossible; now native)\n   → Expected lift: [X]% efficiency gain\n\n2. **[INTEGRATION_B]** — Access to [BUYER_DATA] \n   (Complementary data stream you've been asking for)\n   → Expected impact: [business outcome]\n\n3. **[INTEGRATION_C]** — Dedicated Buyer support team \n   (Faster resolution, 24/7 coverage)\n   → New SLA: [X]-hour response\n\nNo action required from you—these roll out over [Q1 2026].\n\nWe're committed to making [COMPANY] even more valuable to your business.\n\nWant a personalized walkthrough? [CALENDAR_LINK]\n\nCheers,\n[FOUNDER_NAME]\n```\n\n**Strategy:** Concrete new capabilities (not vague promises). Make customer see \"why this is good for me.\"\n\n---\n\n## EMAIL #3 — 1-WEEK CHECK-IN (Send Day 8)\n\n**Subject Line:** One Week In — Answers to Your Questions\n\n```\nHi [CUSTOMER_NAME],\n\nIt's been a week since our announcement, and we've fielded great questions.\n\nHere's what we're hearing + our answers:\n\n**Q: Will my contract terms change?**\nA: No. Your existing contract terms lock in through [RENEWAL_DATE]. \nAfter renewal, we'll discuss pricing—but current customers get [3-month grace] to renegotiate.\n\n**Q: Will I lose my dedicated support contact?**\nA: No. I'm still here, and we've added [BUYER_SUPPORT_TEAM]. \nYou now have backup support 24/7 (vs. business hours before).\n\n**Q: What if I have concerns about the acquisition?**\nA: Schedule a call with me directly. [CALENDAR_LINK]\nI want to hear your concerns and address them personally.\n\n**Q: When do the new integrations ship?**\nA: [INTEGRATION_A] launches Q1 2026 (beta access available now).\n[INTEGRATION_B] targets Q2 2026.\nWe're accelerating because we now have Buyer's engineering team.\n\nThanks for your trust and partnership. We're just getting started.\n\nBest,\n[FOUNDER_NAME]\n```\n\n**Strategy:** Answer real objections + show you're listening. Proactive FAQ beats reactive firefighting.\n\n---\n\n## FAQ — Internal Talking Points (For Your Sales Team)\n\n**Q: Are you shutting down [COMPANY]?**\nA: Absolutely not. We're a strategic acquisition, not an acqui-hire. Full product roadmap continues. Buyer sees us as a long-term strategic asset.\n\n**Q: Will my data be shared with [BUYER]?**\nA: Only for integrations you opt into. Your data is yours. No sharing with Buyer's other business units without your consent.\nOur security & privacy policies remain unchanged.\n\n**Q: What if I want to leave?**\nA: Your contract is unchanged. You can leave at [RENEWAL_DATE] with standard notice.\nWe hope the new capabilities convince you to stay, but the choice is yours.\n\n**Q: Will pricing increase?**\nA: Not during your current contract term. Post-renewal, pricing may adjust for inflation,\nbut we'll offer multi-year discounts for early renewals (better value than spot pricing).\n\n**Q: Is this a fire sale (Buyer got us cheap)?**\nA: Buyer paid fairly. We negotiated hard. This is a strategic fit—not a rescue.\nWe're profitable, growing 40%+ YoY, and had multiple interested acquirers.\n\n**Q: Will the team stay?**\nA: Yes. [CEO], [CTO], [VP Sales] all have 2-year employment commitments.\nYour primary contacts are staying.\n\n**Q: What if Buyer removes features I rely on?**\nA: Buyer understands that customer success = business continuity.\nAny major changes would be communicated 90 days in advance.\nWe have contractual commitments to maintain SLAs.\n\n**Q: Is this good for me?**\nA: Yes. You now have:\n  • Better product (faster feature velocity)\n  • Better support (24/7 coverage + Buyer's resources)\n  • Better integrations (access to Buyer's ecosystem)\n  • Same pricing (locked in through [RENEWAL_DATE])\n  • Same team (your primary contacts stay)\n\n---\n\n## TOP 20 CUSTOMERS — OUTREACH PLAN\n\n| Customer | ARR | Risk Level | Contact | Call Date | Status |\n|----------|-----|-----------|---------|-----------|--------|\n| [ACME] | $[500]k | 🔴 High (competitor acquirer) | CEO | [DATE] | ☑ Scheduled |\n| [BigCorp] | $[250]k | 🟡 Medium | VP Ops | [DATE] | ☑ Scheduled |\n| [Growth Inc] | $[150]k | 🟢 Low (loyal) | CFO | [DATE] | ☑ Scheduled |\n\n**Rule:** Touch top 20 customers (80% of revenue) within 1 week of announcement.\n\n---\n\n## SUCCESS METRICS (Post-Acquisition)\n\n**Week 1:**\n- Email open rate: >60% (baseline: 30%)\n- Customers requesting calls: >20% of top 20\n- Customer churn announcement week: 0 (goal)\n\n**Month 1:**\n- Customer churn rate: <2% (baseline)\n- NRR: >110% (baseline)\n- Product feedback positive: >80% sentiment in calls\n\n**If Churn Spikes >3%:**\n- Emergency retention calls (Founder + CEO Buyer)\n- Offer [X] discount / extended trial on new features\n- Root cause analysis (why leaving? Competitor? Price? Feature concern?)\n\n---\n\n## WHAT TO AVOID\n\n❌ **Overselling integration timelines** (\"Ships in 2 weeks!\" then delays to 2 months)\n❌ **Making promises Buyer won't honor** (\"We'll discount 50%\" without Buyer approval)\n❌ **Acknowledging integration risks** (\"We might sunsetting some features\")\n❌ **Sounding uncertain** (\"Hopefully things go well...\")\n❌ **Over-personalizing** (\"This acquisition happened because of YOU, [CUSTOMER]!\")\n\n---\n\n## CUSTOMER FEEDBACK LOOP\n\n**Post-Call Debrief (Founder → Sales Team):**\n1. Did customer express concern? (Note: concern topic)\n2. Did we resolve it? (Yes/No/Partially)\n3. Are they staying? (Commit / Uncertain / Likely Churn)\n4. Follow-up action?
