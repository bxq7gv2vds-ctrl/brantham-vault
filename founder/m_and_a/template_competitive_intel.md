## SIGNALS YOU'RE NOT THE ONLY BID

- Buyer mentions "we've seen other solutions..."
- Buyer asks "how are you different from [competitor]?"
- Buyer's timeline is generous (8+ weeks = time for other due diligence)
- Buyer asks about price "range" (not your number)

---

## SIGNALS YOU'RE THE FRONT-RUNNER

- Buyer shares internal roadmap / org chart (trust signal)
- Buyer pushes for faster timeline (wants to lock you down)
- Buyer limits LOI discussions (single-bidding for exclusivity)
- Buyer's team is excited (excitement is real when you're the bet)

---

## HOW TO LEVERAGE

**If you're NOT the front-runner:**
- Run parallel buyers, create competitive tension
- Share "other buyer is moving faster" tactfully

**If you ARE the front-runner:**
- Don't rush into bad terms just because they're interested
- Negotiate hard on Tier 1 items (you have leverage)
