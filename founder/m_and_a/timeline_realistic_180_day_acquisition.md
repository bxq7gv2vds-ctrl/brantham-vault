# REALISTIC 180-DAY M&A TIMELINE

**Plan 6 months from initial conversation to close. Here's what happens when.**

---

## MONTH 0 (Week 0–4): INITIAL OUTREACH

| Week | Milestone | Owner | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Week 0 | Warm intro to [3–5] buyers | You + Advisor | Staggered contact |
| Week 1 | Send 1-pager to interested buyers | You | [Seller's Summary template] |
| Week 2 | Initial calls (30 min each) | You | Gauge interest; soft anchor price |
| Week 3–4 | Buyer due diligence request | Buyer | Data room access; first questions |

**Deliverables:** 1-pager; initial NDA; buyer research

---

## MONTH 1 (Week 5–8): ENGAGEMENT & PRELIMINARY OFFERS

| Week | Milestone | Owner | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Week 5 | First formal meeting (1–2 hours) | You + advisors | Deeper conversation; buyer visits office |
| Week 6 | Buyer provides preliminary indication (LOI-lite) | Buyer | "Soft" offer; terms flexible |
| Week 7–8 | Parallel conversations with [2–3] other buyers | You | Multi-track strategy; get comps |

**Deliverables:** Preliminary offers from [2–3] buyers; cap table confirmed; financials updated

---

## MONTH 2 (Week 9–12): FINAL BUYER SELECTION & LOI PREP

| Week | Milestone | Owner | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Week 9 | Compare offers; select top buyer | You + Board | Deal scorecard framework |
| Week 10 | Negotiate LOI terms with top choice | You + Lawyer | [LOI template]; [30] day turnaround |
| Week 11 | Sign LOI with winner | Both | Exclusivity kicks in; other buyers declining |
| Week 12 | Set up data room; assign diligence lead | You | Prepare for intensive diligence |

**Deliverables:** Signed LOI; preliminary data room; diligence lead assigned

---

## MONTH 3 (Week 13–16): BUYER DILIGENCE BEGINS

| Week | Milestone | Owner | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Week 13–16 | Financial diligence | Buyer's accountant | Review 3 years of financials; audit issues |
| Week 13–16 | Technical diligence | Buyer's CTO/Engineers | Code review; architecture assessment |
| Week 13–16 | Customer diligence | Buyer's team | Reference calls with [10–20] customers |
| Week 13–16 | Legal diligence | Buyer's counsel | Contracts; litigation; IP; compliance |

**Deliverables:** Diligence questions answered; reps confirmed; no major issues found

---

## MONTH 4 (Week 17–20): NEGOTIATE FINAL TERMS

| Week | Milestone | Owner | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Week 17 | Buyer identifies [2–3] open issues | Buyer | Working document; not blockers |
| Week 18–19 | Negotiate reps, warranties, escrow | Lawyers | [SPA negotiation]; [Indemnification terms] |
| Week 20 | Diligence wind-down | Both | Final Q&A; close remaining items |

**Deliverables:** Draft SPA; agreed reps/warranties; earnout structure final

---

## MONTH 5 (Week 21–24): SPA FINALIZATION & CLOSING PREP

| Week | Milestone | Owner | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Week 21 | Final SPA draft | Buyer's counsel | [SPA template] circulated |
| Week 22 | SPA redline; negotiate final terms | Your lawyer | Closing statement; working capital adjustment |
| Week 23 | Board approvals | You + Board | Cap table sign-off; shareholder approval |
| Week 24 | Closing readiness; tax planning | You + CPA | Final tax advice; earnout structuring |

**Deliverables:** Final SPA; employment agreements; equity documentation

---

## MONTH 6 (Week 25–26): CLOSING

| Week | Milestone | Owner | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Week 25 | Final closing statement | Buyer + Your CPA | Working capital true-up; adjust price |
| Week 26 | **CLOSING DAY** | All parties | Wire transfer; sign final docs; ownership transfer |

**Deliverables:** Purchase price paid; company ownership transfers; earnout begins

---

## TOTAL TIMELINE SUMMARY

| Phase | Duration | Days | Owner |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Outreach & Engagement** | Month 0–1 | Days 0–30 | You |
| **Buyer Selection & LOI** | Month 1–2 | Days 30–60 | You |
| **Diligence** | Month 3–4 | Days 60–120 | Buyer |
| **Negotiation** | Month 4–5 | Days 120–160 | Lawyers |
| **Closing** | Month 6 | Days 160–180 | All |

---

## KEY MILESTONES (DON'T MISS)

🎯 **Day 60:** LOI signed (exclusivity begins)  
🎯 **Day 120:** Diligence substantively complete (no major issues)  
🎯 **Day 150:** Final SPA agreed (ready to close)  
🎯 **Day 180:** **CLOSED** (money in bank)

---

## WHAT CAUSES DELAYS

⏱️ **Diligence taking longer than expected:** Add [+30] days  
⏱️ **Buyer's internal approvals slow:** Add [+20–30] days  
⏱️ **SPA negotiation drags:** Add [+20] days  
⏱️ **Financing contingency (if any):** Add [+30] days  
⏱️ **Earnout dispute:** Can delay close [+10] days  

**Reality check:** Plan for [180] days; expect [210–240] days (1–2 month overrun normal).

---

## PARALLEL WORKSTREAMS (MANAGE SIMULTANEOUSLY)

During Months 2–6, manage in parallel:
1. **Diligence:** Answer buyer questions
2. **Negotiations:** Lawyer redlines SPA  
3. **Integration planning:** Discuss post-close plans
4. **Employee communication:** Keep team in loop (if appropriate)
5. **Tax planning:** Work with CPA on structure
6. **Earnout prep:** Understand what you'll need to hit targets
