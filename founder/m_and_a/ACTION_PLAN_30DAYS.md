# 30-Day M&A Action Plan — What to Do Next

Use this template to execute in the next 30 days.

---

## **Week 1: Foundation (Days 1–7)**

- [ ] **Day 1–2:** Fill out Walk-Away Price Calculation
- [ ] **Day 1–2:** List 10 buyer targets (Strategic, PE, Founder)
- [ ] **Day 2–3:** Set up Deal Pipeline Tracker
- [ ] **Day 3–4:** Prepare 3-statement financial model (last 12 months)
- [ ] **Day 4–5:** Calculate adjusted EBITDA (add-backs documented)
- [ ] **Day 5–6:** Organize data room (get 50+ documents listed)
- [ ] **Day 6–7:** Get lawyer + accountant intros (email advisors)

**By end of Week 1:** You know your price, targets, and financials.

---

## **Week 2: Outreach (Days 8–14)**

- [ ] **Day 8–9:** Customize 5 outreach emails (Strategic + PE templates)
- [ ] **Day 9–10:** Send emails to top 5 buyers
- [ ] **Day 10–11:** Follow up on non-responses (call if you have number)
- [ ] **Day 12–13:** Get meetings scheduled (aim for 3+)
- [ ] **Day 13–14:** Prep for meetings (read Negotiation Playbook)

**By end of Week 2:** You have 2–3 meetings scheduled.

---

## **Week 3: Meetings & Diligence Prep (Days 15–21)**

- [ ] **Day 15–17:** First buyer meeting (60 min)
  - Tell your story (founder background, market opportunity)
  - Walk through financials (last 24 months)
  - Answer questions (use Q&A Response Protocol)
  
- [ ] **Day 18–19:** Debrief (email summary to buyer, address any concerns)
- [ ] **Day 19–20:** Send NDA for signature (if interested)
- [ ] **Day 20–21:** Prepare data room (upload 50+ docs to DataSite)

**By end of Week 3:** First buyer has NDA + data room access.

---

## **Week 4: Close First LOI (Days 22–30)**

- [ ] **Day 22–24:** Answer buyer's diligence questions (weekly call)
- [ ] **Day 25–26:** Receive LOI draft (review with lawyer)
- [ ] **Day 27–28:** Negotiate LOI terms (use Negotiation Playbook)
- [ ] **Day 29–30:** Sign LOI + celebrate

**By end of Week 4:** First LOI is signed (or you've moved to next buyer if they dropped out).

---

## **What Happens Next (Weeks 5–18)**

After LOI signature, follow the 5-phase process:

| Phase | Weeks | Focus |
|-------|-------|-------|
| **Phase 1: Diligence** | 5–8 | Answer buyer's 100+ questions. Organize data. Handle objections. |
| **Phase 2: Negotiation** | 8–12 | Negotiate earnout, reps, indemnification. Get to deal terms. |
| **Phase 3: Closing Prep** | 12–16 | Legal docs. Disclosure schedules. Tax planning. |
| **Phase 4: Final Close** | 16–18 | Wire transfer. Signature. Celebration. |
| **Phase 5: Earnout Year 1** | 19–52 | Monthly reporting. Hit earnout targets. |

## **If You Hit a Wall**

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| No one responds to emails | Call instead. Email has 30% response rate; calls have 60%+. |
| Meetings go poorly | Review [Negotiation Playbook](template_negotiation_playbook.md) + practice your pitch. |
| Buyer asks hard questions | Use [Objection Handlers](#phase-4-loi--objection-handling) + get lawyer's advice. |
| Deal terms are bad | Know your walk-away. Walk if needed. |
