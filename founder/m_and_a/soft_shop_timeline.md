# Soft Shop Timeline — Critical Path

**Total duration: 12-16 weeks typical**

| Week | Milestone | Owner | Deliverable |
|-----|-----------|-------|-------------|
| **W1-2** | Teaser + outreach list | Advisor + You | Identify 15-20 buyers, send teaser |
| **W3-4** | NDA signatures | Advisor | 5-10 buyers sign NDA, get full deck |
| **W5-6** | Management presentations | You | 4-5 buyer calls (diligence questions) |
| **W7** | First indications of interest | Buyers | 3+ LOIs or term sheets received |
| **W8-9** | Exclusive negotiation | You + Legal | Pick top buyer, negotiate exclusivity |
| **W10-12** | Full due diligence | Buyer + You | Financial audit, tech audit, customer calls |
| **W13-14** | Closing docs + final terms | Legal + Advisor | SPA, reps & warranties, earn-out |
| **W15-16** | Closing | All | Transfer docs, wire funds, keys handed over |

**Red flag:** If W7 → no LOI received = process slow or buyers uninterested. Pivot.

**Velocity goal:** Week 7 → first offer, Week 13 → close
