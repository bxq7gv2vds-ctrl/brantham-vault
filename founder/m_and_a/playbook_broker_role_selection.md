# Broker Role Playbook — Selection & Fee Negotiation

**Brokers = investment bankers focused on M&A. Good for $10M+ deals.**

---

## WHEN TO USE BROKER

✅ **Yes, use broker if:**
- Deal size > $10M (broker fee ROI positive)
- You have zero warm intros to strategic buyers
- You want parallel buyer approach (broker has access)
- You want anonymity (broker sells your "teaser" without naming you)

❌ **No, don't use broker if:**
- Deal size < $5M (fee > 10% is bad)
- You have warm intros to 5+ strategic buyers (do it yourself)
- Broker is exclusive (locks you in for 90+ days)

---

## FEE NEGOTIATION

| Deal Size | Typical Fee | Your Counter |
|---|---|---|
| $5-10M | 1.5-2% | Negotiated to 1% (cap $100K) |
| $10-20M | 1-1.5% | Negotiated to 0.8% |
| $20-50M | 1% | Capped at 0.8% |

**Golden rule:** Broker should NOT be exclusive > 90 days.

---

## RED FLAGS TO REJECT

- ❌ "Exclusive process only"
- ❌ "We'll shop you around, but you're tied to us for 6 months"
- ❌ "Fee is contingent on close" (so they push you to accept bad deals)
