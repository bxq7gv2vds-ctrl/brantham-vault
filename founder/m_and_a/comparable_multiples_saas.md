# SaaS Valuation Multiples — Benchmark 2025-2026

| Growth Rate | EV/ARR Multiple | EV/EBITDA Multiple | Examples | Acquirer Type |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **<20% (slow growth)** | 3-5x | 8-12x | Mature SaaS | Strategic/PE |
| **20-40%** | 5-8x | 12-18x | Normal growth | Strategic |
| **40-60%** | 8-12x | 18-25x | Strong growth | Strategic + VC |
| **60-100%** | 12-20x | 25-35x | Hypergrowth | VC + Strategic |
| **>100%** | 20-35x | 35-50x | Exceptional | Top-tier VCs |

**Recent M&A Examples (adjusted):**
- Figma (80%+ growth, 2024): ~25x ARR
- Canva (60% growth, 2024): ~12x ARR
- HubSpot baseline (30% growth): ~8x ARR

**Your multiplier = Growth% / (EBITDA % + 5%)**
- If 50% growth, 25% EBITDA → 50/(25+5) = 1.67x → use ~8-10x ARR

**Rule of 40:** Growth + EBITDA Margin ≥ 40 = premium valuation
