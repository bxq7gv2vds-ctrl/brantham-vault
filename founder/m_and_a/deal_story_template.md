# Deal Story — 1-Page Narrative

**Utilisation** : Email, appel de découverte, teaser PDF. Répond : "Pourquoi ce deal existe, pourquoi maintenant, pourquoi vous?"

**Longueur** : 500 mots max. Doit se lire en 2 min.

---

## Template à adapter

### 1. Opening — The Problem (50 words)

**[Secteur] companies lose $[X]M yearly when [problem]. This costs them [consequence].**

_Exemples :_
- "Hair salon managers waste 6 hours/week on scheduling. This costs them $4K/month in missed appointments."
- "SME accounting firms spend 15% of revenue on compliance. This is pure waste."
- "[Niche] has no centralized [solution]. Teams use 5+ tools."

**Your hook** : Lead with *their pain*, not your product.

---

### 2. Meet the Company (100 words)

**Name** : [Nom]
**Founded** : [Année]
**Founder(s)** : [Names + 1-line bios]
**Stage** : [ARR / MRR / pre-revenue]
**Location** : [City]

**What they built** :
[1-sentence product]. Used by [#customers] companies including [2 names]. Solves [problem] by [mechanism].

**Traction snapshot** :
- ARR: $[X] (up [%] YoY)
- Customer count: [#] (retention [%])
- NPS: [#] / Team: [#] people
- Margins: [%] EBITDA

_Why this matters_ : Shows momentum, stickiness, profitability.

---

### 3. The Market (75 words)

**Addressable market** : $[XB] TAM in [sector] across [geography].
**Trend** : [Why now? Regulation, shift to remote, new tech, consolidation?]
**Competition** : [# competitors], but [unique angle].

_Keep this honest._ Buyers hate fake TAM. Say "conservative estimate" if needed.

---

### 4. Why a Buyer Should Acquire (75 words)

**For Strategic Buyers** :
- Revenue synergies : [Detailed — how does this fit your product ecosystem?]
- Cost synergies : [Eliminate duplicate roles, infrastructure, sales cost.]
- Customer cross-sell : [# mutual customers you could upsell.]

**For Financial Buyers / PE** :
- Margin improvement path : [SG&A reduction, price increase, automation.]
- Add-on to platform : [Bolt-on revenue for [X] existing portfolio company.]
- Roll-up opportunity : [Consolidate [sector] fragmentation + exit in 3-5y.]

---

### 5. The Ask & Timeline (75 words)

**Asking price** : $[X]M cash + [earn-out structure if relevant].
**Rationale** : [X] multiple of ARR based on [comparable company / buyer profile].

**Why this price is fair** :
- Growing at [%] YoY (vs. industry [%])
- [X]% EBITDA margin (vs. [Y]% peers)
- [Unique asset — IP, customer list, team, market position]

**Timeline** : Seeking to close by [date]. Flexible on structure.

---

### 6. Why Now? (50 words)

**For founder exit** :
- Market opportunity is shifting; expansion requires capital [founder doesn't have].
- Team has other priorities / founder want to move on to [next thing].
- Buyer is best positioned to scale this → win-win.

_Don't apologize for selling._ Frame as "right buyer for next phase."

## Real Example (Fill-In)

**[Your Company Name] — Deal Story**

> Hair salon owners waste 6 hours/week managing staff schedules. This costs them $4-6K/month in missed shifts and customer churn.
>
> [Your Company] automated salon scheduling across 400+ salons. Users report 70% time savings and 12% revenue lift. ARR is $[X]M, up 150% YoY, with 85% NPS.
>
> The salon market ($180B globally) is consolidating — large chains want vertical software. We built [Your product] for independent salons, but strategic buyers (major salon chains, POS providers) see it as a must-have to retain customers.
>
> We're asking $[X]M based on [comparable acquisition multiples]. Timeline: close by [month]. Happy to structure earn-outs tied to retention KPIs.

## Distribution Strategy

- **Teaser email** : Excerpt (opening + traction)
- **Buyer call (discovery)** : Full story, 2-min read during call
- **LOI stage** : Story is now part of data room (reference document)
- **Q&A process** : Buyer questions against this narrative

---

## Anti-Patterns to Avoid

❌ "This is the next Uber of [space]"  
✅ "We serve [specific niche] with [specific problem]"

❌ "Market is $100B"  
✅ "Conservative TAM is $[X]M; we're going after [segment]"

❌ "Founder has 20 years experience"  
✅ "Founder scaled [past company] from $[Y] to $[Z], sold to [buyer]"

❌ "Perfect timing to sell"  
✅ "Buyer is uniquely positioned to leverage our customer base + distribution"
