# NDA Templates — 4 Variations for Different Buyer Stages

**Use based on buyer type + stage. Never negotiate NDA terms; use template as-is.**

---

## NDA #1: Cold Intro / Teaser (Minimal)

**For:** Initial cold email intro, teaser one-pager only.

**Duration:** 3 years

**Key clauses:**
- Confidential Info = teaser + discussion context only
- No obligation to proceed with sale
- Either party can terminate with 5 days notice
- Return / destroy docs within 10 days of termination

**Use case:** "Before we share any detail, let's sign a light NDA. Takes 5 min, covers both of us."

**Link:** [Simple 2-pager NDA from SAFE / Y Combinator](https://www.ycombinator.com/documents)

---

## NDA #2: Warm Intro / Serious Buyer (Standard)

**For:** Broker intros, warm intros, buyer has signed LOI intent.

**Duration:** 5 years (covers post-sale period)

**Key clauses:**
- Confidential Info = company financials, customer list, tech details
- Permitted use = evaluation only (no competitive use)
- Permitted disclosures = legal counsel, accountants, lenders (bound by similar terms)
- Return / destroy within 15 days of termination
- Survival clause = obligations continue 5 years post-termination

**Use case:** "We're ready to share detailed info. Here's our standard NDA."

**Red flags to reject:**
- ❌ "Perpetual confidentiality" (too long; negotiate to 5 years)
- ❌ "No permitted disclosures" (you can't show lawyer/accountant; reject)
- ❌ "Non-compete for 5 years post-signature" (this is LOI, not NDA; reject)

---

## NDA #3: Buyer NDA (Your Seller Version)

**For:** Buyer brings their template NDA (most will).

**Key edits to demand:**
- Duration: Change from perpetual → 5 years
- Definition: Add "public information available in [date] is not confidential"
- Permitted use: Add "evaluation AND potential acquisition"
- Disclosures: Ensure lawyer/accountant/lender included
- Cure period: Add 10-day notice to cure before breach claim
- Remove non-compete clause entirely (belongs in LOI, not NDA)

---

## NDA #4: Post-LOI / Late Stage (Seller Protective)

**For:** After LOI signed, sharing sensitive financial details.

**Duration:** 7 years (post-close)

**Extra clauses:**
- Permitted use = "closing acquisition only"
- Buyer shall indemnify seller for unauthorized use of confidential info
- Buyer shall maintain standard data security (SOC 2 level)
- Data breach notification = 24 hours
- Survival clause = 7 years post-deal close

**Use case:** "Now that LOI is signed, here's the detailed financial data. Stronger confidentiality terms apply."

---

## NEGOTIATION SCRIPT

**Buyer:** "We need to sign our NDA first."

**You:** "Perfect, I have a standard template [Option 1 or 2]. Takes 5 minutes. Changes allowed: [list 2-3 non-negotiable items like duration, permitted disclosures]. Everything else we can flex on."

**Buyer:** "Our legal insists on our template."

**You:** "Totally understand. Send it over. I'll mark red-lines for [item A, B, C] — standard seller protections. We can align in 48 hours."

---

## RED LINES (DON'T MOVE)

| Item | Your Position | Why |
|---|---|---|
| **Duration** | 3-5 years max (7 if post-LOI) | Perpetual is oppressive |
| **Permitted Disclosures** | Lawyer, CPA, lender, investor | You need advisors |
| **Non-Compete in NDA** | REJECT — belongs in LOI | NDA = confidentiality only |
| **Audit Rights** | REJECT — belongs in LOI | NDA doesn't need data access |
| **Indemnification** | Add mutual clause (if buyer breaches, seller recovers) | Protects both sides |
