# Teaser Template — 1 Page (Pré-NDA)

**Objectif :** Un document d'1 page (max) pour intéresser sans révéler l'identité exacte ni les chiffres détaillés.

**Timing :** Envoyé immédiatement après email, avec signature teaser visible seulement si acheteur signe NDA.

## Deployment Checklist

- [ ] Teaser approved by legal (NDA strategy, IP safe)
- [ ] Teaser approved by board/investors (valuation range OK?)
- [ ] Teaser saved in password-protected folder
- [ ] Distribution list prepared (emails, not downloads)
- [ ] Tracking: who got it, when, did they ask for NDA?
- [ ] NDA template ready to send same-day if interest

---

## What to AVOID

❌ Revealing customer names (pre-NDA)  
❌ Specific product roadmap (IP risk)  
❌ Deep financials or customer acquisition cost (leverage risk)  
❌ Founders' personal details (privacy)  
❌ Admissions of weakness (churn, product issues, etc.)  
❌ Multiple pages (kills conversion; they won't read it)

---

## What to HIGHLIGHT

✅ Growth rate (bigger is better)  
✅ Unit economics defensibility (NRR, churn)  
✅ Market TAM (big = better valuation)  
✅ Founder pedigree (track record > degree)  
✅ Competitive moat (why us not others)  
✅ Team staying (rare, valuable)  
✅ Synergy potential (not generic, specific to acquirer type)
