# Founder Retention Letter — Template

---

## [BUYER_NAME] | Retention & Employment Offer

**To:** [Founder Name]  
**Effective Date:** [Close Date]  
**Position:** [VP/Chief Product Officer/Advisor/Head of Product]  

---

## I. Role & Duties

**Title:** [Title]  
**Reporting to:** [CEO / CTO / Board]  
**Responsibilities:** [Vague is OK here; specificity comes in employment agreement]

---

## II. Compensation

### Cash Salary
- **Base Salary:** $[X]/year (or $[Y]/month)
- **Target Bonus:** [20-50%] of base (performance-based)

### Equity
- **Sign-On Bonus:** [N] shares / $[X] cash (if forfeited equity from prior company)
- **Vesting Schedule:** [New/accelerated] grant of [N] shares
  - **Vesting:** 4-year vest, 1-year cliff (standard)
  - **Acceleration:** [25-50%] accelerates upon [change of control / termination without cause]

---

## III. Earnout & Retention Bonus

**Retention Bonus** (Golden Handcuffs):
- **Tranche 1:** $[X] paid [12 months post-close] if still employed
- **Tranche 2:** $[Y] paid [24 months post-close] if still employed

*(Or: Earnout tied to performance metric — see main LOI)*

---

## IV. Benefits

- **Health Insurance:** [Buyer's standard plan; Buyer covers X%]
- **401(k):** [Standard match]
- **PTO:** [X days/year]
- **Other:** [Relocation package / sign-on bonus / etc.]

---

## V. Termination Protection

### Termination for Cause
- **Definition:** Felony conviction, gross negligence, willful misconduct, breach of confidentiality
- **Severance:** None

### Termination Without Cause
- **Severance:** [6-12 months] base salary
- **Bonus:** [Prorated] for current year
- **Acceleration:** [25-50%] of unvested equity accelerates
- **Earnout:** [50-100%] of earnout paid (if termination due to buyer's decision, not you)

### Good Leaver / Bad Leaver
- **Good Leaver:** You quit or retire after [12-24m]; earn accrued earnout
- **Bad Leaver:** You quit in first [12m]; forfeit earnout

---

## VI. Restrictive Covenants

### Non-Compete
- **Geography:** [Worldwide / local]
- **Duration:** [6-12 months post-employment]
- **Scope:** Cannot solicit [customers / employees] or work in [competitive product area]

### Non-Solicitation
- **Employees:** Cannot recruit or solicit [key employees] for [X years]
- **Customers:** Cannot solicit [top 20 customers] for [12-24 months]

---

## VII. Earnout Clarity

**Earnout Metric:** [Same as LOI]  
**Earnout Payout:** [50% at Month 12, 50% at Month 24] — Vests regardless of employment  
**Earnout Calculation:** Independent audit by [third party]

---

## VIII. Dispute Resolution

- **Governing Law:** [State]
- **Disputes:** Resolved via [arbitration / litigation in [County]]
- **Attorney Fees:** [Prevailing party recovers fees]

---

## Founder Checklist Before Signing

- [ ] **Title is real** (not "advisor" when you're chief architect)
- [ ] **Salary is market** ($150k+ for founder in major company)
- [ ] **Earnout is independent of employment** (vests even if fired)
- [ ] **Severance is 6+ months** (if no earnout)
- [ ] **Equity acceleration is meaningful** (≥25% upon termination without cause)
- [ ] **Non-compete is scoped** (not "all software" forever)
- [ ] **Retention bonus is paid monthly/quarterly** (not a lump sum at end)
- [ ] **No clawback if buyer's decision deprioritizes product**
- [ ] **You have board seat or observer rights** (stay informed)

---

## Negotiation Red Flags

| ❌ Red Flag | ✅ Counter |
|-----------|----------|
| No severance | Demand 6+ months or earnout vesting |
| "At-will" employment, no severance | Use severance as substitute for earnout |
| Non-compete >12m | Counter with 6 months |
| Earnout tied to employment | Make it independent |
| Equity with 4-year vest, no acceleration | Get acceleration at 25-50% upon termination |
| "Buyer can fire you anytime" | Specify "without cause" severance |

**If buyer won't budge on severance:** Demand higher earnout + front-loaded retention bonuses instead.
