## Red Flags (Stop If Present)

- ❌ Co-founder still employed but disputes terms
- ❌ SAFE/note terms include "double-trigger" M&A acceleration
- ❌ Employee options vest on M&A (could drain 30%+ of proceeds)
- ❌ Any shareholder not signing consent to sale
- ❌ Unknown liens or claims against the company
