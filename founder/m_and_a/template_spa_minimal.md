# STOCK PURCHASE AGREEMENT (SIMPLIFIED)

**THIS AGREEMENT** is made as of [Date] ("Effective Date") between [BUYER], a [State] corporation ("Buyer"), and [SELLER], a [State] corporation ("Seller").

---

## 1. PURCHASE & SALE

**Buyer agrees to purchase, and Seller agrees to sell, all issued and outstanding capital stock of Seller (the "Shares") for the Purchase Price.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Base Purchase Price | $[X]M |
| Less: Assumed Liabilities | $([Y]M) |
| Less: Closing Adjustment | $([Z]M) |
| **Net Cash at Close** | $[A]M |

---

## 2. CLOSING

**Closing Date:** [Date, typically 5 business days after satisfaction of closing conditions]

**At Closing, Seller delivers:**
- Stock certificates, signed stock powers
- Secretary's certificate (authority, cap table certification)
- Resignations of officers/directors
- Bill of sale (for all assets)

**Buyer delivers:**
- Purchase price via wire transfer

---

## 3. REPRESENTATIONS & WARRANTIES (18-Month Survival)

**Seller represents and warrants:**

### (a) Organization
- Seller is duly organized, validly existing [State] corporation
- Has full power to execute this Agreement

### (b) Cap Table
- Schedule 3(b) lists all issued shares, options, warrants, convertibles
- No side letters, voting agreements, or option modifications
- All options fully vested or subject to [describe vesting schedule]

### (c) Intellectual Property
- All IP developed by Seller is owned by Seller or properly licensed
- No infringement claims pending or threatened
- No third-party IP embedded in product [except: open-source listed in Schedule 3(c)]

### (d) Contracts
- All material contracts (customers >$[X]k, vendors, employment) listed in Schedule 3(d)
- No material breaches; change-of-control clauses disclosed
- Seller may assume/assign or buyer may terminate (list exceptions in Schedule 3(d)-2)

### (e) Financials
- Financial statements for years [20X1, 20X2, 20X3] are accurate and complete
- Include all assets, liabilities, equity
- Prepared using consistent accounting methods
- No undisclosed liabilities >$[Y]K

### (f) Compliance
- All tax filings made and taxes paid through close
- All labor/employment laws, regulations complied with
- No pending litigation, settlements, or threatened claims

### (g) Data & Privacy
- No security breaches in past [2] years; all disclosed if any
- GDPR, CCPA, HIPAA (if applicable) compliant; customer data properly handled
- All customer data will transfer to Buyer free of claims

### (h) Employees
- All employees listed in Schedule 3(h) with equity holdings, vesting status
- No secret agreements, equity side letters, or acceleration clauses
- All termination obligations disclosed

---

## 4. INDEMNIFICATION

### (a) Seller Indemnification
**Seller indemnifies Buyer against losses >$[50]K aggregate (with $[200]K basket) caused by:**
- Breach of Seller's reps/warranties
- Breach of Seller's covenants
- Undisclosed liabilities
- Breach of IP warranty

**Cap:** $[X]M (typically 10-15% of purchase price)

**Survival:** 18 months from close (except IP: indefinite; tax: 3 years)

### (b) Earnout Seller Indemnification
If earnout target missed due to Seller's breach of reps/warranties → earnout reduced $[amount] per [metric] miss.

### (c) Insurance
Seller maintains [D&O liability insurance / representation & warranty insurance] with Buyer named beneficiary through [survival period].

---

## 5. WORKING CAPITAL ADJUSTMENT

**Target Working Capital:** $[X] (definition: current assets − current liabilities, excluding cash & debt)

**Calculation at Close:**
- Buyer calculates closing working capital within [30] days post-close
- If closing WC > target: Buyer pays Seller difference (max $[Y])
- If closing WC < target: Seller pays Buyer difference (max $[Z])

**Dispute Resolution:** Either party may dispute via independent auditor (cost split); determination binding.

---

## 6. EARNOUT (if applicable)

**Earnout Structure:**
- **Target:** [Revenue/EBITDA target for Year 1, Year 2, Year 3]
- **Calculation:** Measured per Schedule 6(a); audited by Buyer's CPA
- **Payment:** [Quarterly/annually] within [30] days of measurement
- **Cure Right:** Seller may audit within [60] days of preliminary earnout calculation

**Example:**
- If Y1 Revenue = $[10]M (target $[9]M): Earnout = $[1]M × [formula]
- If Y1 Revenue < $[9]M: Earnout = $0 (no catch-up in future years)

**Clawback:**
- If Seller breaches material rep/warranty → Earnout reduced by [50]%
- If [key person] departs within 12 months → Earnout forfeited
- If Seller non-competes violation → Full earnout clawback

---

## 7. COVENANTS & CLOSING CONDITIONS

### (a) Pre-Closing
- Seller to obtain third-party consents (Schedule 3(d)-2) or obtain waivers
- Seller to operate business in ordinary course until close
- No new debt, equity issuance, or material expense >$[amount]

### (b) Non-Compete & Confidentiality
- Seller agrees to [1–3 year] non-compete within [geography/industry]
- Non-solicitation of employees for [12] months
- Confidentiality of Buyer's systems/strategy for [3] years

### (c) Closing Conditions
**Buyer's obligation to close contingent on:**
- Seller reps/warranties true and accurate at close
- No MAC (loss >25% of revenue, departure of [key person], litigation >$[X])
- Third-party consents obtained

**Seller's obligation to close contingent on:**
- Buyer's ability to fund purchase price
- No material breach of Buyer's obligations

### (d) Termination
If closing conditions not satisfied by [Date + 90 days], either party may terminate; all obligations cease except confidentiality.

---

## 8. MISCELLANEOUS

- **Governing Law:** [Delaware/New York]
- **Entire Agreement:** This SPA + Exhibits supersedes all prior LOIs, term sheets, discussions
- **Amendment:** Written consent of both parties only; no oral modifications
- **Expenses:** Each party bears its own legal/accounting costs
- **Waiver:** Waiver of one breach ≠ waiver of future breaches or other provisions
- **Severability:** If one clause is void, rest survives
- **Assignment:** Neither party may assign without written consent (except Buyer to affiliate)
- **Notices:** Via email/courier to addresses in signature blocks

---

## SCHEDULES

- **Schedule 1:** Closing Statement (final purchase price, WC adjustment)
- **Schedule 2:** Assumed Liabilities (list)
- **Schedule 3(b):** Cap Table (fully diluted, all convertibles)
- **Schedule 3(c):** IP Assets & Open-Source Licenses
- **Schedule 3(d):** Material Contracts (customers, vendors, employment)
- **Schedule 3(d)-2:** Consents Required & Status
- **Schedule 3(e):** Financial Statements (years 1-3)
- **Schedule 3(f):** Pending Litigation, Tax Issues
- **Schedule 3(h):** Employees, Option Grants, Equity Holdings
- **Schedule 6(a):** Earnout Calculation Formula & Milestones
