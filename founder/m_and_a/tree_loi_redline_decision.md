# LOI Redline Decision Tree — 30 Flags & Auto-Recommendations

**Buyer sends LOI with 30+ terms. Use this tree to decide: FIGHT / CONCEDE / WALK.**

## PRICE TERMS (3 flags)

| Flag | Buyer's Offer | Your Response |
|---|---|---|
| **F1: Price Below Anchor** | $13M (vs your $18M ask) | FIGHT if > 25% below; else CONCEDE and compensate with earnout |
| **F2: Earnout % Too High** | 40% of deal in earnout | FIGHT (30% max); tie to revenue-only metrics |
| **F3: No Hold-Back** | All cash, no escrow | CONCEDE (good for you) |
