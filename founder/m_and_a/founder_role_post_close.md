# Founder Role Post-Close — Negotiation Template

**Use** : Before LOI signing. Lock in your role, title, compensation, and exit plan.

---

## Negotiation Framework

### Option A: Full Exit (Clean Break)

**Role** : Consultant only (if needed). 30-day transition. Then goodbye.

**Compensation** :
- Cash at close: 100% of sale price (no earnout).
- Earnout: $0 (buyer owns all upside).
- Advisor fee (optional): $5-10K/month × 3 months (transition support).

**Pros** : Freedom immediately. No earnout risk. Tax optimization (lump sum).

**Cons** : Buyer won't pay full price (5-15% haircut for loss of founder). Earnout not available.

**Timeline** : 30-90 days to transition.

---

### Option B: Founder Stays (CEO/Advisor)

**Role** : CEO or Chief Product Officer (your choice). Full employment.

**Compensation** :
- Base salary: $[X]/year (usually 50% of pre-sale salary, raised 5-10% per year).
- Equity: [%] of new buyer's stock (if buyer is PE-backed or acquisition is consolidation).
- Earnout: $[X] tied to KPIs (revenue, retention, milestones).

**Example** :
```
Cash at close: $1.2M
Earnout (24 months): $300K if:
├─ ARR ≥ $600K (Year 1 end) → $150K
└─ NRR ≥95% + churn <5% (Year 2 end) → $150K

Base salary post-close: $120K/year (you were making $150K pre-sale)
Equity: 0.5% of buyer's company (if buyer has future exit, you get piece)
```

**Pros** : Larger sale price (buyer sees founder value). Earnout incentivizes you to succeed. Continued income.

**Cons** : Work for buyer (may not suit you). Earnout at risk if metrics miss.

**Timeline** : 12-24 months (then transition to advisor or exit).

---

### Option C: Founder Moves to Advisory Board

**Role** : Board advisor or strategic advisor. Part-time (5-10h/week).

**Compensation** :
- Base salary: $[X] (reduced 70-80% vs CEO role).
- Equity: [%] (if applicable).
- Earnout: 50% of original earnout (lower incentive).

**Example** :
```
Cash at close: $1.3M (full price + advisor premium)
Earnout (12 months): $100K if NRR ≥95%
Base salary: $30K/year (very part-time)
Equity: 0.25%
```

**Pros** : Flexibility. Lower workload. Still earn money. Credibility with buyer.

**Cons** : Lower earnout. Less influence on product decisions.

**Timeline** : 12 months, then exit.

---

## Key Negotiation Points

### 1. Title & Role Clarity

**Lock this in SPA:**
```
"Founder shall serve as [CEO/CTO/Chief Product Officer] for [12] months 
following closing. Responsibilities include:
├─ [Customer retention & success]
├─ [Product roadmap / strategic direction]
├─ [Team leadership / hiring]
└─ [Board participation / reporting]

Review at month 6: If expectations misaligned, either party can renegotiate."
```

**Why it matters** : Avoids "founder does nothing" scenario post-close.

---

### 2. Earnout KPIs (Tie to Your Behavior)

**Bad** : "Earnout if ARR >$600K" (you can't control market conditions).

**Good** : "Earnout if NRR ≥95%, churn <5%, customer satisfaction ≥8/10" (you control these).

**Example** :
```
40% of earnout: Revenue KPI (you drive this)
30% of earnout: Retention KPI (you drive this)
20% of earnout: Team satisfaction / no departures (you drive this)
10% of earnout: Product roadmap (you drive this)
```

---

### 3. Exit Timeline (When Can You Leave?)

**Lock in SPA:**
```
Month 1-6: Founder required full-time
Month 7-12: Can transition to 50% time (if handoff complete)
Month 12+: Can exit or renegotiate role

OR

Earnout vests over 24 months:
├─ 25% at closing
├─ 25% at month 6 (if KPI hit)
├─ 25% at month 12 (if KPI hit)
└─ 25% at month 24 (if cumulative KPI hit)
```

**Why it matters** : Gives you exit path without leaving money on table.

---

### 4. Equity (If Buyer is Growing/PE-Backed)

**Negotiate** : Slice of buyer's company (0.1-1%).

**Why** : If buyer sells in 3-5 years at 3x multiple, your 0.5% = extra €200K+.

**Example** :
```
You get: 0.5% of buyer's company
Vesting: Monthly over 24 months (4-year cliff)
Participation: Pro-rata in future rounds + exit
```

---

### 5. Severance (Downside Protection)

**Lock in SPA:**
```
"If buyer terminates founder without cause within [12] months:
├─ Founder receives [3-6 months] salary + benefits
├─ Earnout proceeds normally
└─ Any unvested equity accelerates [25-50%]"
```

**Why it matters** : Protects you if buyer's direction changes post-close.

---

## Negotiation Scripts

| Situation | What to Say |
|-----------|------------|
| Buyer wants you to stay 2 years | "Happy to stay. I want clear KPIs + exit path at month 12. And earnout tied to what I control." |
| Buyer offers low base salary | "Understand cost controls. But I was making $X pre-sale. We can bridge gap with equity in your company." |
| No equity offered | "If there's no upside beyond earnout, my incentives are misaligned with buyer's. Let's add 0.25% equity." |
| Earnout KPI seems unfair | "I control retention & product. I don't control macro market. Let's weight earnout 50% retention, 30% revenue, 20% ops." |

## Template: SPA Language

**Section: Post-Closing Engagement**

```
3.1 Founder Role
Founder shall serve as [Title] for [period]. Duties: [list]. 
Reporting to: [Buyer's CEO / Board].

3.2 Compensation
Base salary: $[X]/year (payable monthly).
Annual review: +[%] each year.
Benefits: [Health insurance / equity / other].

3.3 Earnout
Total earnout: $[X] over [period].
Tied to: [List KPIs], measured quarterly.
Acceleration: If founder terminates without cause, earnout vests.

3.4 Exit Path
Founder may transition to part-time at month [6], provided handoff complete.
Founder may exit at month [12], subject to [notice / transition plan].

3.5 Equity (if applicable)
Founder receives [%] of buyer company stock.
Vesting: Monthly, 4-year vest, 1-year cliff.
```

## Real Examples

### Example 1: SaaS Founder, PE Acquisition

```
Price: $2M cash + $500K earnout
Founder role: CEO, 24 months
Base salary: $100K/year
Equity: 0.5% of buyer
Earnout: 
├─ $250K if NRR ≥95% (Year 1)
└─ $250K if ARR ≥$1.2M (Year 2)
Exit path: Month 12 transition to advisor, month 24 full exit
```

### Example 2: Solo Founder, Strategic Acquisition

```
Price: $1.5M cash only (no earnout)
Founder role: Advisor, 12 months (10h/week)
Base salary: $30K/year
Equity: 0.1% of buyer
Earnout: None (founder already got paid)
Exit path: Month 12 clean exit
```

## Avoid These Mistakes

❌ "We'll figure out the role later"  
✅ "Role, compensation, and KPIs locked in SPA before we sign"

❌ "I'll stay as CEO if buyer wants me to" (vague)  
✅ "I'll stay as CEO for 24 months. At month 12, we renegotiate or I transition."

❌ "Earnout tied to buyer's goals, not mine"  
✅ "Earnout tied to KPIs I control: retention, product delivery, team satisfaction"

---

## Checklist Before Signing SPA

- [ ] Role title & duties clearly defined?
- [ ] Base salary competitive with market?
- [ ] Earnout KPIs aligned with your behavior?
- [ ] Exit path clear (when can you leave)?
- [ ] Equity negotiated (if applicable)?
- [ ] Severance protection included?
- [ ] Sign-off from tax advisor (on earnout structure)?
