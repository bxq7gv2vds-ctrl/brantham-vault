# M&A Teaser — 1 Page Pre-NDA

**FROM:** [Your Lawyer Name], [Law Firm]  
**TO:** [Buyer CFO/CEO Name]  
**DATE:** [Today]  
**RE:** Strategic Opportunity — [Your Company]

---

## Opening

We represent [Your Company], a leading provider of [1-line category] for [customer type]. Our clients are exploring strategic options, which may include a partnership or acquisition with a strong strategic buyer.

---

## Why You? (3 lines)
- You serve [buyer's customer segment] + would benefit from [your advantage]
- [Specific synergy] could unlock [specific outcome] for your [product/platform]
- Your [distribution/customer base] + our [technology/IP] = cost synergy of ~$[X]M annually

---

## What You Get (5 lines)

**Traction:**
- $[X]M ARR (growing [Y]% YoY), [Z] customers including [2-3 brand names]
- [Key metric]: [Value] (e.g., NRR: 120%, Churn: <3%/m)
- Profitability: [EBITDA margin], cash flow positive since [date]

---

## Market Opportunity
- TAM: $[X]B, growing at [Y]% CAGR
- Positioned in [white space]: [1-line differentiation]

---

## Unit Economics
- CAC: $[X], LTV: $[Y], Payback: [Z]m
- Gross margin: [X]%, Rule of 40 score: [score]

---

## Why Now?
- [Market trend] accelerating (cite industry report)
- [Regulatory change] creating tailwinds for [your solution]
- [Competitive pressure] intensifying

---

## Next Steps
If interested, [Your Lawyer] will arrange a brief call with [Your CEO] to discuss strategic fit. Process will include:
1. Initial conversation (30m)
2. NDA + data room access (if mutual interest)
3. Management presentation
4. Final buyer due diligence

**Confidentiality:** All information is privileged and confidential. Do not forward without approval.

---

## Key Numbers (Keep Visible)
- **Valuation Range:** $[X]M–$[Y]M (based on [X]x revenue multiple for SaaS)
- **Timeline:** Process closes [Month], buyer expected to sign LOI within [weeks]
- **Process:** [# other buyers] in parallel review

---

## DO's
✓ Lead with why _they_ benefit, not why you're selling  
✓ Use concrete numbers, not percentages  
✓ Include a social proof (customer name or industry award)  
✓ Keep it to 1 page — make them ask for more  

## DON'Ts
✗ Don't overpromise (earnout is coming — be conservative here)  
✗ Don't mention your burn rate or liability issues  
✗ Don't name competitors explicitly (hint, don't name)  
✗ Don't say "we're running out of cash" — ever
