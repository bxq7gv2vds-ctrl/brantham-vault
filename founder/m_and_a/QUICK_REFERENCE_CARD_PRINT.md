📞 SCRIPT TEMPLATES (Memorize these)

  OUTREACH: "I'm exploring strategic options. We've built [X] for [Y]. 
             We're $[ARR]M ARR, [growth]% growth. Interested in 30 min?"

  PRICING:  "I appreciate the offer. My floor is $[BATNA]M. That's [multiple]× 
             our ARR for [growth]% growth. Fair value is $[range]M."

  EARNOUT: "Earnout should be <20% of deal, on metrics I control 
            (not buyer's cost savings)."

  WALK-AWAY: "This isn't working for me. My BATNA is $[X]M. If you can't 
             get there, let's stay friends but I'm building independently."

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

🚩 RED FLAGS (Stop if 2+ are true)

  ☐ Churn >10% per month
  ☐ Top 3 customers = >70% ARR
  ☐ Cap table is a mess (founders don't agree on shares)
  ☐ Tech debt is catastrophic (code unmaintainable, founders only)
  ☐ Legal liability exists (pending lawsuit, IP unclear, customer contracts blocked)
  ☐ Earnout metric is "buyer's profit" (uncontrollable)
  ☐ Reps cap is uncapped (unlimited liability)
  ☐ Buyer is unstable (layoffs, financing at risk, CEO changing)
  ☐ Founder forced out in <6 months (no stay opportunity)

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

💰 DEAL SCORECARD (When you have 2+ LOIs)

  For each buyer, score 1-10 on:
    1. Price & terms (35%)
    2. Certainty & risk (25%)
    3. Buyer fit (20%)
    4. Post-close opportunity (15%)
    5. Timeline (5%)

  If total < 5.5/10 → Don't take it
  If total 5.5-7.5 → Consider it, try to improve
  If total > 7.5 → Serious contender

RULES TO LIVE BY

  ✅ ALWAYS: Have co-founder alignment before any big decision
  ✅ ALWAYS: Know your BATNA before negotiating (don't wing it)
  ✅ ALWAYS: Create FOMO ("we have other offers") even if you don't (yet)
  ✅ ALWAYS: Anchor first (buyer's offer is never their best)
  ✅ ALWAYS: Be honest about red flags (they WILL find them)

  ❌ NEVER: Accept earnout you can't control
  ❌ NEVER: Sign uncapped reps (disaster tail risk)
  ❌ NEVER: Accept founder departure <12 months post-close
  ❌ NEVER: Hide tech debt or legal issues
  ❌ NEVER: Let one buyer think they're exclusive (until LOI)
