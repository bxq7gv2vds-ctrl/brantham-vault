## BY USE CASE (Jump to your immediate need)

### "I want to sell my company"
→ Start here:
1. `AUDIT_READINESS_SELF_CHECK.md` (20 min)
2. `pricing_strategy_initial_ask.md` (30 min)
3. `email_templates_m_and_a.md` (15 min)
4. Deploy `buyer_tracking_sheet.md` (tracking)

### "Buyer sent a lowball offer (50% below ask)"
→ Use immediately:
1. `competitive_response_playbook.md` — Counter with data
2. `financial_normalization_template.md` — Build your EBITDA bridge
3. `12_MULTIPLES_BENCHMARKS_INDUSTRY.md` — Cite comps
4. `objections_playbook.md` — Preempt buyer pushback

### "Deal is at LOI stage, now negotiating SPA"
→ Deploy now:
1. `deal_structure_comparison.md` — Understand terms
2. `warranty_indemnity_risks.md` — What to flag
3. `founder_transition_guide.md` — Negotiate your role
4. `batna_card_template.md` — Know your walk-away

### "30 days to close, managing checklist"
→ Activate:
1. `preclosing_checklist_100.md` — Ops readiness (assign owners)
2. `integration_risk_matrix_2_0.md` — Risk mitigation
3. `customer_winback_kit.md` — Draft emails
4. `legal_red_flags_quick_ref.md` — SPA review

### "Deal closed, now managing earnout + retention"
→ Execute:
1. `founder_transition_guide.md` — Hit earnout milestones
2. `post_close_kpi_dashboard_template.md` — Track weekly
3. `customer_winback_kit.md` — Send retention emails (D0, D7, D30)
4. `FOUNDER_NEGOTIATION_SCRIPTS.md` — Manage tough convos

### "Buyer asking tough questions in DD"
→ Prepare:
1. `checklist_due_diligence_acquereur.md` — Anticipate all asks
2. `warranty_indemnity_risks.md` — Know liability exposure
3. `financial_normalization_template.md` — Have EBITDA ready
