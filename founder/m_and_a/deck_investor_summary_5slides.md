## Design Tips

- **Use brand colors** (consistent with website)
- **One visual per slide** (chart, icon, product screenshot)
- **Large fonts** (18pt body min; 28pt headlines)
- **Data over jargon** (show numbers, not buzzwords)
- **White space** (don't cram)

---

## How to Use

1. **Warm intro call (5 min):** Lead with slide 1. If buyer interested, walk through 2–5.
2. **Email teaser:** Attach slides 1 + 3. "Quick overview; happy to dig deeper."
3. **Dataroom:** Upload as PDF. Let buyer absorb offline.
4. **Meeting prep:** Buyer may ask you to present first 10 min. Slides 1–3. Then Q&A.
