# Red Flags M&A — Par Phase

**Chaque flag = action immédiate. Pas "on verra", pas "probablement OK".**

---

## PHASE 1 : Prep

| Flag | Trigger | Action |
|------|---------|--------|
| **"CIM rejeté"** | Buyer dit "pas assez convaincant" | Réécrire narrative : moins buzzword, plus traction chiffrée |
| **"Valuation folle"** | Vous demandez 10x ARR, market fait 3-4x | Lower ask ou pivot vers buyer type (ex: strategic premium) |
| **"Financials pas propres"** | Accountant dit "EBITDA pas clair" | Add-backs documentés ou abandon (deal-killer) |
| **"Data room = chaos"** | Vous ne trouvez pas 10% de docs demandés | Full audit, cleansing, structure before sharing |

**Action :** Jour 2 de chaque flag : fix ou abandon du deal.

---

## PHASE 2 : Prospection

| Flag | Trigger | Action |
|------|---------|--------|
| **"0 réponses après 10 emails"** | Pas 1 meeting en 10 jours | Analyse : targeting wrong, message weak, ou just not right time |
| **"Buyer veut NDA 50 pages"** | Buyer lawyering up dès jour 1 | Red flag = very adversarial, probablement lowball offer incoming |
| **"Buyer demande tout avant teaser"** | "Envoyez financials avant meeting" | Refuser : protocol broken, buyer non-professionnel |
| **"Competing buyer ghosting"** | Buyer A en data room, puis silence 10 jours | Relance jour 8 : "Check in" call ou kill |

**Action :** Chaque flag = 2 emails de follow-up ou abandon buyer.

---

## PHASE 3 : Diligence

| Flag | Trigger | Action |
|------|---------|--------|
| **"Buyer question pattern = fishing"** | "Détail clients, API uptime, unit econ..." sans acheter | Pivot : demander sérieusement "vous êtes dans data room pourquoi ?" |
| **"Tech audit = butcher fest"** | Buyer tech dit "code audit coûte $50k, vous payez" | Soft walk-away : buyer setting up negotiation anchor (bail) |
| **"Reference calls = freezing"** | Un client clé refuse de parler à buyer | Big red flag : customer churn risk, prob. exit imminent |
| **"Buyer delays management call >2x"** | Schedules 3x, cancels 3x | Buyer non-serious ou dealing with internal blockers. Cool off. |
| **"Team rebellion"** | Employee says "I'll leave if acquired by them" | Talk to employee : why? Risk retention problem = -20% valuation |

**Action :** Flag 3+ : escalate ou kill buyer.

---

## PHASE 4 : Négociation LOI

| Flag | Trigger | Action |
|------|---------|--------|
| **"Earnout >50% of price"** | Buyer proposes 40% cash, 60% earnout | Counter max 30% earnout ou kill (too risky) |
| **"Earnout = impossible milestones"** | "Post-close, revenue must 3x or earnout zero" | Reject : you can't control it post-acquisition |
| **"Reps & warranties = unlimited"** | Buyer says "reps survive forever, no cap" | Negotiate : 18 months max, capped at 10-20% purchase price |
| **"Lockup >2 years"** | Non-compete / non-solicitation 3+ years | Negotiate down to 1 year, max 2 (you'll go insane) |
| **"Buyer suddenly lawyering up hard"** | LOI negotiation = 5+ rounds, getting adversarial | Shift to deal principal (skip lawyers), or kill |

**Action :** Flag detected = "This is my walk-away line" phone call, then walk.

---

## PHASE 5 : Due Diligence Complète

| Flag | Trigger | Action |
|------|---------|--------|
| **"Buyer discovering "material adverse change""** | "We found X problem, valuation drops 30%" | Question : was X disclosed? If yes, buyer negotiating. If no, you're liable. |
| **"Key person wants to leave"** | CTO says "I'm not staying post-close" | Retention bonus NOW or deal falls apart (earnout vests to no one) |
| **"Customer concentration >30%"** | Buyer discovers top 3 clients = 35% revenue | Expect 15-20% discount, unless customers signed retention letters |
| **"IP lawsuit discovery"** | Old patent dispute surfaces in DD | Resolve before close or expect indemnification insurance cost |
| **"SPA redline goes >50 versions"** | Legal fights over 20+ SPA terms | Hire deal principal or mediator (lawyers grind wheels) |

**Action :** Flags 1-2 = negotiate fix. Flag 3+ = kill or call advisors.

---

## PHASE 6 : Closing

| Flag | Trigger | Action |
|------|---------|--------|
| **"Closing condition not yet met"** | "Wire will happen, but we need 3 more weeks" | Demand clear deadline or walk (deal is dying) |
| **"Buyer wants renegotiate at close"** | Suddenly "valuation was based on assumption X, adjust price" | No. Sign SPA as-is or let them breach. Don't re-trade. |
| **"Key employee leaving day after close"** | CTO resigns on day 2 | Retention plan failure. Earnout at risk. Escalate to buyer immediately |
| **"Hidden contingency appears"** | "Board approval pending" or "Financing not final" | This should have been disclosed LOI. Breach of good faith. Call lawyers. |

**Action :** Flag = immediate call with buyer CEO + your advisor. No BS.

---

## Universal Red Flags (All Phases)

| Flag | Always means | Action |
|------|--------------|--------|
| **Buyer ghosting >1 week** | Non-serious or internal veto | Kill buyer, move on |
| **"Off the record, we lowball"** | Standard negotiation, but sets tone adversarial | Walk-away price anchored low |
| **Buyer asking for exclusivity before LOI** | Stalling tactic | Refuse : "Exclusivity = LOI signed" |
| **"We're not experienced in your sector"** | Higher integration risk = lower valuation | Expect 20-30% discount or walk |
| **"We buy 10 companies a year"** | Likely tuck-in shop, integration chaos risk | Smaller upside for team, earnout at risk |

## Go/No-Go Decision Matrix

| Phase | 0 flags | 1-2 flags | 3+ flags |
|-------|---------|-----------|----------|
| Prep | ✅ GO | ✅ GO (fix) | ❌ NO-GO |
| Prospection | ✅ GO | ✅ GO (qualify) | ❌ NO-GO |
| Diligence | ✅ GO | ⚠️ ESCALATE | ❌ NO-GO |
| LOI | ✅ GO | ⚠️ ESCALATE | ❌ NO-GO |
| DD Complète | ✅ GO | ⚠️ ESCALATE | ❌ NO-GO |
| Closing | ✅ GO | ⚠️ CALL LAWYERS | ❌ BREACH |

## Playbook : "When to Kill a Deal"

✅ Kill IMMEDIATELY if :
1. Buyer asks to lower price >20% post-LOI
2. Key person wants to leave & buyer won't retain
3. Buyer discovers customer concentration & offers no solution
4. Buyer breaks exclusivity to talk to competitors mid-process

✅ Kill if you see >3 flags and buyer won't address in 48 hours.

❌ Don't kill if :
- Buyer has just 1 red flag but addresses it quickly
- Terms are 90% there, just negotiating final %
