# DECISION CARD — Should I Sell NOW?

**Utilité** : Score 0-100 en 4 minutes. 70+ = explore soft shop. <50 = attendre 12m.

---

## SCORING MATRIX

| Category | Score 0 (Pas prêt) | Score 5 | Score 10 (Prêt) |
|----------|-------------------|--------|-----------------|
| **Growth** | <20% YoY | 30-50% YoY | >50% YoY |
| **Unit Economics** | Churn >10%/m | Churn 7-10% | Churn <7% |
| **Revenue Stage** | <$500k ARR | $500k-$2M ARR | >$2M ARR, predictable |
| **Founder Dependency** | Solo founder | 1-2 key hires | Full team, founder optional |
| **Product Maturity** | MVP, iterating | Core features locked | Multiple tiers, roadmap 2 yrs |
| **Cap Table Clarity** | Embrouilée, disputes | Clean but complex | Crystal clear, no disputes |
| **Market Opportunity** | <$100M TAM | $100M-$500M TAM | >$500M TAM, expanding |
| **Buyer Optionality** | 0-1 interested | 2-3 warm | 3+ strategic buyers ready |
| **Timing (external)** | Bear market / off-season | Neutral | Bull market / vertical heating |
| **Personal Motivation** | Unsure, tired | Curious | Clear exit goal + timeline |

---

## CALCUL

```
Total Score = Sum of 10 categories

INTERPRETATION:
0-30:   NOT READY. Focus on growth/team/product. Revisit in 12m.
30-50:  CONSIDER WARM SHOP (2-3 buyers, soft approach).
50-70:  READY FOR SOFT SHOP (parallel conversations, 2-3 buyers).
70-90:  READY FOR FORMAL PROCESS (LOI, data room).
90+:    MOVE FAST. Multiple buyers circling.
```

---

## EXAMPLE: Alliance Coiffure SaaS (Salon booking platform)

| Category | Score | Rationale |
|----------|-------|-----------|
| Growth | 8 | 65% YoY, seasonal peaks |
| Unit Economics | 7 | 6%/m churn, <15% CAC payback |
| Revenue | 6 | $1.2M ARR, recurring decent |
| Founder | 5 | Solo + 1 dev, operations scalable |
| Product | 9 | 3 tiers, integration roadmap clear |
| Cap Table | 10 | Founder 85%, 2 angels, clean |
| TAM | 8 | $500M+ (salons worldwide) |
| Buyers | 6 | 2-3 strategic (salon software giants) |
| Timing | 5 | Small market, Q2 upswing |
| Motivation | 4 | Curious, not urgent |
| **TOTAL** | **68** | → SOFT SHOP ready, 3 buyer conversations |
