## Usage Notes

### Who Gets This Teaser?
- ✅ Strategic buyers (after warm intro or CEO-to-CEO call)
- ✅ PE firms (via intro, never cold)
- ✅ M&A advisors (confidential, under NDA)

### What Happens After Teaser?
```
Buyer reads teaser (2-3 days)
  ↓
Buyer requests management presentation call (1h)
  ↓
You deliver deep-dive (traction, unit economics, tech)
  ↓
Buyer expresses interest (or passes)
  ↓
LOI negotiation (if serious)
```

### Key Messaging Lockdowns
- **Never say** : "We're desperate to sell" or "Multiple buyers interested" (lie if not true)
- **Always say** : "We're exploring strategic options" (truthful, neutral)
- **Avoid** : Specific customer names, proprietary margins, IP details (until NDA)
- **Lead with** : Market opportunity, growth proof, buyer synergies

### Personalization Per Buyer Type
- **For PE buyer** : Lead with unit economics + IRR story
- **For strategic buyer** : Lead with cross-sell TAM + integration ease
- **For international buyer** : Lead with market entry + local expertise
