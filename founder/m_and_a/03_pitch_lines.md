# 5 M&A Pitch Lines — Cold Outreach

## Pitch 1: Platform Play
**Buyer Type:** Large platform + vertical expansion
```
"[Your Company] brings [capability] into [buyer's ecosystem]. 
With [Y] customers and $[X]M ARR in [vertical], this acquisition 
accelerates your [platform goal] by [X] months and adds [specific value]."
```
**When to use:** Buyer is aggregating competitors or building ecosystems.

---

## Pitch 2: Technology Bolt-On
**Buyer Type:** Tech-forward acquirer wanting to build faster
```
"Rather than [Y months of build + hiring], you acquire [Your IP + team]. 
Our [specific tech advantage] solves [their known problem], and our 
[Z-person team] de-risks the execution."
```
**When to use:** They've tried to build this internally; you beat the timeline.

---

## Pitch 3: Customer Access
**Buyer Type:** Seller with installed base you can cross-sell into
```
"Our [Z] customers in [segment] are already your [ICP]. 
Embedding [your solution] into your product immediately adds 
$[X]M ARR upside + [Y]% NRR lift from deeper engagement."
```
**When to use:** Buyer has distribution; you have happy customers they want.

---

## Pitch 4: Cost Synergy (M&A+Efficiency)
**Buyer Type:** Private equity or cost-conscious strategic
```
"By consolidating [your infrastructure/team with buyer's equivalent], 
we unlock $[X]M in annual OpEx savings within [6] months, 
while maintaining [Z]% growth trajectory."
```
**When to use:** Buyer is optimizing margins post-acquisition.

---

## Pitch 5: Defensive (Anti-Competitor)
**Buyer Type:** Worried about competitor or new entrant
```
"[Competitor Name] is targeting [segment]. By acquiring [Your Company], 
you prevent them from using our [capability] against you and reclaim 
[Z]% market share."
```
**When to use:** Urgency exists; buyer fears losing ground.

---

## Template: Adapt by Buyer Profile

| Buyer Type | Lead With | Anchor | Timeline |
|-----------|-----------|--------|----------|
| Strategic Large | Platform vision + TAM | Revenue multiple (4–7x) | "Closes [Q]" |
| PE Fund | Cost synergies + EBITDA | Multiple (6–10x EBITDA) | "12m active" |
| Competitor | Competitive threat | Top-line + margin accretion | "Before they do" |
| Distribution Play | Customer value | Retention + NRR lift | "[Months] to payback" |
| Financial Buyer | Growth + multiples arbitrage | DCF fair value | "Market-tested" |

## Cold Email Structure (Using Pitch)

```
Subject: [Acquisition Opportunity] [Your Company] + [Buyer Company]

Hi [Name],

[Pitch Line 1, customized to them — 2 sentences]

[Social proof: "We serve [Customer Name] who also uses [Their Product]"]

[Ask: "Would [CFO/CEO] have 20min next week to explore fit?"]

[Your Lawyer's name + contact]
```

**Success rate:** 15–20% response rate on warm intros; 2–5% on cold.
