# CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION AGREEMENT
**Short Form (Seller-Friendly)**

---

## 1. PURPOSE

**Disclosing Party** (["Seller"]) will share certain confidential information ("**Confidential Information**") with **Receiving Party** (["Buyer"]) to explore a potential **business combination, investment, or strategic transaction**.

---

## 2. CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION DEFINED

**Confidential Information includes**:
- Financial data, projections, customer lists
- Product roadmap, technical documentation, source code
- Business strategy, pricing, unit economics
- Contracts, employee data, cap table

**Confidential Information does NOT include**:
- Information already public (not through breach)
- Information independently developed by Receiver
- Information received from third parties without confidentiality obligation
- General business insights not unique to [Seller]

---

## 3. PERMITTED USE

Receiver may use Confidential Information **only to**:
- Evaluate the transaction
- Conduct legal, financial, and technical diligence
- Share with internal team members and external advisors who need to know
- Make disclosures required by law or regulation

**Advisors** (lawyers, accountants, bankers) must sign confidentiality side-letters agreeing to the same terms.

---

## 4. CONFIDENTIALITY OBLIGATION

Receiver agrees to:

✓ **Keep Confidential Information secret**  
✓ **Use reasonable security** to protect it (password-protected folders, encrypted emails)  
✓ **Limit access** to employees and advisors with a "need to know"  
✓ **Not disclose publicly** or to competitors without Seller's written consent  

---

## 5. PERMITTED DISCLOSURES

Receiver may disclose Confidential Information **only if**:

- **Required by law or court order**: Receiver must give Seller [3 business days] notice to seek protective order
- **Board/investor notification**: May brief board members or existing investors if they sign confidentiality agreements
- **Regulatory filing**: May disclose to [SEC, FCA, etc.] as required, with notice to Seller
- **Lenders/financing partners**: May share with banks/PE sponsors, who also agree to confidentiality

---

## 6. RETURN OF INFORMATION

Within [10 business days] of **either party's request**, or upon **deal termination**, Receiver will:

- Return or certify destruction of all Confidential Information
- **Exception**: Receiver may retain [1 copy] for legal/compliance records only, subject to confidentiality

---

## 7. NO OBLIGATION TO DEAL

Receiver is **under no obligation to**:
- Proceed with the transaction
- Begin negotiations
- Make an offer
- Disclose its evaluation

Disclosing this Confidential Information does **not** create a binding obligation to acquire [Seller].

---

## 8. NO WARRANTY

Confidential Information is provided **AS-IS**. Seller makes **no representation** regarding:
- Accuracy, completeness, or relevance
- Absence of errors or omissions
- Ownership of information or IP

Receiver conducts its own due diligence and risk assessment.

---

## 9. REMEDIES FOR BREACH

**If Receiver breaches this agreement**:

- Seller is entitled to **injunctive relief** (court order to stop the breach)
- Seller may seek **liquidated damages of $[50,000 – $250,000]** (reasonable pre-estimate of harm)
- **Note**: Injunctive relief is in addition to, not instead of, damages

---

## 10. TERM & SURVIVAL

This agreement:
- **Begins** on the date signed
- **Expires** [3 years] from the date Confidential Information is last disclosed
- **Survives** any failure to complete the transaction

---

## 11. NO SHOP / EXCLUSIVITY (OPTIONAL)

*Only include if agreed during initial call*:

For [30–45 days], Receiver agrees **not to**:
- Approach Seller's employees, customers, or investors about competing services
- Share Confidential Information with direct competitors (e.g., [Competitor A], [Competitor B])

**Exception**: Receiver may discuss with existing shareholders/board members.

---

## 12. ASSIGNMENT & GOVERNED LAW

- **Assignment**: This agreement may not be assigned without Seller's consent (except to lenders/financing sources with notice)
- **Governing Law**: [English Law / French Law / [Seller's jurisdiction]]
- **Jurisdiction**: [Courts of [City/Jurisdiction]]

---

## 13. ENTIRE AGREEMENT & SEVERABILITY

This is the **complete agreement** regarding confidentiality. It replaces all prior discussions or emails on this topic.

If any provision is unenforceable, the remaining provisions survive.

```

1. You have read and understand the Confidential Information Agreement
2. You will maintain confidentiality as stated in that agreement
3. You will not use the information for any purpose other than to advise your client
4. You understand that unauthorized disclosure may result in legal action
```

## Negotiation Tips

| Common Pushback | Response |
|-----------------|----------|
| **"Can we share with our PE sponsor?"** | Yes, if they sign the side-letter. Provide sample language. |
| **"3 years is too long"** | Offer to reduce to 2 years if diligence starts within 30 days. |
| **"Can we talk to customers?"** | Not yet—add "Upon Seller's written request, Receiver may approach [X top customers] after LOI." |
| **"What if we're acquired?"** | Add clause: "Survives change of control; successor firm assumes obligations." |
| **"Liquidated damages seem high"** | Offer to reduce to $25K–$50K if exclusivity period is added. |

---

## When to Use This Template

✓ **Pre-LOI conversations** (early buyer calls)  
✓ **Non-exclusive discussions** (talking to multiple buyers)  
✓ **No earnest money commitment yet** (just exploring)  

→ **If you're past initial call + buyer is serious**, move to **LOI with fuller terms** (earnout structure, reps, indemnification).
