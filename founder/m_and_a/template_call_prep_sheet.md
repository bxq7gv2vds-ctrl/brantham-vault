# Call Prep Sheet — 10 Min Before Meeting

**Fill this out before EVERY buyer call. Keep it one page.**

---

## BUYER INTEL (2 min)

- **Company:** ________________
- **Buyer Contact:** ______________ (Title: __________)
- **Introducer:** ______________ (How they know buyer: _________)
- **Recent News:** [Search LinkedIn, Crunchbase, press release]
  - Latest acquisition: ___________
  - Latest fundraise: ___________
  - Recent CEO quote: ___________

---

## THEIR STRATEGIC PAIN (2 min)

**Why are THEY considering this acquisition?**

- Problem they have: ___________________
- Current gap in their product: ___________________
- Competitor threat to them: ___________________
- Time window before problem gets worse: ___________________

**Script:** "I know you're focused on [their pain]. We're designed to solve exactly that because [our advantage]."

---

## YOUR TOP 3 TALKING POINTS (2 min)

1. **Proof:** "[Customer Name] saved 40% on [metric]" OR "[Market Traction] shows demand"
2. **Speed:** "Build this yourself = 12-18 months + $3M. Acquire us = 90 days integration."
3. **Synergy:** "Fills your product gap AND expands TAM into [new segment]."

---

## OBJECTIONS & RESPONSES (2 min)

| Objection | Your Response |
|---|---|
| "We're not ready to acquire yet." | "Totally understand. When's your next M&A window? Let's stay in touch." |
| "Revenue is lower than we typically buy." | "Fair point. But [product fit / margin / growth] makes this an exception. Let's explore." |
| "We could build this ourselves." | "You could. But building = 18mo, $3M, and team distraction. Acquire us = 90 days live." |
| "[Competitor] offered us this feature." | "Great. But their integration is slower / more expensive / less complete. We're the best path." |
