# COLD EMAIL SEQUENCES — PHASE-BY-PHASE

**Purpose:** 3 email templates to move deal forward (hook → interest → traction)  
**How to use:** Copy + customize. Send as individuals (not mass), personalized.  
**Open rate goal:** 40%+ (cold B2B SaaS is 15-25%, warm is 40%+)  

---

## EMAIL 1: INITIAL OUTREACH (Hook + Context)

**Subject line options:**
- "Strategic opportunity: [Company] + [Buyer name]"
- "Quick thought on your [product / strategy]"
- "[Your name] @ [Company] — potential fit?"

**Best practice:** Mention specific thing about their company (shows research)

---

### EMAIL 1 TEMPLATE A (Warm intro / referral)

```
Hi [Name],

[Mutual connection] suggested I reach out—[he/she] thought you'd be 
interested in what we're building.

We run [Company], a [product description] for [customer type]. 
We've hit $3.5M ARR with [key metric: NRR, churn, etc].

Why I'm reaching out: You're expanding into [market segment / geography] 
and we have strong traction there. 

Happy to send over a quick overview if it's interesting. No pressure either way.

[Your name]
```

**Why it works:**
- Opens with credibility (mutual connection)
- Explains what you do in 1 sentence
- Gives reason they should care (your traction in their target market)
- Low pressure (no "let's talk" hard sell)

**Length:** 6 sentences = 1 minute to read

---

### EMAIL 1 TEMPLATE B (Cold outreach)

```
Hi [Name],

I came across [Buyer company] while researching [category / market] leaders. 
Your focus on [specific product feature / market] caught my eye.

I run [Company]—we help [customer type] [primary benefit]. We're at $3.5M ARR 
and growing 75% YoY. I noticed you've been [specific recent news: acquisition, 
product launch, hiring] and thought there might be synergy.

Would be happy to send a quick overview if you're interested. Otherwise, 
no worries—just wanted to be top of mind.

[Your name]
```

**Why it works:**
- Shows research (credibility)
- Specific reason for outreach (news, strategy)
- Explains your business briefly
- Soft ask (soft CTA)

**Length:** 5 sentences

---

### EMAIL 1 TEMPLATE C (Referral request if no warm intro)

```
Hi [Name],

I'm reaching out on the recommendation of [mutual contact]. [He/she] 
suggested you might be interested in what we're building.

[Company] helps [customer type] [benefit]. We're growing fast 
($3.5M ARR, 75% YoY) in [vertical / geography] where you're also focused.

If you're open to hearing more, I'd love to send a quick teaser. 
Otherwise, happy to stay in touch.

Thanks,
[Your name]
```

---

## EMAIL 2: POST-TEASER (Interest + Value prop)

**Timing:** Send 48 hours after teaser is opened (if tracked), or day 3 (if not tracked)

**Subject line options:**
- "More on [strategic opportunity from Email 1]"
- "[Your name] again – following up on teaser"
- "What if [bold hypothesis]?"

---

### EMAIL 2 TEMPLATE

```
Hi [Name],

Quick follow-up on the teaser I sent earlier. Happy to answer any questions, 
but wanted to highlight why I think [Buyer company] + [Your company] could be 
interesting:

1. Audience synergy: Your [product] reaches [market segment]. We have 
   [number] customers in [adjacent segment]—immediate cross-sell opportunity.

2. Standalone value: You can keep [Your company] as a standalone product 
   (we've built strong brand with our customers).

3. Multiple play: If you're looking at the [market] space, you could 
   do what [competitor] did: acquire vertical players, bundle + roll-up.

I'm not looking to go through a formal process. Just exploring if there's 
mutual interest in a conversation.

Thoughts?

[Your name]
```

**Why it works:**
- Moves from "let me introduce us" to "here's why it makes sense"
- Gives 3 specific reasons (one of them should resonate)
- Frames it as exploration, not a hard sale
- Shorter, more urgent tone

---

## EMAIL 3: MOVE TO CONVERSATION (LOI prep)

**Timing:** After 2+ email exchanges or initial call

**Subject line options:**
- "Next steps: preliminary term sheet?"
- "Let's move forward – timeline"
- "Quick call to align on structure"

---

### EMAIL 3 TEMPLATE

```
Hi [Name],

Sounds like there's mutual interest. Before we get lawyers involved, 
wanted to align on a few things on a call:

1. Valuation range: You mentioned [their ask]. Our sense is $[range]. 
   Happy to walk through the math.

2. Structure: Are you thinking all cash / earnout mix / earn-out?

3. Timeline: What does close look like for you? (Our sense is 60–90 days 
   if aligned on terms.)

Let's do a quick call [offer 2–3 times] to get aligned, then bring in 
our legal teams.

Looking forward,
[Your name]
```

**Why it works:**
- Moves from open-ended to specific
- Proposes valuation range (gives them anchor)
- Asks about deal structure (gets clarity on seriousness)
- Sets expectations on timeline
- Moves to phone call (higher conversion)

## VARIATIONS BY BUYER TYPE

### For Strategic Acquirer

**Email 2 emphasis:**
```
"Why this is interesting for [Buyer company]:
• Instantly adds [number] customers in [vertical]
• Product integrates seamlessly with your [product]
• Your sales team can immediately cross-sell [revenue opportunity]"
```

### For PE Buyer

**Email 2 emphasis:**
```
"Why this is interesting:
• Unit economics: $[CAC] → $[LTV], [LTV/CAC ratio]
• Multiple play: If we double revenue in 2 years, you're at [new multiple]
• Cash flow: EBITDA positive (rare for SaaS at our stage)"
```

### For Founder/Acqui-hire

**Email 2 emphasis:**
```
"Why this is interesting:
• Strong technical team ([number] engineers, ex-[companies])
• Proven ability to build [specific type] product at scale
• Culture fit: We value autonomy, impact, [your values]"
```

## RESPONSE SCENARIOS + REBUTTALS

### If they reply: "Not looking right now"

**Your response:**
```
Totally understand. We're not actively shopping either—just exploring 
if it's a fit. Want to stay in touch? I can send over updates when 
something moves.
```

**Action:** Add to email list for quarterly updates. Follow up in 6 months.

---

### If they reply: "Interested, but need to do DD first"

**Your response:**
```
Great! Before we spend time on DD, want to align on valuation range 
and deal structure? Better to make sure we're in the same ballpark 
before the legal work starts.

When are you free for a 20-min call?
```

**Action:** Move to call → LOI → DD

---

### If they say: "Send more info"

**Your response:**
```
Happy to! What's most important for you to understand? 
[Pick one: financials / product roadmap / customer references]

I can send a CIM if you've signed an NDA, or happy to jump on a 
call if you want to ask questions.
```

**Action:** Don't send "more info" via email (too easy to ignore). Move to call.

## EMAIL CADENCE

```
Day 1: Email 1 (initial outreach)
Day 3: Email 1 follow-up (if no response) — "Did you get this?"
Day 5: Email 2 (post-teaser, value prop)
Day 8: Email 2 follow-up — "Anything I can clarify?"
Day 12: Email 3 (move to conversation) OR check if they've opened teaser

If no response by Day 14: Move them to "Nurture" list (check in quarterly)
```

## TONE GUIDELINES

**DO:**
- ✅ Be specific (mention their product, news, market)
- ✅ Be brief (5-7 sentences max per email)
- ✅ Show respect for their time
- ✅ Give them an easy out (no pressure)
- ✅ Use [their name] not "Hi there" or "Hey team"

**DON'T:**
- ❌ Blast 100 people the same email (personalize)
- ❌ Use sales language ("incredible opportunity," "game-changer")
- ❌ Ask for too much too early ("let's have a 1-hour call")
- ❌ Include attachments (use links, send after they ask)
- ❌ Mention valuation in initial email (too early)

---

## SUCCESS RATE BENCHMARK

```
Email 1 → Open rate: 25-40% (if personalized)
Email 1 → Reply rate: 5-10% (target: meetings)
Email 2 → Reply rate: 10-15% (they saw teaser)
Email 3 → Move to call: 30-50% (if they engaged so far)

Overall: For every 20 cold emails, expect 3-5 meetings
For every 10 meetings, expect 1-2 LOIs
```

---

## FINAL CHECKLIST

Before you send Email 1:

- ✅ Spellcheck
- ✅ Use their actual name (not "Hi [Name]")
- ✅ Mention something specific about THEM (not just your company)
- ✅ Clear reason why now (news, market, growth)
- ✅ Easy next step (no 1-hour asks)
- ✅ Your phone number in signature
- ✅ No BCC (send individually)
