## 4. Preparation Roadmap

### 6 mois avant
- [ ] Financials normalisés (3 ans historique)
- [ ] Comparable companies research
- [ ] Legal audit (contrats critiques, IP, litigations)
- [ ] Product audit (tech debt, scalability)

### 3 mois avant
- [ ] Financial advisory hire (optionnel)
- [ ] Management team clarified roles
- [ ] Customer health dashboard built
- [ ] RFP / teaser document draft

### 1 mois avant
- [ ] Final financials audit
- [ ] Data room structure plan
- [ ] NDA template ready
- [ ] Buyer outreach list finalized

### Courant (active selling)
- [ ] Meetings scheduled
- [ ] Due diligence packages distributed
- [ ] Q&A tracker
- [ ] LOI negotiation

## 5. Key Risks & Mitigations

| Risque | Probabilité | Impact | Mitigation |
|--------|------------|--------|-----------|
| Valuation mismatch | H / M / L | Kills deal | Benchmark vs comps, hire advisor |
| Customer concentration | H / M / L | DD red flag | Expand top 10, reduce dependency |
| Tech debt revealed | H / M / L | Price discount | Audit + fix now or disclose |
| Team turnover | H / M / L | Earnout fails | Retention bonus, clear roadmap |
| Market downturn | H / M / L | Timing slips | Lock buyer early, build optionality |

## 6. Go/No-Go Criteria

**Proceeding if:**
- [ ] Valuation within X% of target
- [ ] Buyer culturally aligned
- [ ] Due diligence timeline acceptable (<6 months)
- [ ] Earnout terms reasonable (achievable KPI)
- [ ] Founder role post-close clear

**Pausing if:**
- [ ] Multiple red flags in DD
- [ ] Valuation <X (walk-away price)
- [ ] Buyer not serious (no signed LOI in Y months)
