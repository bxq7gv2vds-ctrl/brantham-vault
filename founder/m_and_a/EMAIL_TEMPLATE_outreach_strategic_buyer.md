# Email Template: Strategic Buyer Outreach

**Subject:** Quick conversation about [Product/Company Name]

---

Hi [Name],

I know you're focused on [buyer's strategic direction]. We built [Product Name] specifically to solve [problem] for [customer segment], and it's become the category leader in that niche.

**Quick numbers:**
- $[ARR]k ARR, [NRR]% NRR, [Margin]% gross margin
- [X] customers including [tier-1 customer names]
- [Y]% YoY growth

We've had interest from other [buyer type] firms, but given your [specific strategic fit], I thought it made sense to have a conversation first.

Would you have 20 minutes this week to chat?

[Name]

---

**Notes:**
- Customize "strategic fit" to buyer's quarterly strategy
- Drop 2–3 recognizable customer names (if possible)
- Imply urgency without being pushy ("interest from other firms")
- Ask for small commitment (20 min, not 1 hour)
