# 100-DAY INTEGRATION ROADMAP

**Utilité** : Prevent "integration theater." Deliver results in first 100 days post-close.

---

## CRITICAL PATH (Day 1-30)

### DAYS 1-3: TEAM & COMMUNICATION

**Day 1 (Closing Day)**
- [ ] Announce deal to employees (before market/news)
  - ✓ Script: "We've been acquired by [Buyer]. You're valued. Here's what doesn't change: [salary, health insurance, location]. Here's what does: [reporting line, new product direction]."
  - ✓ Q&A: Be ready for "will I be laid off?" "will my equity vest?" "should I leave?"
  - ✓ Email all-hands with: new org chart, new email domain (if changing), your CEO bio, first buyer meeting time
- [ ] Send closing update to top 5 customers
  - ✓ Script: "Great news! We've been acquired by [Buyer]. This accelerates [specific product feature]. Your contract terms don't change. We're same team, new resources."
- [ ] Notify investors (cap table holders)
- [ ] Notify key partners (integrations, co-marketing)
- [ ] Press release (if public)
- [ ] CEO 1-on-1s with founders + top 5 leaders (reassure)

**Days 2-3: Org Structure Clarity**
- [ ] Finalize org chart (who reports to whom? chain of command clear?)
- [ ] Schedule daily standup with new leadership
- [ ] Set up buyer's Slack/Teams channel + password managers
- [ ] Migrate email to buyer domain (if required)
- [ ] Move GitHub/critical accounts to buyer control
- [ ] Document access grants (who has AWS/prod/customer data access?)

---

### DAYS 4-10: PRODUCT & ROADMAP

**Product Alignment**
- [ ] Day 4: Product roadmap alignment meeting (your roadmap vs buyer's priorities)
  - Map features: what stays? what gets cut? what accelerates?
  - Set foundation: are you getting absorbed (killed), integrated (merged), or standing alone?
- [ ] Day 5-6: API integration planning (if products merge)
  - Scope: which endpoints are critical?
  - Timeline: can it go live in 90 days?
  - Owner: assign single point of contact
- [ ] Day 7-10: Engineering & Tech Debt Audit
  - Code review (scan for critical tech debt)
  - Security review (penetration test? code audit?)
  - Scalability review (can product handle 2x load?)
  - Data migration plan (if moving database)

**Decision Points**
- Is the product being:
  - [ ] **Absorbed** (killed, team reassigned) → focus on sunset + knowledge transfer
  - [ ] **Integrated** (merged into buyer product) → focus on API + migration
  - [ ] **Standalone** (acquired but independent) → focus on go-to-market

---

### DAYS 11-20: CUSTOMER & REVENUE

**Customer Retention**
- [ ] Days 11-15: Customer Success Roadshow
  - Call top 20 customers (reassurance)
  - Script: "Excited to announce acquisition. We're bringing [feature] you asked for. Your contract terms unchanged. New [support engineer] is your POC."
  - Ask: "Any concerns? Anything you need to know?"
- [ ] Days 16-20: Churn Risk Mitigation
  - Identify at-risk customers (reason: product changes? price? team departure?)
  - Assign executive sponsor (buyer VP) to at-risk accounts
  - Define success: <5% churn in next 90 days

**Revenue Clarity**
- [ ] Audit GAAP revenue recognition (did you record it correctly?)
- [ ] Confirm recurring revenue % (who's annual? who's monthly?)
- [ ] Identify upsell opportunities (what do your customers need?)
- [ ] Sales process integration (seller rep join buyer's team? or independent?)

---

### DAYS 21-30: OPERATIONS & FINANCE

**Finance & Compliance**
- [ ] Set up FP&A reporting (daily/weekly dashboard for buyer CFO)
  - What are the KPIs? ARR, churn, CAC, LTV?
  - Who owns reporting? What's the cadence?
- [ ] Audit accounting records (did you record correctly per GAAP?)
  - AR aging (how old are invoices?)
  - Accruals (have you deferred revenue correctly?)
- [ ] Tax & Legal Compliance
  - Have you filed all required documents?
  - Are there any regulatory filings (state, federal)?
  - Is IP assignment complete?

**Vendor & Contract Management**
- [ ] Renegotiate key vendor contracts (AWS, SaaS tools, etc.)
  - Can buyer get better rates?
  - Can you consolidate tools?
- [ ] Consolidate IT systems (email, Slack, GitHub, Jira, etc.)
- [ ] Cancel tools you don't need (reduce SaaS bloat)

---

## TRACTION PHASE (Days 31-60)

### DAYS 31-40: PRODUCT DELIVERY

**Launch Integration** (if applicable)
- [ ] Day 31: Public announcement of integrated product
- [ ] Days 32-40: Launch go-to-market
  - New landing page (integrated value prop)
  - Case study + webinar
  - Email nurture campaign
  - Sales training (buyer's reps learn your product)

**KPI Tracking**
- [ ] Dashboard setup: daily view of
  - ARR, net expansion, churn
  - Product adoption (DAU, feature usage)
  - Support tickets (response time, resolution)
  - NPS trend (customer satisfaction)

---

### DAYS 41-50: TEAM & CULTURE

**Retention Bonuses & Role Clarity**
- [ ] Finalize employment terms for key 5 people
  - Titles, compensation, responsibilities clear
  - Clawback conditions documented (what causes loss of earnout equity?)
- [ ] Team integration (how do your people interact with buyer team?)
  - Weekly syncs scheduled
  - Shared projects identified
- [ ] Cultural onboarding (learn buyer's processes, meetings, communication style)

**Talent Acquisition**
- [ ] Identify open roles (do you need to hire to hit roadmap?)
- [ ] Recruit: leverage buyer's brand + recruiting team

---

### DAYS 51-60: MOMENTUM & FOCUS

**Metrics & Decision**
- [ ] Are we on track for earnout targets?
- [ ] ARR growth: on pace?
- [ ] Churn: acceptable? if not, action plan?
- [ ] Team morale: high? any risk departures?
- [ ] Roadmap: achievable? or need to adjust?

**Pivot (if needed)**
- If metrics are off: rapid course correction
  - Change go-to-market strategy?
  - Pivot product focus?
  - Adjust pricing?

---

## SOLIDIFICATION PHASE (Days 61-100)

### DAYS 61-80: EXECUTION & SCALE

**Product Launch Results**
- [ ] Measure: How many new customer acquisitions?
- [ ] Measure: ARR lift from integration?
- [ ] Measure: Team productivity (sprints, delivery)?
- [ ] Adjust: Double down on what works, cut what doesn't

**Enterprise Expansion**
- [ ] Target 3-5 large accounts (buyer's relationships)
- [ ] Launch enterprise tier (if applicable)
- [ ] Build case studies (proof of integration value)

**Systems & Process**
- [ ] Standardize on buyer's processes (reporting, approvals, etc.)
- [ ] Full financial integration (accounts payable, receivable, GL)
- [ ] Data integration (CRM, financial system, analytics)

---

### DAYS 81-100: FOUNDATION FOR NEXT PHASE

**Knowledge Transfer**
- [ ] Document critical processes (customer onboarding, product roadmap, vendor relationships)
- [ ] Cross-train team (what happens if you leave?)
- [ ] Create playbooks (go-to-market, customer success, sales process)

**Earnout Preparation**
- [ ] Review earnout targets (on track? what needs to accelerate?)
- [ ] Document progress (for earnout claims later)
- [ ] Plan Q2 (if earnout extends 18-24m)

**Final Checklist**
- [ ] Customer retention >95% (low churn)
- [ ] Team morale high (no unexpected departures)
- [ ] Financial reporting clean (no surprises for buyer)
- [ ] Product roadmap clarity (next 6 months clear)
- [ ] Earnout metrics on pace (60%+ of first-year target hit)

---

## RAPID-RESPONSE PLAYBOOK (If something goes wrong)

### SCENARIO: Key Customer Threatens to Churn
- [ ] Day 1: Call customer + VP/SVP from buyer (show support)
- [ ] Day 2: Deliver [feature request / discount / exclusive access]
- [ ] Day 3: Schedule monthly check-in (executive sponsor)

### SCENARIO: Top Engineer Quits
- [ ] Day 1: Immediate 1-on-1 (understand why)
- [ ] Day 2: Counterfounder + buyer HR (retention package if possible)
- [ ] Day 3: Promote #2 engineer (reduce panic)
- [ ] Day 4: Plan backfill (internal or hire)

### SCENARIO: Earnout Targets Miss (e.g., revenue off 20%)
- [ ] Days 1-3: Root cause analysis (product? sales? market?)
- [ ] Days 4-7: Propose corrective actions (pivot? additional headcount? different go-to-market?)
- [ ] Day 8: Present plan to buyer (transparency = trust)
- [ ] Weeks 2-4: Execute plan + weekly progress updates

## STAFFING & OWNERSHIP

|----------|-------|------------------|-----------------|

| Sales | Your Sales Lead | Buyer's VP Sales | 2x/week |
| Engineering | Your CTO | Buyer's VP Eng | Daily standup + weekly 1-on-1 |
| Finance | Your CFO | Buyer's Controller | Weekly financial review |
| Customers | Your CS Lead | Buyer's Head of CS | 2x/week |
