# SDE Calculation — Seller's Discretionary Earnings

SDE = EBITDA + Add-backs (expenses du fondateur que buyer assume)

| Item | Amount | Justification |
|---|---|---|
| **EBITDA (Adjusted)** | $150k | From financial audit |
| **Add-backs:** | | |
| Founder salary (if excessive) | $80k | Buyer hires replacement @ $100k market |
| Founders' health insurance | $15k | Buyer covers via company |
| Founders' car / travel | $20k | Personal, not business |
| Professional fees (legal, accounting) | $12k | One-time M&A fees, recurring lower |
| Rent (if family property @ premium) | $0k | At market rate |
| Depreciation (add back) | $5k | Non-cash |
| **SDE Total** | **$282k** | |
| SDE Margin | 36% | Good for SaaS |

**Buyer formula:** EV = SDE × Multiple (5-8x = typical SaaS)
- Conservative: $282k × 5 = $1.41M
- Market: $282k × 6.5 = $1.83M
- Optimistic: $282k × 8 = $2.26M
