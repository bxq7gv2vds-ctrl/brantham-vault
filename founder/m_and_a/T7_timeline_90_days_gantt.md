## Critical Path (Things That Could Slip)

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation | Buffer |
|------|--------|-----------|--------|
| Lawyer too slow | +2 weeks | Hire by Jun 18 | +1w buffer |
| Buyer VDD slow | +2-3 weeks | Weekly check-ins | Start data room early |
| Price negotiation deadlock | Deal dies | Have 2 buyers in process | Parallel track |
| Earnout terms contentious | +1 week | Pre-negotiate structure | BATNA clear |
| Legal/compliance surprises | +1 week | Audit early | Early cap table clean |
