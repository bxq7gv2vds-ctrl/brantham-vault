## INFO SHARING LEVELS

**Level 0: Teaser Only**
- Company name: HIDDEN
- Revenue: Rounded ($2-3M range)
- Customers: None listed
- Growth: General ("40%+ YoY")

**Level 1: Basic Info**
- Company name: Revealed
- Revenue: Exact ($2.1M ARR)
- Customers: 3-5 sample names only
- Growth: Exact YoY % + MoM metrics

**Level 2: Financials** (Requires NDA)
- 3 years P&L
- CAC, LTV, churn, NRR
- Customer breakdown by ARR bucket
- Unit economics

**Level 3: Data Room** (Requires NDA + serious buyer)
- Full financial statements
- Customer list with contracts
- Codebase / tech audit prep
- Employee roster

**Level 4: Full DD** (LOI signed)
- Everything + IP docs, contracts, legal
- Customer calls allowed
- Code access granted

## NDA STATUS TRACKING

| Status | Action | Timing |
|---|---|---|
| 🟡 **Pending** | Buyer hasn't signed NDA yet | Follow up in 48 hours |
| 🟢 **Signed** | Can share Level 1-2 info | Proceed to next stage |
| 🔴 **Rejected** | Buyer refused NDA | Don't share sensitive info; consider disqualifying |
| ⏸️ **Stalled** | Buyer's lawyer reviewing (7+ days) | Flag as low-signal; move on |

---

## LEAK RISK ASSESSMENT

**Who might leak:**

| Person | Leak Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| Buyer's CEO | 🟢 Low | Wants acquisition confidential |
| Buyer's attorney | 🟢 Low | Bound by confidentiality duty |
| Buyer's board | 🟡 Medium | May discuss with other board members; flag if tight circle |
| Buyer's external advisor (banker, investor) | 🟠 High | May discuss with other buyer targets; ask buyer to limit |
| Your advisor / investor | 🔴 Critical | If they're also investor in other buyer, conflict of interest |

**Highest leak risk = your own advisors. Limit info sharing to 1 lawyer, 1 accountant.**

---

## LEAK RESPONSE PROTOCOL

**If you hear competitor/employee/customer heard about sale:**

1. **Assess damage:** What did they hear? Who told them?
2. **Trace source:** Which buyer's team likely leaked?
3. **Escalate:** Call buyer's CEO directly: "We heard rumors. This jeopardizes deal. How do we stop this?"
4. **Tighten control:** Stop sharing with that buyer's team. Reduce info scope.
5. **Contingency:** Accelerate with other buyers (run parallel process)

## RED FLAGS

- ❌ Buyer wants "exclusive" without signing LOI (reject; negotiates for weeks)
- ❌ Buyer's lawyer still reviewing NDA after 5 days (low signal; move on)
- ❌ Multiple buyers from same buyer group knowing about deal (leaked; escalate)
- ❌ Customer / employee asking why [Buyer] called them (someone leaked; tighten control)
