## Your Ask Price (Anchor)

### The "First Number" Strategy
```
Anchoring principle : First number in negotiation sets frame.
If you say €4.5M, midpoint negotiation ≈ €3.5M.
If buyer says €2.5M, midpoint negotiation ≈ €2M.

Your advantage : You move first (teaser deck).
```

## Structure Matters (As Much as Price)

€3.5M cash = €3.5M
€2M cash + €1.8M earnout = **potentially €2-2.5M real** (execution risk)

**When quoting €4.5M, specify structure** :
- **Option A (Preferred)** : 65% cash (€2.9M) + 35% earnout (€1.6M, 24m)
