# EMAIL OUTREACH TEMPLATE — INITIAL BUYER CONTACT

**Use this as template for initial outreach. Customize for each buyer; keep it short (1 paragraph).**

---

## VERSION 1: WARM INTRODUCTION (via advisor)

```
Subject: [Advisor Name] Suggested I Reach Out

Hi [Buyer CEO/BD Lead],

[Advisor Name] recently mentioned your strong interest in [acquiring / partnering with] companies in the [product category] space. We're building [Your Company], a [1-line description] that's now processing [key metric] from [number] customers including [customer logos].

We've reached ~$[X]M ARR, growing [Y]% YoY, with strong unit economics ([metric]). [Advisor] thought there might be strategic alignment worth exploring.

Would you be open to a 20-minute call to see if there's an opportunity here?

Best,
[Your Name]
[Your Company]
[Phone]
```

---

## VERSION 2: COLD OUTREACH (no warm intro)

```
Subject: Strategic Opportunity in [Category]

Hi [Buyer],

We're building [Your Company] — [1-sentence description] for the [category] market. We've grown to $[X]M ARR with [Y]% growth and strong retention.

We're exploring strategic partnerships and acquisitions to accelerate our mission [mission statement]. Given your leadership in [relevant area], I thought a conversation might be valuable.

Attached: 1-pager overview.

Open to a 20-minute call?

[Your Name]
[Your Company]
```

---

## VERSION 3: MULTI-BUYER OUTREACH (testing interest)

```
Subject: [Your Company] — Exploring Strategic Options

Hi [Buyer],

We're in the exciting position of exploring strategic options for [Your Company] — a [category] business now at $[X]M ARR, [Y]% growth, serving [key customers].

We're talking with a few strategic partners to understand the market's view of our value and explore partnerships that could accelerate our mission.

Given your work in [relevant area], thought a conversation might be worthwhile.

Would you be open to a quick call?

[Your Name]
[Your Company]
```

---

## KEY PRINCIPLES

- **Keep it short** (<150 words)
- **Lead with numbers** (ARR, growth, customers)
- **Name-drop if possible** (advisor warmly introduces you)
- **End with clear ask** (20-minute call)
- **Attach 1-pager** (give them something to review)

---

## WHAT HAPPENS AFTER YOU SEND

**Week 0:** Send email  
**Week 1:** If no response, send 1 follow-up ("Checking in...")  
**Week 2:** If still no response, move to next buyer (don't stalk)  
**Week 1–2:** If interested, they'll respond with calendar invite  

**Success rate:** Expect [10–15]% response rate from cold outreach; higher with warm intro ([50–70]%)
