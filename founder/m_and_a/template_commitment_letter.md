# COMMITMENT LETTER TEMPLATE
**Financing Proof (Bank / PE Sponsor)**

**Purpose**: Seller wants assurance buyer has cash before signing LOI. This letter proves it.

---

## COMMITMENT LETTER

**Date**: [DATE]  
**To**: [Seller Company Name] and its shareholders  
**From**: [Bank Name / PE Sponsor Name] ("Lender" / "Financing Source")

**Re**: Acquisition financing commitment for [Buyer Company] acquisition of [Seller]

---

## 1. COMMITMENT

We confirm that we are prepared to provide **senior debt financing** to [Buyer Company] for the acquisition of [Seller Company], structured as follows:

**Financing Structure:**
- **Senior term loan A (TLA)**: $[X]M
- **Senior term loan B (TLB)**: $[X]M
- **Revolving credit facility (RCF)**: $[X]M (undrawn, for working capital)
- **Total commitment**: $[X]M

**Use of Proceeds:**
- Acquisition of 100% equity of [Seller]: $[Y]M
- Refinance existing [Buyer] debt: $[Z]M
- Transaction fees & costs: $[A]M
- Working capital: $[B]M

**Conditions Precedent** (Lender's conditions to fund):

✓ [Buyer] successfully closes acquisition of [Seller]  
✓ Key reps & warranties from [Buyer] and [Seller] are true & accurate at closing  
✓ No material adverse change in [Buyer] or [Seller] business  
✓ [Buyer's] debt-to-EBITDA ratio not to exceed [X]x (post-financing)  
✓ Pro forma [Buyer] + [Seller] combined EBITDA support debt service  
✓ Senior management employment agreements signed (key persons: [list])  

---

## 2. PRICING & TERMS

**Interest Rate**: SOFR + [X]% margin (approximately [X]% all-in at current rates)  
**Term**: [5 / 7]-year amortization  
**Upfront fees**: [0.5 / 1.0]% of committed amount (paid at closing)  
**Legal fees**: Lender's legal fees (typically $[25K–50K], paid by borrower)

**Covenants** (post-closing):
- Debt service coverage ratio (DSCR) ≥ [1.25x]
- Leverage ratio (Debt/EBITDA) ≤ [3.5x] (declining to [3.0x] over time)
- Minimum cash balance: $[X]M

**Prepayment**: [No penalties / 1% penalty years 1–2, none after]

---

## 3. CONDITIONS TO COMMITMENT

This commitment is conditioned on:

1. **Acquisition close** by [DATE] (or extended by mutual written consent)
2. **Financials review**: Lender receives audited financials of [Seller] within [10] days of signing this letter
3. **Legal due diligence**: Lender's counsel completes diligence; no material issues discovered
4. **Insurance policies**: [Buyer] maintains [D&O, liability, etc.] with Lender named as loss payee
5. **No material adverse change** (MAC): No material adverse change in [Seller] or [Buyer] business between now and closing
6. **Regulatory approvals**: Any required regulatory approvals are obtained
7. **Borrowing request**: [Buyer] submits formal borrowing request 5 business days before closing

---

## 4. SYNDICATION

We reserve the right to syndicate this commitment to other lenders. Any syndication partners will be bound by terms no less favorable to [Buyer] than this commitment letter.

---

## 5. ASSIGNMENT

[Buyer] may not assign this commitment without our written consent. [Buyer] may not use this commitment for any other purpose without our consent.

---

## 6. TERM & TERMINATION

This commitment:
- **Becomes effective** upon [Buyer]'s execution below
- **Expires** at [5:00 PM Eastern Time] on [DATE] (the "Commitment Expiration Date")
- **Automatically terminates** if:
  - Acquisition is not consummated by [DATE], OR
  - [Buyer] and [Seller] mutually agree to terminate negotiations, OR
  - [Seller] terminates LOI with [Buyer]

After expiration, we have no further obligation to provide financing.

---

## 7. CONFIDENTIALITY

[Buyer] and [Seller] agree to maintain this commitment letter in confidence. [Buyer] may disclose it to:
- Board of directors / shareholders
- Legal advisors and accountants
- Existing lenders (for refinancing purposes)

[Seller] may disclose it to:
- Board / shareholders / counsel
- No one else without our written consent

---

## 8. REPRESENTATIONS & WARRANTIES

We represent that:
- We have full authority to make this commitment
- This is a binding commitment (subject to conditions outlined)
- We are a [bank licensed to lend / PE fund with $[X]B+ AUM]
- We have the financial capacity to fund $[X]M

---

## 9. NO OBLIGATION TO WAIVE CONDITIONS

We have **no obligation to waive** any condition listed in Section 3, even if requested by [Buyer] or [Seller]. Waiver requires our **explicit written consent**.

---

## 10. FEES

If [Buyer] fails to close the acquisition due to [Buyer's] breach or bad faith, [Buyer] pays:
- **Commitment fee**: [0.5–1.0]% of committed amount (non-refundable)
- **Lender legal fees**: $[25K–75K]

If closing fails due to reasons outside [Buyer's] control (e.g., regulatory denial, force majeure), no fee is due.

---

## 11. GOVERNING LAW & DISPUTE RESOLUTION

This commitment is governed by [English Law / New York Law].

Disputes are resolved via:
1. **Good faith negotiation** (14 days)
2. **Mediation** (if step 1 fails)
3. **Arbitration** (if step 2 fails)

No public litigation without mutual consent.

## KEY POINTS FOR SELLER

✓ **This protects you**: Buyer can't sign LOI without proof of cash  
✓ **Conditions matter**: Review Section 3 carefully — if they're too loose, financing might not materialize  
✓ **Ask for updates**: Lender should provide updated commitment letter 10 days before closing (reconfirm funds are still available)  
✓ **Check the lender**: Verify [Lender] is a real bank/fund (call their corporate finance team to confirm)  
✓ **Watch for "subject to"**: If letter says "commitment is subject to [additional conditions]", that's a red flag

---

## RED FLAGS 🚩

| Red Flag | Meaning |
|----------|---------|
| **"Subject to due diligence"** (vague) | Not a real commitment; can be withdrawn |
| **Lender asks for guarantee from seller** | Unusual; means they're unsure of buyer's creditworthiness |
| **Expiration date is very soon** | Pressure tactic; bad faith |
| **Conditions require seller approval** | Lender trying to involve seller; unorthodox |
| **Lender is unknown / not Googl-able** | Likely fraudulent; walk away |
