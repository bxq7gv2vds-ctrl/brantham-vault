# 100-Day Integration Roadmap — Post-Close

## Pre-Close (Days -30 to -1)

**Goal:** Build buyer confidence you'll execute; tie to earnout structure.

| Phase | Task | Owner | Deliverable |
|---|---|---|---|
| Week 1–2 | Joint integration planning sessions (1h/day, founder + buyer ops) | Founder | Integration playbook draft |
| Week 2–3 | Identify integration risks & dependencies | Buyer's ops + founder | Risk register (20–30 items) |
| Week 3–4 | Lock customer communications & retention plan | Founder | Customer announcement script + timeline |

---

## Day 1–15: Communication & Culture

**Objective:** Announce acquisition internally & externally; stabilize team & customer confidence.

| Day | Task | Owner | Audience | Proof of Completion |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Day 1** | Town hall: Announce acquisition; explain benefits | Founder + Buyer CEO | Employees | Deck + recording |
| **Day 2** | Customer announcement: Email + webinar | Founder + Customer Success | Top 50 customers | Email sent; registration numbers |
| **Day 3** | Partner/vendor notification | Founder + Ops | Key partners | Notification log |
| **Day 4** | Investor update: Why this was the right exit | Founder | Board members | Email sent |
| **Day 5–7** | 1-on-1s with key employees: Confirm role, earnout eligibility | Founder | Top 10 team members | Notes on each; confirmations signed |
| **Day 8–15** | Team integration kickoff: Meet buyer's team, product roadmap alignment | Buyer + Founder | Full team | Org chart + reporting structure finalized |

## Day 16–30: Product Integration Planning

**Objective:** Define how your product fits into buyer's ecosystem; build integration roadmap.

| Day | Task | Owner | Deliverable | Success Metric |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Day 16–20** | Product roadmap alignment session (daily standup) | Buyer Product + Founder | Unified 12-month roadmap | Feature priorities locked; engineering start date set |
| **Day 21–25** | Tech integration audit: APIs, SDKs, integrations needed | Buyer Tech + Founder | Integration architecture doc | Zero blockers identified for 100-day plan |
| **Day 26–30** | Engineering kickoff: First feature integrated | Buyer Eng + Founder Eng | Working prototype / PR merged | Demo working; no surprises |

---

## Day 31–60: Customer Success & Retention

**Objective:** Ensure zero major customer churn; lock in retention for earnout validation.

| Day | Task | Owner | Proof of Completion | Red Flag |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Day 31–45** | Customer success transitions | Buyer CSM + Founder | Top 10 customers have assigned buyer's CSM; monthly QBRs scheduled | Any customer says "I'm evaluating alternatives" |
| **Day 31–60** | Reference call program (buyer with your customers) | Buyer + Founder | 3–5 reference calls completed; positive feedback documented | Any customer expresses concern about acquisition |
| **Day 45–60** | New customer onboarding under buyer (first 2–3 customers) | Buyer onboarding + Founder | Onboarding completed; customers at target usage | Onboarding delayed >3 days; customer adoption slow |
| **Day 60** | Revenue retention checkpoint | Founder + Buyer Finance | ARR check-in: Is retention on track for earnout? | Churn is >2% vs. baseline |

---

## Day 61–100: Product & Operations

**Objective:** Ship first integrated features; prove product-market expansion; hit earnout targets.

| Day | Task | Owner | Deliverable | Earnout Impact |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Day 61–75** | Ship feature(s) combining buyer + acquired product | Buyer Eng + Founder | Shipped to production; live metrics dashboard | +$100k ARR from new cross-sell |
| **Day 76–85** | Pricing & packaging review (if combining products) | Buyer Product + Founder | New pricing model approved by leadership | Upsell motion defined |
| **Day 86–100** | Go-to-market for integrated product | Buyer Marketing + Founder | Campaign live; sales enablement complete | Lead pipeline filled for next quarter |
| **Day 100** | 100-day review & earnout confirmation | Buyer + Founder | Dashboard: ARR, churn, customer count, NPS | Earnout KPIs on track? (YES = payment trigger) |

## Risk Mitigation (If Things Go Wrong)

| Risk | Early Warning Sign | Contingency | Owner |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Customer churn accelerates** | Day 45 check-in shows >5% churn | Escalate to buyer CEO; fast-track product improvements | Founder |
| **Integration blocked by tech debt** | Engineering reports "architecture doesn't support feature" | Allocate sprint to refactor; slip timeline 2 weeks | Buyer Tech |
| **Key employee leaves** | Employee signals post-acquisition | Counter-offer or hire replacement immediately | Founder |
| **Earnout metrics not tracking** | Day 60 checkpoint: ARR flat, not growing | Emergency roadmap review; shift to retention focus | Founder + Buyer |

## Communication Cadence

**Weekly:**
- Product team standup (buyer + founder)
- Revenue/churn update (CFO + founder)

**Bi-weekly:**
- Integration steering committee (founder + buyer execs)

**Monthly:**
- Earnout dashboard review (founder + buyer finance)
- Customer reference calls (founder + buyer SVP)

**At close:**
- Integrated org chart published
- 100-day roadmap confirmed with buyer's board

---

## Success Criteria (Day 100)

✅ **Revenue:** ARR ≥ close amount ($2M) + growth target ($500k) = $2.5M  
✅ **Retention:** Top 10 customers retained; overall churn < 5%  
✅ **NPS:** Score ≥ 40 (validated by survey)  
✅ **Integration:** Feature(s) shipped combining both products  
✅ **Team:** 0 key employee departures; morale stable  
✅ **Earnout:** All Year 1 conditions met; payout triggered  

**If Day 100 misses:** Day 100–365 is your recovery window to hit earnout; adjust roadmap and communicate clearly to buyer.
