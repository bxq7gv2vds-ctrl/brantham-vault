# Founder Burnout Scorecard — Post-Acquisition Risk

**Why this matters:** Buyer pays premium if founder committed to 24m+ stay. Burnout risk = deal value loss.

---

Score each 0-3. Higher = higher burnout risk.

| Factor | Score (0-3) | Your Status |

## INTERPRETATION

| Score | Risk Level | What Buyer Will Do |
|---|---|---|
| **0-5** | 🟢 Low | Pay full valuation, minimal retention hold-back |
| **6-10** | 🟡 Moderate | Require 15-25% earnout, 12-24m retention bonus |
| **11-15** | 🟠 High | Demand 30% earnout + retention hold-back, monthly check-ins |
| **16-21** | 🔴 Critical | 50% earnout, founder must accept role changes, or walk away |

**Your Risk Level:** _____

## MITIGATION STRATEGIES

### If Burnout Risk > 10:

**Pre-Close (Do NOW):**
- [ ] Hire COO/VP Ops to absorb founder admin burden
- [ ] Document all decision-making (reduce founder reliance)
- [ ] Train #2 on key customer relationships
- [ ] Founder takes 2-week vacation (prove company works without you)

**In LOI (Negotiate):**
- [ ] Retention bonus is **cash**, not escrow (fund from deal proceeds)
- [ ] Earnout is tied to revenue, not subjective KPIs
- [ ] Clear role definition post-close (avoid scope creep)
- [ ] Monthly call with buyer instead of daily integration demands
- [ ] 6-month option to buy out of earnout (if you want to exit)

**Post-Close (Protect Yourself):**
- [ ] Write down role expectations and KPIs in first 30 days
- [ ] Schedule monthly "pulse check" with buyer (proactive burnout detection)
- [ ] Build an exit plan for month 20 (final 4 months are negotiation prep)

## EXAMPLE: FOUNDER WITH 15/21 SCORE

**Signals:** Works 65h/week, all revenue from company, no COO, unimpressed with new owner's PE playbook.

**Buyer's View:** "This founder will burn out or leave. We need safeguards."

**Negotiation Impact:**
- Buyer: "We'll offer $8M upfront + $2M earnout (24m, revenue-based)"
- You: "I need $7M + $3M earnout + $500K retention bonus (cash at close) + clear COO hire. Deal only works if I'm not doing ops."
- Final: $7.5M + $2.5M earnout + $500K bonus + buyer funds COO hire within 60 days

## NEXT: BURNOUT PREVENTION PLAYBOOK

If your score > 10, implement **before** telling buyer you're for sale:

1. **Hire Deputy** (COO or VP Ops) — Takes 4-8 weeks recruiting + ramp
2. **Document Decisions** — 30-day playbook of decision trees (reduces founder bottleneck)
3. **Customer Relationship Map** — Who knows which customers? Train backups.
4. **Prove Independence** — Take 1-week vacation, return to email < 10 new fires
5. **Define "Success"** — What does good look like years 1-2 post-close? Write it down.
