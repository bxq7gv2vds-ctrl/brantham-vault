# Timeline Pressure Detector — Is Their Urgency Real or Fake?

**Assess whether buyer's timeline is genuine or negotiation tactic.**

---

## RED SIGNALS (Fake Urgency)

- ❌ "We need to close by [date] but no real reason given"
- ❌ "CEO is leaving in 2 months; we'll discuss post-departure" (means low priority)
- ❌ "Board gives us only Q3 budget" (they could ask for more if serious)
- ❌ "We do all acquisitions in 8 weeks" (rigid process = low flexibility)
- ❌ "Other buyer waiting in wings" (you'll find out it's a bluff)

## GREEN SIGNALS (Real Urgency)

- ✅ "Major customer demanding [feature] by [date]; we're getting churn"
- ✅ "Competitor launching [product] Q4; we need to defend"
- ✅ "We close acquisitions in 6-8 weeks; our last 5 deals [dates] all hit timeline"
- ✅ "Board already approved budget; we just need the right company"
- ✅ "Our funding round closes [date]; acquisition budget available post-close"

---

## TACTICAL RESPONSE

**If timeline is FAKE:**
Don't rush. Counter with "Let's do this right, not fast."

**If timeline is REAL:**
Move fast. You control timing = higher leverage.

---
