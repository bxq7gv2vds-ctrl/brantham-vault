# MUTUAL NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT

**THIS AGREEMENT** is entered into as of [Date] ("Effective Date") between:

| Party | Role |
|-------|------|
| [Buyer Company Inc.] | "Buyer" |
| [Your Company Inc.] | "Seller" |

(Collectively, the "Parties" or individually, a "Party")

---

## 1. CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

### (a) Definition
**"Confidential Information"** means all non-public, proprietary information disclosed by one Party (the "Discloser") to the other (the "Recipient"), whether oral, written, electronic, or visual, including:

- Business plans, strategies, financial data, revenue, margins, projections
- Customer names, contracts, pricing, usage, churn data
- Product roadmaps, technical architecture, source code, algorithms
- Employee names, salaries, options, organizational structure
- Marketing plans, vendor relationships, supplier contracts
- M&A discussions, valuation analyses, advisors involved

### (b) Exclusions from Confidential Information
Confidential Information **does NOT include** information that:

1. Is or becomes publicly available through no breach of this Agreement
2. Is rightfully received from a third party without confidentiality obligations
3. Is independently developed without use of Discloser's Confidential Information
4. Is required to be disclosed by law or court order (with notice to Discloser)
5. Is disclosure by Recipient's advisors under equivalent confidentiality (counsel, accountants, banks)

---

## 2. PERMITTED USE

**Recipient agrees to use Confidential Information solely for:**
- Evaluating a potential business combination, investment, or partnership
- Assessing strategic, financial, legal, and operational compatibility
- Due diligence investigation

**Permitted disclosure:**
- To employees, directors, and advisors with need to know (counsel, accountants, banks, insurance brokers)
- All recipients must be bound by written confidentiality obligations
- Recipient remains liable for breaches by its advisors

---

## 3. CONFIDENTIALITY OBLIGATIONS

**Recipient shall:**

- Store Confidential Information securely (access restricted to authorized personnel)
- Use reasonable security measures (encryption, access controls, secure servers)
- Not disclose to any third party without Discloser's prior written consent
- Not combine Confidential Information with public information to infer non-public details
- Promptly notify Discloser if unauthorized disclosure occurs

**Return/Destruction:**
- Within [30] days of termination or upon Discloser's request, Recipient shall:
  - Return all physical copies, documents, materials to Discloser, OR
  - Certify in writing that all materials have been destroyed (except archival legal copy)
  - Permanently delete all electronic copies

---

## 4. PERMITTED DISCLOSURES (NO WAIVER)

If Recipient is required by law, court order, or regulatory authority to disclose Confidential Information:

1. **Notice:** Recipient shall provide Discloser written notice of the requirement as soon as legally permissible
2. **Limitation:** Recipient shall disclose only the minimum information legally required
3. **Cooperation:** Recipient shall cooperate with Discloser's efforts to obtain protective order
4. **Privilege:** If disclosure is to counsel/auditors under professional privilege, no waiver occurs

---

## 5. NO LICENSE OR OBLIGATION

**This Agreement grants NO RIGHTS** to Confidential Information. Specifically:

- No license, option, or right to purchase
- No obligation to proceed with any transaction
- No obligation to negotiate in good faith (except as to confidentiality terms)
- Either Party may terminate discussions at any time without cause

---

## 6. NO WARRANTY

Discloser makes **NO WARRANTY** regarding:
- Accuracy, completeness, or reliability of Confidential Information
- Non-infringement of third-party intellectual property
- Fitness for any particular purpose

**Recipient relies on its own due diligence** and advisors' analysis.

---

## 7. PUBLICITY & ANNOUNCEMENTS

**Without Discloser's prior written consent**, neither Party shall issue press releases, make public announcements, or disclose the existence of discussions (except:)
- To employees with need to know
- To advisors under NDA
- If required by law or stock exchange rules

---

## 8. NO SOLICITATION

**During discussions and for [12 months] after termination:**

- Neither Party shall solicit or hire the other Party's employees without consent
- Neither Party shall solicit customers or business partners for competing purposes

---

## 9. TERM & TERMINATION

**Effective Date:** [Date]

**Term:** [2 years] from Effective Date

**Automatic Termination:**
- If either Party terminates discussions in writing
- Upon completion of a business transaction between the Parties

**Survival:** Confidentiality obligations survive termination for [2 years], except:
- Obligations for legally required disclosures (indefinite)
- Return/destruction obligation (upon termination)

---

## 10. REMEDIES

**Breach of this Agreement may cause irreparable harm.**

- Discloser is entitled to seek injunctive relief without posting bond
- Right to injunction is in addition to (not instead of) damages
- If litigation arises, prevailing party recovers reasonable attorneys' fees & costs

---

## 11. COMPELLED DISCLOSURE

**If Recipient is compelled** by subpoena, court order, or regulatory demand to disclose:

1. Recipient shall immediately notify Discloser in writing
2. Recipient shall cooperate with Discloser's request for protective order
3. Recipient shall disclose only the minimum legally required
4. Recipient shall request Discloser's advice on limiting disclosure

---

## 12. MISCELLANEOUS

- **Governing Law:** [Delaware/New York State law]
- **Entire Agreement:** This NDA constitutes the entire confidentiality agreement; supersedes prior NDAs, LOIs, or verbal assurances
- **Amendment:** May only be amended in writing, signed by both Parties
- **No Waiver:** Failure to enforce any provision ≠ waiver of that provision or future enforcement
- **Severability:** If one clause is invalid, remainder survives
- **Assignment:** Neither Party may assign this NDA without consent
- **Notices:** Sent via email/courier to addresses below; effective upon receipt
- **Interpretation:** Section headings are for convenience only

---

## 13. COUNTERPARTS & ELECTRONIC SIGNATURES

- This NDA may be executed in counterparts (each considered an original)
- Electronic signatures (DocuSign, Adobe Sign, email PDF) are binding and enforceable
