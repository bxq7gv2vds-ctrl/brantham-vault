# LETTER OF INTENT

**This Letter of Intent ("LOI") is subject to the execution of definitive agreements and is non-binding except as noted below.**

---

## 1. PARTIES & TRANSACTION

| Item | Value |
|------|-------|
| **Buyer** | [Buyer Company Inc., a [State] corporation] |
| **Seller** | [Your Company Inc., a [State] corporation] |
| **Transaction** | Stock purchase; all equity acquired, no carve-out |
| **Closing Date** | [D+45 to 90 days from LOI execution] |

---

## 2. PURCHASE PRICE & TERMS

**Enterprise Value:** $[X]M

| Component | Amount | Notes |
|-----------|--------|-------|
| **Base Cash** | $[A]M | Paid at close |
| **Earnout** | $[B]M | [Revenue/EBITDA target for 12/24/36 months] |
| **Seller Financing** | $[C]M (optional) | [Interest rate, terms] |
| **Stock Consideration** | [N]% (optional) | [Details] |

**Closing Cash Adjustment:**
- Target working capital: $[X]K
- Cash at close reduced $[Y] per $[Z] of variance
- Inventory, receivables true-up post-close

---

## 3. REPRESENTATIONS & WARRANTIES (Seller)

**Seller represents & warrants:**
- Organization & authority to execute transaction
- All cap table & equity fully disclosed (no side letters, options, conversions pending)
- Intellectual property: all IP created by employees assigned to company; no third-party claims
- Compliance: no pending/threatened litigation; all tax, labor, regulatory compliance current
- Financial statements: last 3 years accurate, consistent accounting; no material undisclosed liabilities
- Contracts: all material customer/vendor contracts disclosed; no termination clauses triggered by change of control (except listed exceptions)
- Data: no security breaches; GDPR/CCPA compliance; customer data properly handled
- Employees: full disclosure of equity grants; no retention agreements outside [list]

**Survival:** 18 months post-close (except tax: 3 years; IP/IP indemnification: indefinite)

---

## 4. EARNOUT TERMS (if applicable)

**Earnout Calculation:** 
- [Revenue-based: $1 per $1 of revenue above $[baseline]M]
- [EBITDA-based: $1 per $0.50 of EBITDA above $[baseline]M]
- [Milestone-based: $[amount] for each of [3 milestones]]

**Measurement:** Audited by [Buyer's auditor / mutually agreed CPA]

**Payment:** [Quarterly/annually] within [30] days of measurement

**True-up:** If earnout target missed, seller has right to audit within [60] days

**Clawback:** Earnout subject to clawback if [seller breach of reps / key person leaves within 6 months / [other trigger]]

---

## 5. CLOSING CONDITIONS

**Conditions Precedent to Close:**
- Execution of definitive agreements (SPA, employment agreements, non-solicit)
- Third-party approvals/consents (material customer contracts per Exhibit A)
- No material adverse change (MAC clause below)
- Satisfactory completion of diligence
- [Financing contingency, if any]

**Material Adverse Change (MAC):**
- Loss of [X]% of ARR / [Y]% of customers
- Departure of [key person]: [CEO], [CTO], [VP Sales]
- [Regulatory action / litigation]
- Excludes: industry/market conditions, force majeure, COVID-like events

---

## 6. DILIGENCE & DISCLOSURE

**Data Room:** Seller provides access to:
- Financial statements (3 years audited/unaudited)
- Customer contracts, EBITDA waterfalls
- Cap table, equity docs, option pool
- HR: employment agreements, option plans, equity grants
- Legal: lawsuits, settlements, insurance
- Compliance: SOC2, GDPR, CCPA docs
- Technical: infrastructure, security, IP audit

**Diligence Window:** [30] days from LOI execution

**Exclusivity:** Seller agrees not to solicit/engage other buyers during LOI term ([60] days); permitted to respond to unsolicited inbound

---

## 7. EMPLOYMENT TERMS

**Seller/CEO:** 
- Offer: [Position] at [salary], [equity], [benefits]
- Commitment: [24-month non-compete / 12-month transition / other]
- Earnout tied to continued employment: [Yes/No]

**Key employees:** 
- [CEO, CTO, VP Sales] offered retention: [bonus/equity/cash]
- Non-compete: [12 months]

---

## 8. CONFIDENTIALITY & EXCLUSIVITY

**Confidentiality:** Information shared remains confidential; permitted use = evaluation only. Survives 2 years post-LOI termination.

**Exclusivity:** Seller agrees to [strict/modified] exclusivity — cannot shop to other buyers / can respond to unsolicited inbound. Period: through [Date or 60 days].

---

## 9. BINDING vs. NON-BINDING PROVISIONS

**BINDING (Enforceable):**
- Confidentiality
- Exclusivity
- Governing law (Delaware/NY)
- Non-solicitation of employees/customers during LOI

**NON-BINDING (Subject to SPA):**
- Price and terms (subject to diligence)
- Representations and warranties
- Indemnification & escrow
- Closing conditions

---

## 10. TERMINATION

**Automatic Termination:**
- If definitive agreements not executed by [Date + 60 days]
- If either party materially breaches LOI and doesn't cure in [5] business days

**Either Party May Terminate:**
- If buyer finds MAC
- By mutual written consent

**Effect:** All obligations cease except confidentiality, which survives [2 years]

---

## 11. MISCELLANEOUS

- **Governing Law:** [Delaware/New York]
- **Entire Agreement:** This LOI + Exhibits (seller list, cap table, financials)
- **Amendment:** Written consent of both parties only
- **Waiver:** No waiver of one provision = waiver of others
- **Severability:** If one clause invalid, rest survives
