# Closing Mechanics Checklist — 48 Hours Before Signature

Use this to verify nothing is missed in the final 48 hours.

---

## **Documents Ready to Sign (48h before)**

- [ ] **Purchase Agreement** (final version, all parties reviewed)
- [ ] **Disclosure Schedules** (exceptions to reps, all indexed)
- [ ] **Certificate of Representation & Warranty** (you sign this at close)
- [ ] **Equity Rollover Agreement** (if you're rolling equity)
- [ ] **Earnout Agreement** (measurement mechanics, dispute resolution)
- [ ] **Transition Services Agreement** (if needed, e.g., payroll, IT)
- [ ] **Employment Agreement** (post-close role, salary, benefits)
- [ ] **Non-Compete / Non-Solicit** (sign if required)
- [ ] **Officer Certificates** (verify your authority to sign)

---

## **Financial Mechanics**

- [ ] **Wire instructions** from buyer verified by buyer's counsel (ask 3 times)
  - Amount: $[X]
  - Bank name: [Bank]
  - Account: [XXXXXXX]
  - Routing: [XXXXXX]
  - SWIFT: [if international]
- [ ] **Your bank account verified** (where you'll receive wire)
- [ ] **Expected closing time** confirmed (usually 9 AM ET)
- [ ] **Tax ID/EIN** provided to buyer (for Form 8594 allocation)
- [ ] **W-9 form** signed (for Form 1099 reporting if earnout)

---

## **Capitalization Table & Share Transfers**

- [ ] **Final cap table prepared** (who owns what % post-close?)
- [ ] **Share certificates issued** (if you have stock certificates)
- [ ] **Preferred stock conversion** (converting preferred to common at close)
- [ ] **Option exercise decisions made** (any options you're exercising at close?)
- [ ] **Stock ledger updated** (reflects final ownership)
- [ ] **Escrow arrangement confirmed** (if any shares held in escrow)

---

## **Buyer's Board Approvals**

- [ ] **Buyer's board approval** (completed and documented)
- [ ] **Buyer's financing approval** (confirmed available funds)
- [ ] **Buyer's legal team signoff** (no last-minute surprises)
- [ ] **Buyer's finance team ready** (they're processing wire at close)

---

## **Your Approvals**

- [ ] **Your board approval** (if board exists, they've voted)
- [ ] **Shareholder approval** (if required by bylaws)
- [ ] **Co-founder signoffs** (if multiple founders, all signed purchase agreement)
- [ ] **Investor signoff** (if preferred investors have rights, they approve)

## **Day-Before Verification**

- [ ] **Call buyer's finance team** (confirm: wire amount, timing, account details)
- [ ] **Call your banker** (they'll expect the wire; verify details align)
- [ ] **Review PA one final time** (read through for surprises)
- [ ] **Review disclosure schedules** (what exceptions did you disclose?)
- [ ] **Verify all signatures collected** (PA has all required signatures?)
- [ ] **Confirm earnout measurement** (both sides agree on metrics?)
- [ ] **Get final signing order** (who signs first, when, where?)

---

## **Day-Of Closing (Closing Day)**

### **Morning**
- [ ] **All parties logged into signing platform** (DocuSign, Ironclad, etc.)
- [ ] **Counterparties in holding pattern** (ready to sign, waiting for signal)
- [ ] **Wire transfer PENDING** (buyer's bank has initiated, clearing in progress)

### **Signing**
- [ ] **Purchase Agreement signed** (seller + buyer signatures)
- [ ] **All exhibits/schedules signed** (disclosure schedules, earnout agreement, etc.)
- [ ] **Certificates of Rep signed** (you affirm reps/warranties are true)
- [ ] **Employment agreement signed** (post-close role confirmed)
- [ ] **All documents initialed** (where required by PA)

### **Post-Signing (Immediate)**
- [ ] **Closing statement reviewed** (accounting of proceeds, deductions)
- [ ] **Tax withholding calculated** (state withholding, federal, if applicable)
- [ ] **Wire verified as received** (your banker confirms wire hit your account)
- [ ] **Funds swept to correct accounts** (personal account, investor distributions, etc.)

---

## **Post-Closing (Next Business Day)**

- [ ] **Earnout agreement with buyer signed** (confirm measurement, reporting)
- [ ] **First earnout reporting** scheduled (when is 1st measurement date?)
- [ ] **Share certificates delivered** (if issuing new buyer shares)
- [ ] **Cap table updated** (new ownership post-close documented)
- [ ] **Notify investors** (they've received distributions)
- [ ] **File Form 8594** with IRS (asset/goodwill allocation)
- [ ] **Notify accountant** (they'll file amended return if needed)
- [ ] **Update cap table record** (for company records)

---

## **Critical: Triple-Check Wire Details**

**Scam alert:** Wire fraud is common in M&A. Verify bank details via phone, not email:

```
DO NOT trust:
- Email from buyer's counsel (could be spoofed)
- Wire instructions sent via email alone

DO THIS:
1. Get wire details from purchase agreement (PA has them)
2. Call buyer's finance person DIRECTLY (from company directory, not email signature)
3. Verify: Bank name, account number, routing number, amount
4. Your bank calls buyer's bank to verify (inter-bank verification)
5. Wire only after 3-party confirmation
```

---

## **If Closing Is Delayed**

If buyer requests 24–48 hour delay:

- [ ] **Ask why** (financing issue? Legal issue? Business concern?)
- [ ] **Confirm funds are still committed** (not falling apart?)
- [ ] **Adjust closing timeline** (push earnout measurement date out by same amount)
- [ ] **Get written commitment** (updated closing date in writing)
- [ ] **Escalate if needed** (if delay is >48 hours, something's wrong)

---

## **If Closing Falls Apart**

If buyer backs out in final 48 hours:

- [ ] **Immediately notify your lawyer** (potential breach of PA)
- [ ] **Document everything** (emails, calls, reasons given)
- [ ] **Invoke "specific performance"** (sue for deal completion, or damages)
- [ ] **Notify investors** (they'll want to know if deal is failing)
- [ ] **Restart buyer search** (if deal fails, go back to market)

---

## **Post-Close Communication**

Send to buyer within 1 hour of close:

```
Subject: [Company Name] Acquisition — Closing Complete

Hi [Buyer CFO],

Confirming we've closed the acquisition of [Company Name]. 
All documents have been executed and funds received.

Earnout mechanics:
- Measurement period: [Start date] to [End date]
- Metrics: [Revenue, churn, customer retention, etc.]
- First reporting: [Date]
- Contact for questions: [Your contact info]

Looking forward to making this successful.

[Your name]
```

## **Final Mindset**

✅ **Trust but verify everything** — Even at final hour
✅ **Talk to humans** — Email can be spoofed
✅ **Have your lawyer on standby** — For last-minute questions
✅ **Know your walk-away number** — Don't get pressured into bad deal
✅ **Celebrate after wire clears** — Not before
