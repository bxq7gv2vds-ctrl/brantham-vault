# BATNA Card — Your Walk-Away Plan

**Fill this out BEFORE negotiations start.**

```
BATNA = My Plan B if deal falls apart

MY WALK-AWAY NUMBER:     $__M (minimum accept)
MY SWEET SPOT:           $__M (target ask)
MY OPTIMISTIC:           $__M (best case)

TIMELINE:
Walk away if offer not received by:  [Date] (e.g., 90 days)

ALTERNATIVES IF NO DEAL:
1. Keep running independently (continue growth)
   - Profit potential: $X/yr
   - Risk: competition, financing gaps
   
2. Join acquirer as employee/advisor
   - Role: VP Product / CTO
   - Salary: $200k + equity
   - 4-year vest

3. Pivot to new business (if exit desired)
   - Timeline: 6 months planning, launch Y2
   
4. Seek different buyer
   - Secondary option: Competitor Y
   - Tertiary: PE firm Z
   - Timeline: 3-6 months more

WALK-AWAY DECISION RULE:
IF (Offer < $[X]M) AND (Non-compete too long) AND (No earnout upside)
→ WALK (deploy BATNA #1: keep running)

RESERVATION PRICE (minimum accept):
- Cash: $[X]M minimum
- + Earnout: Up to $[Y]M over 2 years
- Non-compete: Max 2 years (not 3+)
- Equity rollover: ≤20% acceptable (if buyer stable)

MY POWER POSITION:
□ Multiple offers (leverage)
□ Growing without exit (show confidence)
□ Key person risk (they need you)
□ Profitable company (not desperate)

MY WEAK POSITION (if yes, weaker BATNA):
□ Single buyer interested
□ Burning cash (need exit soon)
□ Key customer dependent
□ Need buyer's technology
```

**Owner:** [Your name]
**Date prepared:** [Date]
**Review before each negotiation call**
