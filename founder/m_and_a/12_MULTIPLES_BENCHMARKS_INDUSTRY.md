# Multiples d'Industrie & Benchmarks (SaaS, Services Professionnels)

**Utilisé pour :** Valoriser Alliance Coiffure, comparer avec transactions similaires, fixer ancrage initial.

---

## SaaS B2B Multiples (ARR-based)

| Taille | ARR | Revenue Multiple | EBITDA Multiple | Median Price |
|--------|-----|-----------------|-----------------|--------------|
| **Micro** | $0.5M–$1M | 4–6x | N/A (pre-profit) | $2–3M |
| **Small** | $1M–$5M | 5–8x | 6–10x (if profitable) | $5–15M |
| **Mid** | $5M–$20M | 6–10x | 8–15x | $30–100M |
| **Large** | $20M–$50M | 7–12x | 10–20x | $150–600M |
| **Mega** | $50M+ | 8–15x | 12–25x | $600M+ |

**Hairline rules :**
- Add +0.5–1x for >80% NRR (net revenue retention)
- Subtract 0.5–1x for >10% YoY churn
- Subtract 0.5–1.5x for customer concentration (top 5 = >50% revenue)
- Add 0.5–1x for strong moat (IP, network effects, switching costs)

## Services Professionnels Multiples (EBITDA-based)

*Hair salons, law firms, consulting firms typically sell on EBITDA.*

| Margin (EBITDA %) | Multiple | Notes |
|------------------|----------|-------|
| <10% | 3–4x | Low margin, commoditized |
| 10–15% | 4–6x | Average service business |
| 15–25% | 6–8x | Strong operations, recurring clients |
| 25–35% | 8–12x | Premium positioning, defensible moat |
| 35%+ | 12–15x | Exceptional: limited supply, high barriers |

**Hair salon benchmarks (closest comp to Alliance Coiffure) :**
- **EBITDA margin typical :** 10–18% (rent, labor, materials = ~75–85% of revenue)
- **Typical multiple :** 4–6x EBITDA
- **Implied price:** If salon = $500K EBITDA @ 5x → **$2.5M**
- **Add-ons (if applicable) :**
  - Strong brand (+0.5–1x)
  - Recurring client base (+0.5–1x)
  - Real estate lease locked in (+0.25–0.5x)
  - Multi-location (+0.5–1x per location)

## Deal Timing & Seasons

| Q | Activity | Buyer Motivation | Best for Founder |
|---|----------|-----------------|-----------------|
| **Q1** | Moderate | Budget reset, new strategies | Fair (avg 10–15% deals close) |
| **Q2** | High | Mid-year acceleration, H2 prep | **Best** (30–40% of annual deals) |
| **Q3** | Moderate | Back-to-school, year-end planning | Fair (15–20% deals) |
| **Q4** | Low | Holiday slowdown, budget exhaustion | **Worst** (10–15% deals; long close in Jan) |

**Implication :** You're in June (Q2 peak) → **aggressive outreach window NOW.**

## Earnout Structures (Market Standards)

| Structure | % of Price | Duration | Triggers | Typical Use |
|-----------|-----------|----------|----------|------------|
| **No earnout** | 0% | — | — | Strong buyer confidence, proven financials |
| **Conservative** | 15–25% | 2–3 years | Revenue/EBITDA growth, retention | Risk-sharing (common in $2–10M deals) |
| **Moderate** | 25–40% | 2–3 years | Revenue, customer retention, integration KPI | Growth-dependent valuation |
| **Aggressive** | 40–60% | 3–4 years | Revenue, EBITDA, operational metrics | Unproven growth, founder stays 2+ years |
| **Seller finance** | 30–50% | 3–5 years | Monthly/quarterly cash flow | When buyer has limited cash |

**Haircut rule :** Discount earnout by 20–30% for time value + execution risk.
- $1M earnout over 3 years @ 25% discount = **~$750K present value today**

## Buyer Motivation by Type

| Buyer Type | Price Sensitivity | Timeline | Multiple Risk |
|------------|------------------|----------|----------------|
| **Roll-up/consolidator** | Low (strategic) | 60–90 days | Will pay 5–7x for network effects |
| **PE firm** | Medium (return-driven) | 90–120 days | Will pay 5–6x if EBITDA growth clear |
| **Strategic (same industry)** | Medium-low | 30–60 days | Will pay for customer sync + cost savings |
| **Founder/individual** | High (cash-constrained) | 120+ days | Will only pay 3–4x, needs seller finance |

**Implication :** Prioritize roll-ups and PE → better price → faster close.

## When Multiples Collapse

**Red flags that kill valuation :**
1. **Customer concentration >60% in top 5** → Discount 20–30%
2. **Churn >15% annual** → Discount 15–25%
3. **Founder dependency** → Discount 20–40% (until replacement hires)
4. **Regulatory risk** → Discount 30–50%
5. **Lease contingency** (salon) → Discount 10–20% if unsecured
6. **Employee turnover** (staff departures) → Discount 15–30%
