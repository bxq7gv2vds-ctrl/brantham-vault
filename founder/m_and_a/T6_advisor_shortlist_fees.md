# Advisor Selection — Who to Hire for €500k ARR Exit

## Do You Need an Advisor?

**For €500k ARR deal** : Optional but recommended.
- **If alone** : Buyer will exploit information asymmetry (lose €200-500k)
- **With advisor** : Professional negotiation, market context, credibility
- **Cost** : €50-150k (justified by defending just 10-20% on price)

---

## Three Types of Advisors

### 1️⃣ M&A Broker (Investment Bank for Small Deals)

**What they do** : Run process, contact 20+ buyers, negotiate LOI/close

**Cost** : 5-8% of deal value
- Example : €3.5M deal = €175-280k fee (usually 50% upfront signing, 50% at close)

**When to use** :
- ✅ You want multi-buyer auction (competition = higher price)
- ✅ You want someone else managing process
- ❌ You prefer speed (brokers take 6+ months)
- ❌ You have 1-2 specific buyers in mind

**Red flags** : Broker won't guarantee buyers, won't start before NDA, wants retainer

---

### 2️⃣ M&A Lawyer (Legal Counsel)

**What they do** : Draft LOI, reps/warranties, manage VDD, close deal

**Cost** : €5-15k/month (typical 4-month process = €20-60k)

**When to use** :
- ✅ Always (non-negotiable for real deal)
- ✅ Manages complex equity structures
- ❌ Will not lead buyer outreach (that's on you/broker)

**Red flags** : Lawyer unclear on deal mechanics, no M&A experience

---

### 3️⃣ CFO/Financial Advisor (Part-time)

**What they do** : Model financials, validate unit economics, answer buyer Q's

**Cost** : €2-5k (one-time or small retainer)

**When to use** :
- ✅ You need financial credibility (auditor backing your numbers)
- ✅ You want scenario models (earnout, tax implications)
- ❌ CFO can't negotiate price

---

## Your Recommended Path (€500k ARR)

### **Minimum Stack**
1. **M&A Lawyer** (€30-50k) — Must have
2. **You + network** — Lead outreach yourself (founders often get better reception)

**Total cost** : €30-50k
**Timeline** : 90 days
**Outcome** : You control process, lawyer manages docs

### **Optimized Stack** (If you value your time)
1. **M&A Broker** (€150-175k, ~5% fee)
2. **M&A Lawyer** (€30-50k)

**Total cost** : €180-225k
**Timeline** : 120-150 days (longer process)
**Outcome** : Multi-buyer auction, higher final price (+€300-500k likely)
