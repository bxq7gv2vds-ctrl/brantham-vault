# Buyer Red Flags by Type

Use this to spot problematic buyers BEFORE signing LOI.

---

## Strategic Buyer (Large SaaS Co, IBM, Salesforce, HubSpot)

### Green Flags ✅
- Recent history of 3+ acquisitions (knows integration)
- CEO personally involved in early conversations
- Clear integration plan within 24 hours of LOI
- Paying 5-8x (confident in synergies)
- Diverse portfolio (not desperate, not betting company on you)

### Red Flags 🚩
| Flag | Why It Matters |
|------|---|
| **Demanded exclusive talks but no signed LOI in 60 days** | Kicking tires, not serious |
| **VP of Product leading (not CEO/CEO leadership)** | Low priority internally |
| **No clear integration plan by month 2 of DD** | Will force fit you = culture clash |
| **Acquisition only to kill competition** | You'll be sunset within 18mo |
| **Financial distress signals (layoffs, stock down 50%)** | Deal falls apart, earnout never pays |
| **Prior acquisitions failed badly (CEO left, churn spike)** | Poor track record |
| **Paying only 2-3x despite high growth** | Underfunding deal = future conflict |
| **Refusing to name who leads integration team** | Disorganized, chaotic integration likely |
| **Insisting on "fire all your team" deal structure** | Not acquiring your product, just killing it |
| **Very long DD (>6 months)** | Cold feet or internal politics |

## PE/Growth Equity Buyer (Thoma Bravo, Insight Partners, Summit)

### Green Flags ✅
- Strong operational improvement team (COO, VP Sales ready)
- Clear 100-day plan before close
- Founder can stay or leave (clarity)
- Fair earnout (achievable milestones)
- History of generating 2-3x returns in 3-5 years

### Red Flags 🚩
| Flag | Why It Matters |
|------|---|
| **Demanding >60% earnout, all at year 3** | You're working for free 3 years |
| **Operating partner team weak (no SaaS expertise)** | Will break your product trying to "optimize" |
| **No 100-day plan by LOI signing** | Chaotic, make-it-up-as-you-go integration |
| **Previous portfolio companies under-performed targets** | Pattern of failed value creation |
| **Insisting on "aggressive" growth (>100% YoY)** | Unrealistic, earnout will never pay |
| **Trying to reduce your team size pre-close** | Will hollow out company |
| **Refusing to define "EBITDA" for earnout** | Ambiguity = dispute later |
| **Fund near end of lifecycle (fund started 2016, now 2026)** | Exit pressure = bad deals |
| **Demanding non-compete > 3 years post-exit** | Don't want you to succeed elsewhere |
| **Multiple executive openings on their team** | Instability, high turnover |

## VC/Growth Buyer (Sequoia, Insight Growth, Gelt Ventures)

### Green Flags ✅
- Founder-friendly (you can stay, build more)
- Stock-heavy deal (they believe in upside)
- Quick DD (30-45 days), confident in thesis
- Clear market thesis (why now, why this category)
- Portfolio strength (can help with customers, hiring)

### Red Flags 🚩
| Flag | Why It Matters |
|------|---|
| **Taking forever to decide (>90 days of soft-shop)** | Partners not aligned internally |
| **Offering only stock (no cash floor)** | Risk all on their ability to exit again |
| **Fund performance poor (down 30% YTD)** | Their LPs may pull capital, fund implodes |
| **Demanding equity in new holdcos** | Wants to own your upside again |
| **No clear vision for your product post-close** | Will make random pivots |
| **Team already over-committed (founded 4 other portfolio cos)** | No bandwidth to help you |
| **Prior acquisitions by fund flamed out** | Pattern of bad bets |
| **Trying to change founding team immediately** | They don't trust you |
| **No customer intro strategy (sales help)** | Just wanted to acquire your tech, not grow it |
| **Valuation all based on stock (illiquid)** | Could be worth $0 if fund fails |

## Financial Buyer / Acqui-Hire

### Green Flags ✅
- Clear role for you (CTO, VP Eng, CEO of subsidiary)
- Team retention plan visible (bonuses, roles)
- Cash floor (50%+ cash, rest vesting)
- No earnout (just hire the team, buy the IP)
- Culture alignment obvious from first chat

### Red Flags 🚩
| Flag | Why It Matters |
|------|---|
| **Wants to "integrate" your product immediately** | You're just a team hire, not a product |
| **No clarity on roles for you or team** | Disorganized, will shuffle people around |
| **Offering only equity/vesting (no signing bonus)** | Money moves around, you're contingent |
| **Founder role = "VP Engineering" (major demotion)** | Playing small ball, depressing |
| **All cash held in earnout tied to "company success"** | Vague definition = you get nothing |
| **Team forced into different orgs immediately** | No cohesion, people leave |
| **Buyer's stock is illiquid / private** | Your equity worth pennies |
| **No mention of what happens in 2-3 years** | They'll churn you out when vesting is done |

## Universal Red Flags (All Buyer Types)

| Flag | Severity | What to Do |
|------|----------|-----------|
| **Won't sign NDA or won't commit to confidentiality** | CRITICAL 🔴 | WALK |
| **Demanding to meet your customers before LOI** | HIGH 🟠 | Offer reference calls only, limited |
| **Asking you to fire people before close** | CRITICAL 🔴 | WALK |
| **Won't provide references from past acquisitions** | HIGH 🟠 | Insist on talking to 2-3 prior CEOs |
| **Valuation assumes unrealistic growth** | MED 🟡 | Clarify earnout triggers, get comfort |
| **Board member has conflict of interest** | CRITICAL 🔴 | Escalate, may need separate legal advice |
| **Requesting "earn-out" reps from you** | HIGH 🟠 | Resist (you can't rep future performance) |
| **Refusing to name deal liabilities** | CRITICAL 🔴 | WALK (hiding something) |
| **Founder of buyer visibly disengaged** | MED 🟡 | Understand power structure internally |
| **Deal contingent on financing** | HIGH 🟠 | Get comfort on funding before committing |

## Buyer Due Diligence (Before LOI)

Ask yourself about potential buyer:

- [ ] Do I want to work for this person 3-5 years?
- [ ] Are their past acquisitions happy? (Call founders)
- [ ] Do they have capital/stability to support us?
- [ ] Will they let me hire/build or micromanage?
- [ ] Is the integration team competent?
- [ ] Are they buying our *product* or just *team*?
- [ ] What happens if we miss earnout targets?
- [ ] Can I trust them (gut check)?

## Decision Tree: Should You Walk?

```
Buyer exhibits CRITICAL flag?
  ├─ YES → WALK (don't waste time)
  └─ NO → Continue

Buyer exhibits 3+ HIGH flags?
  ├─ YES → Re-negotiate hard or WALK
  └─ NO → Continue

Buyer exhibits 2+ MED flags?
  ├─ YES → Get legal review, proceed cautiously
  └─ NO → Move to LOI negotiations

Do you have other buyers?
  ├─ YES → Use alternatives for leverage
  └─ NO → Proceed, but be careful of over-anchoring (you'll take what you can get)
```
