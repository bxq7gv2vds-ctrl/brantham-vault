# Pre-M&A Self-Audit Checklist

**Utilité** : identifier vos propres landmines AVANT qu'un buyer les découvre.

Répondez à chaque question. Si vous avez >3 "Non" ou "Ambigu", ne lancez pas la soft shop encore.

---

## FINANCIALS (5 min)

- [ ] **Cap table 100% clair ?**  
  Chaque shareholder, % exacte, options exercices, conversibles (SAFEs/notes) documentés ?  
  Status: ☐ Yes ☐ No ☐ Messy, cleanup needed

- [ ] **Revenue audit complet ?**  
  P&L dernières 3 années, churn <7%/mois, aucun client = >30% ARR ?  
  Status: ☐ Yes ☐ No ☐ One customer is 40%+ (red flag)

- [ ] **Cash position clair ?**  
  Runway, burn rate, debt/liabilities cachées ?  
  Status: ☐ Yes ☐ No ☐ Unknown

- [ ] **Contracts reviewed by lawyer ?**  
  Customer contracts, employment, vendor agreements (transferability OK ?) ?  
  Status: ☐ Yes ☐ No ☐ Never reviewed

- [ ] **Accounting clean ?**  
  No contingent liabilities, no hidden expenses, no payroll/tax issues ?  
  Status: ☐ Yes ☐ No ☐ Unknown

---

## LEGAL & IP (5 min)

- [ ] **IP 100% owned by company ?**  
  Code, patents, trademarks, domain — founder didn't code it on personal laptop ?  
  Status: ☐ Yes ☐ No ☐ Some external code

- [ ] **No active litigation ?**  
  Customer disputes, employee claims, patent trolls, SEC issues ?  
  Status: ☐ Yes ☐ No ☐ One pending lawsuit

- [ ] **Founders aligned on sale ?**  
  No veto rights, no internal disputes, everyone wants same outcome ?  
  Status: ☐ Yes ☐ All aligned ☐ Disagreement exists

- [ ] **Non-compete / Non-solicit strong ?**  
  If founders leave, they can't restart same business inside 2 years in same geo ?  
  Status: ☐ Yes ☐ No ☐ Very weak

- [ ] **Employment agreements in place ?**  
  Offer letters signed, at-will state (US) or notice period clear (EU) ?  
  Status: ☐ Yes ☐ No ☐ Messy

---

## TECHNICAL (5 min)

- [ ] **No catastrophic tech debt ?**  
  You can hand off codebase to new team without horror stories ?  
  Status: ☐ Yes ☐ No ☐ Complete dumpster fire

- [ ] **Security audits done ?**  
  No hidden breaches, no PII exposures, no critical CVEs in dependencies ?  
  Status: ☐ Yes ☐ No ☐ No clue

- [ ] **Architecture scalable ?**  
  Can handle 10x traffic growth, or at least documented limitations ?  
  Status: ☐ Yes ☐ No ☐ Capacity-capped now

- [ ] **Open source compliant ?**  
  No GPL in core product, no unlicensed dependencies in COGS ?  
  Status: ☐ Yes ☐ No ☐ Unknown

- [ ] **Data quality OK ?**  
  No massive dups, PII secure, error rates normal for vertical ?  
  Status: ☐ Yes ☐ No ☐ Not measured

---

## COMMERCIAL (5 min)

- [ ] **No customer concentration risk ?**  
  Top 3 customers <70% of ARR, or at least long contracts locked in ?  
  Status: ☐ Yes ☐ No ☐ Top 5 = 80% ARR

- [ ] **Sales not founder-dependent ?**  
  Someone else can close deals, or pipeline is clear ?  
  Status: ☐ Yes ☐ No ☐ Only I can sell

- [ ] **Product isn't one person ?**  
  If your genius engineer leaves, product survives 6 months ?  
  Status: ☐ Yes ☐ No ☐ I am that person

- [ ] **Unit economics defensible ?**  
  CAC payback <18m, LTV/CAC >3x, or clear path to profitability ?  
  Status: ☐ Yes ☐ No ☐ Negative unit economics

- [ ] **Churn predictable & low ?**  
  <7% monthly churn, reason for any spikes is known ?  
  Status: ☐ Yes ☐ No ☐ Churn spiking

---

## FOUNDER / TEAM (5 min)

- [ ] **You're not indispensable ?**  
  At least 2 people can run ops, someone can do product direction ?  
  Status: ☐ Yes ☐ No ☐ I'm bottleneck on everything

- [ ] **Team wants to stay post-sale ?**  
  You're not the only one interested in earnout, staff retention matters ?  
  Status: ☐ Yes ☐ No ☐ People queuing to leave

- [ ] **No board drama ?**  
  Investors/board aligned on sale direction, no veto power plays ?  
  Status: ☐ Yes ☐ No ☐ Board split on sale

- [ ] **You're ready to work through closing ?**  
  8 weeks of DD, emails at midnight, queries every day — you can do that ?  
  Status: ☐ Yes ☐ No ☐ Honestly exhausted

- [ ] **Role post-close is defined ?**  
  You want to stay 2+ years, or clean exit? Both require different buyers.  
  Status: ☐ Yes, want to stay ☐ Yes, want to exit ☐ Unsure

---

## DEAL MECHANICS (5 min)

- [ ] **Price ambitions realistic ?**  
  Asking 5-15× ARR multiple if $1-20M ARR ? (10x+ only for outliers)  
  Status: ☐ Yes, realistic ☐ No, asking too much ☐ Unknown

- [ ] **Earnout math is fair ?**  
  <30% of deal, metrics buyer can influence, 12-24 month period ?  
  Status: ☐ Yes ☐ No, >40% earnout ☐ Metrics buyer can't control

- [ ] **You have legal counsel ?**  
  Lawyer (not family friend) who's done M&A before ?  
  Status: ☐ Yes ☐ No ☐ Using free template

- [ ] **You've modeled BATNA ?**  
  If deal falls through, you raise next round (dilution %), or stay independent ?  
  Status: ☐ Yes ☐ No ☐ Haven't thought about it

- [ ] **Timeline is realistic ?**  
  Closing in 14-20 weeks, not 6 (that's rushing, and rushing kills deals) ?  
  Status: ☐ Yes ☐ No, on 6-week timeline ☐ Open-ended

---

## SCORING

Count your YES answers:
- **18-20 YES**: You're ready. Launch soft shop.
- **14-18 YES**: 1-2 cleanup items needed before calling buyers.
- **10-14 YES**: 3-4 major fixes needed. Wait 4-8 weeks.
- **<10 YES**: NOT READY. Fix the red flags first; otherwise this will blow up in DD.

---

## Red Flags That Should Stop You Cold

If you have ANY of these, do NOT launch M&A process yet:

🛑 **Founder misalignment** (co-founder disagrees on sale)  
🛑 **Active litigation** (lawsuit, patent troll, SEC investigation)  
🛑 **IP not owned by company** (key IP with external party)  
🛑 **Churn >10%/month** (customer retention risk is deal-killer)  
🛑 **>40% customer concentration** (buyer can't afford if top customer leaves)  
🛑 **You're burned out** (closing process will destroy you)  
🛑 **You haven't modeled BATNA** (you don't know your walk-away price)

---

## Next Steps

**If you're ready (18+ YES):**
1. Fix any "No" items now (before soft shop)
2. Build buyer list (10-15 targets)
3. Read [Quickstart 90 jours](quickstart_90_days.md)
4. Schedule intro calls with advisors (lawyer, accountant)

**If you're not ready yet (<18 YES):**
1. Identify top 3 issues
2. Make a 4-week plan to fix them
3. Re-run this checklist
4. Then soft shop
