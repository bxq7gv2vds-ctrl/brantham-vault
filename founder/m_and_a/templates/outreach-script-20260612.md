# Outreach Scripts — 3 Modèles

**Utilité** : templates à adapter pour contacter acheteurs potentiels (via avocat ou conseil).

---

## Version 1 : Warm Introduction (Referral)

**Context** : Board member / advisor / CEO mutual friend introduces you.

```
Subject: Introduction — [Your Company] × [Buyer Company] opportunity

Hi [Buyer CFO/CEO name],

[Introducer] mentioned you're focused on [Buyer's strategic priority: vertical expansion/international/product consolidation].

I wanted to introduce [Seller CEO], who's building [Your Company] — a [market descriptor] player in [vertical].

[Seller Company] has:
• $[ARR]M ARR, [growth]% YoY
• [Key strength]: [e.g., "enterprise customer base", "proprietary ML model", "10k+ SMB adoption"]
• [Strategic fit]: solves [specific problem] that your customers face

We suspect there could be meaningful synergies with [Buyer Company]'s [specific product/team/market].
No commitment needed — just wanted to flag this, and happy to introduce if you'd like to explore.

Best,
[Introducer]
```

**Response time** : Usually 2–5 days if warm intro

---

## Version 2 : Cold Outreach (Lawyer-Sent)

**Context** : No warm intro; sent by seller's legal counsel (adds credibility).

```
Subject: Confidential — Strategic Opportunity

Dear [Buyer CFO name],

We represent [Your Company] ("Client"), a leading provider of [product category] serving [customer segment].

Our Client is selectively exploring strategic opportunities, which may include a partnership or transaction with the right strategic buyer.

Given [Buyer Company's] focus on [market/vertical/geographic expansion], we thought your team might find our Client's business of interest.

Attached is a Confidential Overview (NDA required; we can send you the form).

If initial interest exists, we'd welcome a conversation with you or the appropriate member of your team.

Best regards,
[Seller's Lawyer]
[Firm]
[Contact]

---
*This communication and all attachments are confidential and attorney-client privileged.*
```

**Response rate** : Typically 10–20% (1–2 buyers per 10 contact)

**Pro tip** : Send to 5–10 targets simultaneously on day one (gives you backup options if buyer #1 stalls).

---

## Version 3 : Inbound (Buyer Initiated)

**Context** : Buyer reached out asking about acquisition.

```
Subject: RE: Acquisition Interest — Next Steps

Hi [Buyer CEO/VP Strategy],

Thanks for your email — it's great to see that [Buyer Company] is exploring opportunities in this space.

We've been impressed by [Buyer Company's] growth in [market], and there could indeed be meaningful synergies.

To explore this properly, I'd recommend:

1. **NDA + Confidential Overview** (attached)
   → Takes ~2 weeks to review; we're available for initial call (CFO + CTO) within [X] days

2. **Data Room Access** 
   → Once signed, we can give you access to cap table, financials (36m), customer list, tech audit

3. **Timeline**
   → If mutual interest, we'd target close within [90–120 days]

A few questions to align upfront:
- What's your timeline? (Board approval, financing, integration?)
- Are you looking at this as [product bolt-on / vertical expansion / talent acquisition]?
- Rough range you're thinking? (Helps us understand if there's overlap)

Let me know your availability for a call this week.

Best,
[Seller CEO]
```

**Key principle** : Don't give data room access until NDA signed + LOI initiated.

## Email Subject Lines (High Open Rate)

| Scenario | Subject |
|----------|---------|
| Warm intro | "Introduction — [Your Company] × [Buyer Company]" |
| Cold | "Confidential Strategic Opportunity" |
| Buyer initiative | "RE: Acquisition Interest" |
| Follow-up | "[Buyer] — following up on earlier note" |

**Avoid** : "Exciting acquisition opportunity" (spam trigger), "Let's talk about M&A" (too direct).

---

## Timeline Expectations

| Day | Action | Who | Response |
|-----|--------|-----|----------|
| D0 | Send teaser + NDA | Lawyer | N/A |
| D3–5 | Buyer reads + signals interest | Buyer | "Let's explore" |
| D10 | Call: CFO + strategy overview (30 min) | Both | Buyer requests data room |
| D15 | Data room live + LOI discussion begins | Seller | Buyer enters diligence |
| D30 | LOI signed; exclusive negotiation | Seller | Buyer starts deep-dives |
| D90 | Target close | Both | Deal closed |

---

## Contingencies

**If buyer goes quiet after teaser** :
- Follow up day 7 (no response = 50% loss)
- Follow up day 14 (final check)
- Move to next buyer (don't wait >2 weeks)

**If buyer asks for price before LOI** :
- Response: "Price depends on diligence + earnout structure. Once we open the data room, we can discuss range. For now, [give soft range: e.g., '$[30–50]M depending on retention']."

**If buyer wants to meet before NDA** :
- Insist on NDA first (protects your confidentiality + signals buyer seriousness)
