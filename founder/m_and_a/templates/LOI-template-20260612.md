# Letter of Intent (LOI) — Template

**Utilité** : accord de principe non-contraignant qui accélère négociation et signale sérieux buyer.

---

## En-tête

[Buyer Company Name] ("**Buyer**")  
Propose d'acquérir [Seller Company Name] ("**Seller**")

**Effective Date** : [DATE]  
**Confidentiality** : [30–60] days from date

---

## 1. Transaction Structure

**Purchase Price** : $[X] million (Earnout : +$[Y] conditional on [metrics])

**Payment Structure** :
- Cash at close : $[X × (1 – earnout %)]
- Earnout : $[Y] if [metric] reaches [target] by [date]
- [Optional] Seller note : $[Z] @ [%] interest, 3-year amortization

**Timing** : Closing targeted for [90 days from LOI], subject to:
- Satisfactory due diligence
- Financing approval (if contingent)
- Regulatory approvals (if relevant)

---

## 2. Assets & Liabilities

**Assets Included** :
- [ ] All IP (patents, trademarks, code, domain names)
- [ ] Customer contracts + transition support
- [ ] Employee agreements (retention bonuses attached)
- [ ] Capitalized R&D, goodwill

**Liabilities** :
- Seller retains : [e.g., outstanding debt, cap table payouts]
- Buyer assumes : [e.g., warranty obligations to customers]

---

## 3. Purchase Price Adjustments

**Working Capital** : [Estimated $X]; final amount subject to 60-day post-close audit.

**Earnout Trigger** : 
- **Metric** : [ARR / user count / NRR / other]
- **Target** : [number] by [date]
- **Payout** : 100% of earnout if target hit; [graduated] otherwise
- **Buyer Control** : earnout only forfeited if buyer materially changes [product/pricing/marketing]

---

## 4. Reps & Warranties

**Seller represents** (simplified; full definitions in SPA) :
- [ ] Legally organized Delaware C-corp
- [ ] Cap table schedule attached; all shareholder consents obtained
- [ ] No undisclosed liabilities, lawsuits, or regulatory investigations
- [ ] IP fully owned (no third-party claims)
- [ ] Customer contracts transferable; no change-of-control clauses that terminate
- [ ] [Key customer names] not solicited by other buyers (exclusivity period)

**Indemnification** :
- Buyer indemnified for breach via [escrow holdback = 10–15% for 12–18 months]
- Cap on indemnification : [15–25% of purchase price] ; basket : $[50k–100k]

---

## 5. Exclusivity & Confidentiality

**Exclusivity Period** : [30–60 days] from LOI signature. Seller will not:
- Solicit other offers
- Disclose material non-public information to third parties
- Change business operations materially (no new debt, major hires, customer losses)

**Confidentiality** : All information exchanged is under NDA (attached).

---

## 6. Due Diligence & Data Room

**Buyer Access** : 
- Data room access within [5] business days of LOI signature
- Content : cap table, customer list, financial statements (3Y), IP audit, contracts
- Q&A process : buyer questions via email, seller response SLA [2 business days]

**Seller Obligations** :
- Complete financials (P&L, balance sheet, cash flow) for [3 fiscal years]
- Customer churn/NRR analysis
- Employee census (names, titles, vesting, packages)
- List of pending liabilities/claims

---

## 7. Conditions to Closing

**Buyer Conditions Precedent** :
- [ ] Due diligence satisfactory
- [ ] No material adverse change (MAC) in business
- [ ] Key customer [names] retention (>95% ARR)
- [ ] Financing commitment (if contingent)
- [ ] Regulatory approval (if applicable)

**Seller Conditions** :
- [ ] Buyer financing confirmed
- [ ] Board/shareholder approval
- [ ] Key employee retention agreements executed

---

## 8. Binding vs Non-Binding Provisions

**Binding** (enforceable):
- Exclusivity (30–60 days)
- Confidentiality (2–3 years)
- Expense reimbursement (buyer pays seller legal fees if buyer walks)

**Non-Binding** (good faith, not legally enforceable):
- Purchase price
- Earnout structure
- Closing timeline
- Reps & warranties (formalized in SPA)

---

## 9. Termination

**Either party may terminate** if:
- Closing hasn't occurred by [180 days] (extension possible)
- [Material adverse change] in Seller's business
- Buyer financing falls through (if contingent)
- Regulatory blockage

**Termination Fee** : [None typical in non-binding LOI; some buyers request reverse break-up fee]
