# EMAIL OUTREACH — 5 ARCHETYPES ACQUÉREURS

**Usage:** Copy → Customize with company name + metrics → Send D0

---

## 1️⃣ STRATEGIC ACQUIRER (Vertical Integration)
**Archetype:** Large competitor, wants your tech/team/customers  
**Timeline:** 90–120 days | **Interest:** HIGH if complementary  
**Pain Point:** Losing market share, needs faster innovation

**SUBJECT:** Strategic fit opportunity — [YourCo] + [AcquirerCo]

```
Hi [Name],

I wanted to reach out about a potential strategic opportunity that could 
accelerate [AcquirerCo]'s position in [market segment].

We built [YourCo] to solve [specific customer problem] — and it's resonating. 
Last 12 months: $[MRR]k MRR, [X]% month-on-month growth, [Y] enterprise customers 
(including [2-3 brand names if possible]).

Here's why I'm reaching out now:

1. Your [recent acquisition / product launch / market expansion] signals you're 
   consolidating [this segment]. We'd be a natural bolt-on.

2. Our customer base ([list verticals]) overlaps directly with your target 
   accounts. Net-new revenue potential: ~$[X]M in year 1 (no migration needed).

3. Our team ([X engineers with expertise in Y]) could accelerate your [Z] 
   roadmap by 12+ months vs. hiring.

I'm not actively shopping the company, but the timing feels right to explore 
if there's mutual interest.

If this resonates, happy to share more context over a 20-minute call. No NDA 
needed yet — just a conversation.

Best,
[Your name]
[Title]
[Phone]
```

## 2️⃣ PRIVATE EQUITY (Growth Play)
**Archetype:** PE firm sees revenue growth opportunity  
**Timeline:** 120–150 days | **Interest:** MEDIUM if margin upside  
**Pain Point:** Portfolio companies need organic growth lever

**SUBJECT:** Growth investment opportunity — SaaS recurring revenue

```
Hi [Name],

I'm reaching out because [PE Firm]'s portfolio strategy in B2B SaaS aligns 
with what we're building at [YourCo].

Quick snapshot:
• $[MRR]k MRR, [X]% YoY growth, [Y] net new logos last quarter
• Unit economics: CAC $[X]k, LTV $[Y]k, payback [Z] months
• [%] of revenue from enterprise (sticky, long contracts)

We've hit product-market fit and now need capital for growth (sales, support, 
product). We could scale to $[XM] ARR in 24 months with a strong co-investor 
who brings operational expertise + portfolio synergies.

[PE Firm]'s track record with [2-3 existing portfolio companies] makes you 
an ideal partner. We'd love to explore if this fits your thesis.

Open to conversation? I can share financials under NDA.

Best,
[Your name]
```

---

## 3️⃣ CONSOLIDATOR (Roll-up Play)
**Archetype:** Acquires 5–10 SMB SaaS companies, blends them  
**Timeline:** 60–90 days | **Interest:** HIGH if similar revenue size  
**Pain Point:** Finding "add-on" acquisitions in their vertical

**SUBJECT:** Add-on acquisition — [YourCo] for [vertical]

```
Hi [Name],

Your consolidation strategy in [vertical] SaaS is impressive. [Acquirer] now 
has [X] platforms covering [Y use cases] — perfect foundation.

We built [YourCo] to address [specific niche/use case] — we've done it well:
• $[ARR]M ARR, [X]% EBITDA margins, [Y% retention
• [Z] customers in your target verticals
• [Team size] team, all staying post-acquisition

We're a natural add-on to your [most recent acquisition / existing platform]. 
Instead of us competing, we could consolidate under your umbrella and cross-sell 
your existing customer base.

Interested in a 30-minute conversation?

Best,
[Your name]
```

---

## 4️⃣ ACQUI-HIRE (Talent Acquisition)
**Archetype:** Buys team primarily for engineering talent  
**Timeline:** 45–75 days | **Interest:** MEDIUM-HIGH if strong team  
**Pain Point:** Hiring pressure, needs engineering depth

**SUBJECT:** Engineering talent opportunity

```
Hi [Name],

Our team at [YourCo] has deep expertise in [specific tech: "distributed systems", 
"ML infrastructure", "real-time payments", etc.].

We built [YourCo] around this capability, and it's working ([$ARR]M ARR, profitable). 
But we think [YourCo]'s real value to [Acquirer] is the team — [X] engineers with 
[Y] years avg. experience, proven ability to ship production systems.

Given [Acquirer]'s hiring velocity on [department/team], acquiring our team 
(+ the company as foundation for integration) could accelerate your [XYZ] 
roadmap by 12+ months.

We're only interested if the whole team lands somewhere meaningful post-acquisition. 
If that's possible, let's talk.

Best,
[Your name]
```

---

## 5️⃣ STRATEGIC INVESTOR TURNED BUYER (Crossover)
**Archetype:** Existing investor, now wants to acquire majority  
**Timeline:** 30–60 days | **Interest:** VERY HIGH (warm intro)  
**Pain Point:** Portfolio company doing well, wants return + strategic exit

**SUBJECT:** Let's explore taking [YourCo] to the next level

```
Hi [Name],

Following up on your interest in [YourCo]'s growth trajectory.

We've hit inflection ([$MRR]k MRR, [X]% month-over-month), and given your 
network + expertise in this space, I wanted to explore if [Investor/Acquirer] 
sees a next chapter for us.

We could:
1. Raise growth capital for expansion (traditional VC round)
2. Explore a strategic acquisition by [XYZ Acquirer] with founder/team retention
3. Explore a hybrid (minority strategic + growth capital)

Curious your thoughts. Happy to dive into numbers + roadmap.

Best,
[Your name]
```
