## 📊 Dashboard de Reporting

### Frequency Levels
- **Daily** : Uptime, Revenue, Support tickets
- **Weekly** : Churn, Retention, Integration progress
- **Monthly** : Synergies, Customer satisfaction, Cultural metrics
- **Quarterly** : Market share, Brand metrics, Strategic progress

### Alert Thresholds
- **Red Alert** : Immediate action required
  - Revenue drop >10%
  - Churn >10%
  - Employee turnover >15%
  - System downtime >1%

- **Yellow Alert** : Monitoring required
  - Cost overrun >15%
  - Integration delay >2 weeks
  - Customer satisfaction <80%
  - Key talent departure

- **Green Zone** : Normal operations
  - All metrics within target ranges
  - Progress meeting expectations
  - No critical issues
