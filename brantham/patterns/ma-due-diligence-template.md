# M&A Due Diligence Client Template

## Due Diligence Checklist - Client Assessment

### Financial Due Diligence (30 points)

#### Revenue Analysis (10 points)
- [ ] **Revenue Recognition**: Revenue recognition policy review
- [ ] **Customer Concentration**: Top 5 customers analysis (<40% ideal)
- [ ] **Revenue Quality**: Recurring vs. one-time revenue mix
- [ ] **Growth Validation**: YoY growth verification across 3 years
- [ ] **Seasonality**: Seasonal revenue patterns analysis

#### Financial Health (10 points)
- [ ] **Profitability**: EBITDA margins trend analysis
- [ ] **Cash Flow**: Free cash flow validation
- [ ] **Debt Structure**: Debt covenant review and maturity schedule
- [ ] **Working Capital**: Working capital efficiency assessment
- [ ] **Tax Compliance**: Tax filing status and liabilities

#### Historical Performance (10 points)
- [ ] **Financial Statements**: Audited P&L, balance sheet, cash flow (3 years)
- [ ] **Budget vs Actual**: Budget adherence analysis
- [ ] **Capital Expenditures**: CAPEX efficiency and ROI
- [ ] **Contingent Liabilities**: Legal and environmental liabilities
- [ ] **Related Party Transactions**: Business with owners/directors

### Operational Due Diligence (25 points)

#### Business Model (8 points)
- [ ] **Value Proposition**: Core value validation
- [ ] **Unit Economics**: Customer acquisition cost vs. lifetime value
- [ ] **Scalability**: Growth potential assessment
- [ ] **Pricing Power**: Pricing strategy effectiveness

#### Infrastructure (7 points)
- [ ] **Technology Stack**: Modernity and scalability assessment
- [ ] **IP Ownership**: Patents, trademarks, proprietary technology
- [ ] **System Integration**: IT system dependencies and vulnerabilities
- [ ] **Physical Assets**: Property, equipment, and lease terms

#### Operations (10 points)
- [ ] **Supply Chain**: Key supplier dependencies and alternatives
- [ ] **Manufacturing**: Production capacity and efficiency
- [ ] **Quality Control**: Quality assurance processes
- [ ] **Regulatory Compliance**: Industry-specific regulations

### Market Due Diligence (20 points)

#### Market Position (10 points)
- [ ] **Market Share**: Competitive positioning analysis
- [ ] **Competitive Landscape**: Direct/indirect competitors analysis
- [ ] **Growth Trends**: Market growth rate and projections
- [ ] **Customer Loyalty**: Retention rates and NPS scores

#### Strategic Fit (10 points)
- [ ] **Synergy Potential**: Cost/revenue overlap analysis
- [ ] **Market Access**: New markets/customers access
- [ ] **Technology Integration**: Complementary technology assessment
- [ ] **Brand Value**: Brand equity and reputation

### Legal Due Diligence (15 points)

#### Corporate Structure (7 points)
- [ ] **Corporate Documents**: Articles, bylaws, board minutes
- [ ] **Shareholder Agreements**: Rights and restrictions
- [ ] **Capitalization Table**: Share ownership and voting rights
- [ ] **Compliance**: Corporate formalities compliance

#### Legal Risks (8 points)
- [ ] **Litigation**: Active lawsuits and claims
- [ ] **Regulatory**: Compliance with all regulations
- [ ] **Contracts**: Material contracts review
- [ ] **Intellectual Property**: IP protection and infringement risks

### Human Resources (10 points)

#### Team Assessment (6 points)
- [ ] **Key Personnel**: Retention risks of key employees
- [ ] **Organizational Structure**: Team effectiveness and gaps
- [ ] **Culture Fit**: Cultural compatibility assessment
- [ ] **Compensation**: Compensation competitiveness

#### Labor Issues (4 points)
- [ ] **Union Relations**: Union agreements and relations
- [ ] **Employment Contracts**: Key employee contracts review
- [ ] **Benefits**: Benefits packages and costs
- [ ] **Compliance**: Labor law compliance
