# M&A Target Evaluation Framework

## Core Criteria Scoring (0-10)

### Strategic Fit (30%)
- **Market Alignment**: Does target serve same niche/expansion area?
- **Synergy Potential**: Cost/revenue synergy estimates?
- **Strategic Intent**: Matches Brantham's sector focus?

### Financial Health (25%)
- **Revenue Stability**: Consistent growth >3 years?
- **Profitability**: Positive EBITDA margins?
- **Cash Flow**: Positive free cash flow?

### Operational Excellence (25%)
- **Management Team**: Proven track record?
- **Technology Stack**: Modern/competitive infrastructure?
- **Customer Concentration**: <40% from top 3 clients?

### Market Position (20%)
- **Market Share**: Leader in segment?
- **Growth Trajectory**: >15% CAGR?
- **Competitive Moat**: Defensible advantages?

## Deal Attractiveness Matrix

| Score Range | Classification | Action Required |
|-------------|----------------|----------------|
| 8.0-10.0    | Prime Target   | Pursue aggressively |
| 6.0-7.9     | Strong Contender | Competitive bid |
| 4.0-5.9     | Marginal Fit   | Conditional proceed |
| <4.0        | Pass           | Do not pursue |

## Red Flags (Automatic -2 points each)
- Customer concentration >50%
- Revenue decline YoY
- Key person dependency
- Litigation exposure
- Environmental liabilities
- Regulatory compliance issues

## Decision Workflow
1. Initial screen: Score >6.0 and no major red flags
2. Deep dive: Score 7.0+ with comprehensive due diligence
3. Final decision: Executive committee approval for scores >8.0
