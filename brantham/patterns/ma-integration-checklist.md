# Post-Acquisition Integration Checklist

## 30-60-90 Days Integration Plan

### Phase 1: First 30 Days (Stabilization)

#### Immediate Actions (First 48 Hours)
- [ ] **Integration Team Setup**
  - Assign integration lead from Brantham
  - Identify key personnel from target
  - Create communication protocol
- [ ] **IT Systems Assessment**
  - Review IT infrastructure compatibility
  - Address immediate security concerns
  - Plan system integration timeline
- [ ] **Financial Controls**
  - Establish reporting protocols
  - Implement expense controls
  - Review banking relationships

#### First Week
- [ ] **People Integration**
  - Meet with key management team
  - Communicate integration plan to employees
  - Address immediate HR concerns
- [ ] **Customer Communication**
  - Notify key customers of acquisition
  - Maintain service continuity
  - Collect customer feedback
- [ ] **Supplier Assessment**
  - Review key supplier contracts
  - Identify potential renegotiation opportunities
  - Ensure supply chain continuity

#### First Month
- [ ] **Financial Integration**
  - Consolidate financial reporting
  - Implement shared services
  - Optimize cash management
- [ ] **Brand Strategy**
  - Develop unified brand guidelines
  - Plan marketing communications
  - Ensure consistent customer experience
- [ ] **Operations Review**
  - Document current processes
  - Identify quick wins
  - Plan process optimization

### Phase 2: 60-90 Days (Optimization)

#### Month 2
- [ ] **Systems Integration**
  - Implement ERP/HRM systems
  - Consolidate IT infrastructure
  - Standardize reporting tools
- [ ] **Process Optimization**
  - Map end-to-end processes
  - Identify redundancies
  - Implement best practices
- [ ] **Product/Service Alignment**
  - Review product portfolios
  - Identify cross-selling opportunities
  - Plan product roadmap integration

#### Month 3
- [ ] **Cultural Integration**
  - Conduct cultural assessment
  - Implement integration activities
  - Address cultural gaps
- [ ] **Market Strategy**
  - Develop go-to-market strategy
  - Identify market opportunities
  - Plan brand positioning
- [ ] **Risk Assessment**
  - Review compliance requirements
  - Identify operational risks
  - Implement mitigation plans

### Integration Success Metrics

#### Financial Metrics
- **Cost Savings**: Track actual vs. projected synergies
- **Revenue Growth**: Monitor cross-selling performance
- **EBITDA Margin**: Track margin improvement
- **Cash Flow**: Ensure positive cash flow generation

#### Operational Metrics
- **Customer Satisfaction**: NPS/CSAT scores
- **Employee Retention**: Key talent retention rate
- **Process Efficiency**: Cycle time improvements
- **System Uptime**: IT system availability

#### Strategic Metrics
- **Market Share**: Combined market position
- **Product Portfolio**: New products launched
- **Geographic Reach**: Expanded market coverage
- **Innovation Pipeline**: R&D output

### Risk Management

**High Priority Risks**
- **Customer Loss**: Proactive communication + retention programs
- **Key Talent Departure**: Retention packages + career development
- **System Downtime**: Parallel runs + phased implementation
- **Regulatory Issues**: Compliance review + legal consultation

**Mitigation Strategies**
- **Change Management**: Regular town halls + feedback loops
- **Communication Plan**: Multi-channel communication + transparency
- **Integration Timeline**: Phased approach + milestones
- **Stakeholder Management**: Regular updates + expectations management

### Communication Plan

**Internal Communications**
- **Weekly Updates**: All-hands meetings + newsletters
- **Department Briefings**: Team-specific integration plans
- **Feedback Mechanism**: Anonymous reporting system
- **Success Stories**: Share quick wins and progress

**External Communications**
- **Customer Updates**: Regular business updates
- **Supplier Communications**: Partnership continuity
- **Market Communications**: Brand positioning + messaging
- **Investor Relations**: Integration progress reports

### Budget Tracking

**One-Time Costs**
- IT integration expenses
- Legal & advisory fees
- Change management programs
- Employee retention bonuses

**Ongoing Costs**
- Integration management team
- System maintenance
- Communication programs
- Training & development

**Cost Savings Timeline**
- **Month 1-3**: Planning & initial costs
- **Month 4-6**: First wave of savings
- **Month 7-12**: Major synergies realized
- **Year 2+**: Full optimization benefits

### Governance Structure

**Integration Steering Committee**
- Brantham CEO + Target CEO
- Functional heads (Finance, Ops, HR, Tech)
- Integration project lead
- Monthly review meetings

**Working Groups**
- **Financial Integration**: Controllers + FP&A
- **Operations Integration**: COO + Operations Managers
- **Technology Integration**: CIO + IT Teams
- **People Integration**: CHRO + HR Teams

### Critical Success Factors

1. **Leadership Alignment**: Unified vision + commitment
2. **Communication Excellence**: Transparency + frequency
3. **Change Management**: Employee engagement + support
4. **Execution Discipline**: Milestone tracking + accountability
5. **Customer Focus**: Business continuity + service quality
