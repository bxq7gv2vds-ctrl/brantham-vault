# Matrice de Risques - Acquisition Tech

**Objectif** : Identifier et quantifier les risques clés avant acquisition  
**Cadre** : Impact × Probabilité avec indicateurs précurseurs  
**Usage** : Due diligence et planning post-acquisition

---

## 📈 Risk Assessment Dashboard

### Scoring System (1-5)
- **1** : Negligible impact/low probability
- **2** : Minor impact/low probability  
- **3** : Moderate impact/moderate probability
- **4** : High impact/moderate probability
- **5** : Critical impact/high probability

### Risk Heat Map
```
Impact →
    5 | 🟦   🟦   🟥   🟥   🟥
    4 | 🟩   🟦   🟥   🟥   🟥
    3 | 🟩   🟩   🟦   🟦   🟥
    2 | 🟩   🟩   🟩   🟦   🟦
    1 | 🟩   🟩   🟩   🟩   🟩
      └─────────────────────────
        1   2   3   4   5 ← Probability
```

**🟥 Critical (4-5 Impact × 4-5 Probability)**  
**🟦 High Risk (Any 4-5 score)**  
**🟩 Monitor (All scores 1-3)**

### Top 5 Critical Risks
1. **Legacy tech debt** (4×3) - Requires immediate tech plan
2. **Revenue concentration** (5×2) - High impact, medium probability
3. **IP ownership disputes** (5×1) - Critical if occurs
4. **Competitive disruption** (4×3) - Active monitoring required
5. **Key person dependency** (5×3) - Succession planning needed

## 🛡️ Risk Mitigation Framework

### Pre-Acquisition Actions
1. **Tech Audit**: Complete infrastructure review + scalability testing
2. **Financial Deep Dive**: 3-month cash flow projection + stress testing
3. **Legal Review**: All contracts + IP audit + compliance check
4. **Customer Interviews**: Reference calls + retention assessment
5. **Team Assessment**: Key retention risks + cultural fit analysis

### Post-Acquisition Integration Plan
- **Day 0-30**: Integration roadmap, communication plan, team alignment
- **Day 30-90**: System integration, process harmonization, customer comms
- **Day 90-180**: Technology consolidation, cost optimization, growth acceleration

### Red Flag Triggers
- **Immediate pause**: Critical security breach, major customer loss
- **Reconsideration**: Revenue drop >15%, key person departure
- **Speed up**: Competitive threat, regulatory change
- **Adjust valuation**: Material risk discovery

## 🎯 Risk Response Strategy

### Avoid
- Skip acquisitions with >2 critical risks
- Avoid markets with high regulatory uncertainty
- Steer clear of tech with heavy legacy dependencies

### Mitigate
- Implement monitoring systems for high risks
- Create contingency plans for medium risks
- Allocate resources for risk reduction

### Transfer
- Purchase insurance for specific risks
- Outsource non-core functions
- Share risk through partnerships

### Accept
- Low impact risks (<2 score)
- Mitigation costs outweigh benefits
- Strategic necessity despite risks

## 🚨 Critical Risk Questions Due Diligence

### Technology Review
- What is the true maintenance cost ratio?
- Are there any single points of failure?
- How scalable is the current architecture?
- What security vulnerabilities exist?

### Financial Analysis
- Is revenue sustainable or temporary?
- What are the real cash burn drivers?
- Are there any hidden liabilities?
- How predictable is revenue?

### Legal Assessment
- Who truly owns the IP?
- Are there any pending lawsuits?
- Is compliance truly current?
- What contract risks exist?

### Operational Check
- What happens if key people leave?
- How stable is the customer base?
- Can operations handle growth?
- What are the real cost synergies?

### Market Reality
- Is competitive pressure increasing?
- Is the market still growing?
- Are technological shifts threatening?
- What's the real market position?
