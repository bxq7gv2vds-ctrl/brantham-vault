## 📊 2. Earn-Out Structure

### Revenue-Based Earn-Out (Option 1)
```markdown
**Target Revenue** : $[montant] over 24 months
**Payment Schedule**:
- Q1 Year 1: $[montant] ([percentage]%)
- Q2 Year 1: $[montant] ([percentage]%)
- Q3 Year 1: $[montant] ([percentage]%)  
- Q4 Year 1: $[montant] ([percentage]%)
- Q1 Year 2: $[montant] ([percentage]%)  
- Q2 Year 2: $[montant] ([percentage]%)  
- Q3 Year 2: $[montant] ([percentage]%)  
- Q4 Year 2: $[montant] ([percentage]%)

**Adjustments**:
- Revenue recognized per ASC 606
- Net of returns and cancellations
- Excludes one-time or non-recurring revenue
```

### EBITDA-Based Earn-Out (Option 2)
```markdown
**Target EBITDA** : $[montant] cumulative over 24 months
**Payment Structure**:
- Base payment: 75% of achieved EBITDA
- Multiple adjustment: 1.0x for <[target], 1.2x for >[target]
- Caps/Floors: Min $[montant], Max $[montant]

**EBITDA Definition**:
- Operating income + depreciation/amortization
- Exclude restructuring costs, one-time items
- Include normalized opex (fair market value)
```

### Hybrid Structure (Recommended)
```markdown
**Component 1 - Revenue** : 60% of consideration
- Target: $[montant] revenue over 24 months
- Multiple: [multiple]x revenue up to target

**Component 2 - Profitability** : 40% of consideration  
- Target: $[montant] EBITDA over 24 months
- Multiple: [multiple]x EBITDA

**Performance Triggers**:
- Both components must achieve minimum [percentage]%
- Exceeding one component doesn't compensate shortfall in other
- Review committee for dispute resolution
```

## 🛡️ 4. Représentations et Garanties

### Standard Representations
- **Formation & Authority** : Entity legally formed, authority to enter agreement
- **Capitalization** : Correct ownership structure, no undisclosed securities
- **Financial Statements** : Accurate and fairly presented in all material respects
- **Compliance** : All laws, regulations, contracts complied with
- **Intellectual Property** : Valid ownership, no infringement, no material claims
- **Employees** : Proper classification, valid agreements, no major disputes
- **Litigation** : No pending or threatened material litigation
- **Taxes** : Filed returns, paid taxes, no material issues
- **Contracts** : All material contracts valid, no breaches, no terminations
- **Environmental** : No violations, no known contamination

### Special Tech-Specific Representations
- **Technology Assets** : All systems functional, documented, no backdoors
- **Data Security** : Adequate security measures, no known breaches
- **Software Licenses** : All licenses valid, no material violations
- **Open Source** : Compliance with all open source licenses
- **Privacy** : Compliance with GDPR, CCPA, other privacy laws
- **Cloud Services** : All cloud contracts valid, termination rights understood

## 🔧 5. Covenants (Engagements)

### During Due Diligence
- **Information Access** : Provide all requested information, access to facilities
- **Employees** : No material changes to compensation, benefits, structure
- **Contracts** : No material changes to existing customer/supplier agreements
- **Operations** : Continue normal course of business
- **Public Statements** : No material announcements without prior approval

### Pre-Closing Covenants
- **Business as Usual** : Maintain normal operations, no major changes
- **Asset Preservation** : No disposition of material assets without consent
- **Debt** : No incurrence of new debt, no material changes to existing debt
- **Litigation** : No settlement of material litigation without consent
- **Contracts** : No material new contracts without disclosure
