# Workflow Acquisition M&A - Target Sourcing to Closing

## 🎯 Phase 0: Sourcing & Qualification (Jours 1-7)

### 0.1 Lead Generation
- **Sources**: AJ/BODACC/Papers API alerts
- **Volume**: 50+ targets/week
- **Filters**: Score >50, employees 10-100, recent distress

### 0.2 Initial Qualification (30 min/target)
```markdown
**Screening Questions**:
- ✅ SIREN valid & active
- ✅ Effectif 20-50 salariés
- ✅ Procédure type: redressement/liquidation
- ✅ CA > €1M (last available)
- ✅ Pas de passif environnemental connu

**Quick Score**:
- Pappers score >50
- Geographic proximity
- Sector fit with thesis
```

### 0.3 Priority Ranking
- **P0**: Score 75-85, deadline <14j
- **P1**: Score 70-75, deadline <30j  
- **P2**: Score 60-70, no deadline pressure

## 🔍 Phase 1: Due Diligence Deep Dive (Jours 8-21)

### 1.1 Financial DD (48h)
**Actions**:
- [ ] Extract last 3 years financials
- [ ] Calculate EBITDA margins
- [ ] Analyze cash flow trends
- [ ] Identify debt structure
- [ ] Working capital analysis

**Output**: Financial scorecard + valuation range

### 1.2 Legal DD (24h)
**Actions**:
- [ ] Check BODACC for litiges
- [ ] Verify ownership structure
- [ ] Review key contracts
- [ ] Environmental compliance
- [ ] Employment contracts review

**Output**: Legal risk assessment

### 1.3 Operational DD (24h)
**Actions**:
- [ ] Market position analysis
- [ ] Competitive landscape
- [ ] Customer concentration risk
- [ ] Supplier dependencies
- [ ] Technology audit

**Output**: Operational due diligence report

### 1.4 Management Assessment (12h)
**Actions**:
- [ ] CEO background check
- [ ] Key team retention risk
- [ ] Turnaround experience
- [ ] Exit intentions

**Output**: People risk analysis

## 💡 Phase 2: Valuation & Deal Structuring (Jours 22-35)

### 2.1 Valuation Model
**Discounted Cash Flow (DCF)**:
- Base case: 3-5 year projection
- Stress case: -20% revenue scenarios
- Recovery case: +15% turnaround scenarios

**Multiple Analysis**:
- Sector comparables (current multiples)
- Asset-based valuation (floor)
- Distress discount (60-80% of fair value)

### 2.2 Deal Structure Design
**Base Structure**:
- Cash at closing: 60-70%
- Earn-out: 30-40% (24 months)
- Payment terms: Net 30-60 days

**Earn-out Mechanics**:
- Revenue milestones: 60% weighting
- EBITDA targets: 40% weighting
- Caps: 120% of earn-out value
- Floors: 80% of earn-out value

### 2.3 Risk Allocation
**Seller Warranties**:
- Financials accuracy (1 year)
- No hidden liabilities
- IP ownership confirmation
- Regulatory compliance

**Break Fees**:
- Acquiror termination: 5% of price
- MAC termination: No break fee
- Regulatory rejection: No break fee

## 🤝 Phase 3: Negotiation & LOI (Jours 36-49)

### 3.1 Initial Approach
**Contact Strategy**:
- Mandataire/juge-commissaire
- CEO (if available)
- Financial advisor

**Opening Position**:
- Price: 60-70% of fair value
- Exclusivity: 30 days
- DD access: Full data room

### 3.2 LOI Preparation
**Core Terms**:
- Purchase price range
- Exclusivity period
- Due diligence period
- Conditions precedent
- Exclusive negotiating period

**Documentation**:
- Confidentiality agreement
- Non-solicitation
- Exclusivity letter
- Letter of intent

### 3.3 Term Sheet Negotiation
**Key Negotiation Points**:
- Price vs earn-out split
- Warranty coverage
- Liability allocation
- Management retention
- Integration timeline

**Red Lines**:
- Maximum price: 80% of target valuation
- Minimum management retention: 12 months
- No environmental liability assumption

## 📋 Phase 4: Definitive Agreements (Jours 50-70)

### 4.1 Purchase Agreement Drafting
**Sections**:
- Description of assets
- Purchase price and payment
- Representations and warranties
- Covenants
- Conditions to closing
- Termination provisions
- Dispute resolution

### 4.2 Closing Documentation
**Legal Package**:
- Share purchase agreement
- Asset purchase agreement
- Employment contracts
- Non-compete agreements
- Consulting agreements

### 4.3 Financing Documentation
**Debt Financing**:
- Term sheet with bank
- Security documents
- Intercreditor agreements

**Equity Financing**:
- Subscription agreements
- Shareholders' agreement

## 🚪 Phase 5: Closing & Integration (Jours 71-90)

### 5.1 Closing Conditions Verification
**Financial Conditions**:
- Final working capital calculation
- Debt verification
- Tax clearance certificates

**Legal Conditions**:
- Regulatory approvals
- Third-party consents
- No material adverse change

### 5.2 Closing Execution
**Day Activities**:
- Funds transfer
- Asset transfer
- Signing ceremonies
- Press release preparation
- Employee communications

### 5.3 Post-Closing Integration
**Week 1-4**:
- Transition team setup
- System integration planning
- Customer communications
- Employee retention meetings

**Month 1-3**:
- Operational integration
- Cost synergy realization
- Revenue optimization
- Management transition
