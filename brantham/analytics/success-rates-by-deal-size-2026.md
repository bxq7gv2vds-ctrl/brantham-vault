# Taux de Succès Deals par Taille - 2026

## 📊 Overview par Catégorie

| Taille Deal | Volume Annuel | Taux Succès | Temps Moyen | Investissement |
|-------------|---------------|-------------|-------------|----------------|
| **Petit (<5M€)** | 15-20 deals | 65% | 60-90 jours | 100-500K€ |
| **Moyen (5-20M€)** | 8-12 deals | 45% | 90-120 jours | 500K€-2M€ |
| **Grand (>20M€)** | 3-5 deals | 35% | 120-180 jours | 2M€-10M€ |

---

## 🎯 Petits Deals (<5M€) - High Velocity

### Profil Type
- **Entreprise**: TPE/PME 20-100 salariés
- **Secteur**: Services, local, niche
- **Processus**: Simplifié, rapide
- **Decision makers**: 1-2 personnes

### Success Factors
- **Speed to market**: <60 jours diligence
- **Price flexibility**: 10-15% négociable
- **Simple structure**: Cash deal minimal
- **Local expertise**: Deal récurrent

### Risques
- **Scalabilité limitée**: Potentiel de croissance faible
- **Dépendance personnelle**: Success tied to founders
- **Marché fragmenté**: Many competitors

### Strategy
- **Volume**: Plus de deals, plus petits
- **Speed**: Process ultra-rapide
- **Due diligence**: Focus sur cash flow
- **Pricing**: Market-based, flexible

---

## 🏢 Deals Moyens (5-20M€) - Balanced Approach

### Profil Type
- **Entreprise**: PME établie 50-200 salariés
- **Secteur**: Tech, industrielle, services
- **Processus**: Standard diligence
- **Decision makers**: Comité + investisseur

### Success Factors
- **Strategic fit**: Clear synergies
- **Management team**: Experienced and stable
- **Financial health**: Positive EBITDA
- **Market position**: Top 3 segment

### Risques
- **Dilution**: Higher complexity
- **Market timing**: Economic sensitivity
- **Integration challenges**: Cultural fit

### Strategy
- **Quality**: Rigorous due diligence
- **Relationship**: Long-term partnership
- **Value creation**: Active post-acquisition
- **Timing**: Market cycle awareness

---

## 🏗️ Grands Deals (>20M€) - Strategic Bets

### Profil Type
- **Entreprise**: Grande PME/ETI 200+ salariés
- **Secteur**: Platform, market leader
- **Processus**: Comprehensive diligence
- **Decision makers**: Board + advisors

### Success Factors
- **Market dominance**: Leadership position
- **Platform potential**: Network effects
- **Management**: Proven track record
- **Financials**: Scale economics

### Risques
- **Regulatory hurdles**: Antitrust, sector-specific
- **Financing complexity**: Debt/equity mix
- **Integration scale**: Large teams, systems
- **Economic sensitivity**: Revenue diversification

### Strategy
- **Patience**: 6-12 months timeline
- **Expertise**: Specialized advisors
- **Risk management**: Structured deal terms
- **Long-term vision**: 5-10 year horizon
