# Analyse Concurrentielle - Entreprise Tech

**Objectif** : Évaluation approfondie des concurrents et identification d'avantages  
**Cadre** : Matrice 2×2 avec analyse stratégique  
**Applications** : Due diligence, pricing strategy, market positioning

---

## 🎯 Framework d'Analyse Concurrentielle

### Dimensions d'Évaluation
```
Market Position →
    High      |     Innovators    |     Leaders
    Share     |  (Disruptive)     |  (Established)
-------------+-------------------+------------------
    Low      |     Challengers   |     Niche Players  
    Share     |  (Fast-growing)  |  (Specialized)
             |                   |
        Low  ←--- Technology Differentiation ---→ High
```

## 📊 Metrics de Surveillance

### Key Performance Indicators
- **Market Share** : Track quarterly changes
- **Competitor Pricing** : Monitor pricing changes
- **Feature Releases** : Track innovation timeline
- **Customer Churn** : Monitor loss to competitors
- **Talent Acquisition** : Monitor competitor hiring

### Early Warning Signals
- **Competitor Funding** : Large rounds signal potential aggression
- **Executive Moves** : Key hires indicate strategic shifts
- **Product Announcements** : Major releases signal competitive moves
- **Partnership Activity** : New alliances indicate market strategy
