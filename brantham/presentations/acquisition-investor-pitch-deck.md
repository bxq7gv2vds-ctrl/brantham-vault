# Pitch Deck - Acquisition Tech

**Objectif** : Lever $[montant] pour acquérir [Nom de l'entreprise]  
**Durée** : 15-20 slides  
**Format** : Canvases prêts pour PowerPoint/Google Slides

---

## 📊 Deck Structure - 20 Slides

### 1. Titre (1 slide)
- **Logo** + Tagline "[Positionnement unique]"
- **Montant recherché** : $[montant] @ [valuation]
- **Use of funds** : [montant] acquisition, [montant] growth

### 2. The Problem (1 slide)

**Slide 2: Technology Moat**
- [Proprietary technology]: [patent/IP]
- [Architecture advantage]: [scalability]
- [Data advantage]: [network effects]

### 4. Market Opportunity (2 slides)
**Slide 1: Market Size**
- TAM: $[montant]B
- SAM: $[montant]B  
- SOM: $[montant]M

### 5. Business Model (2 slides)
**Slide 1: Revenue Streams**

### 8. Team (2 slides)

**Slide 2: Advisory Board**

## 🎨 Design Guidelines

### Visual Identity
- **Color scheme**: 2-3 brand colors max
- **Fonts**: 1-2 professional fonts
- **Imagery**: High-quality photos/graphics
- **Consistency**: Same template throughout

### Data Visualization
- **Charts**: Simple, clear, labeled
- **Numbers**: Highlight key metrics
- **Icons**: Use sparingly, consistently
- **White space**: Ample margins

### Storytelling Elements
- **Problem/Solution**: Clear narrative arc
- **Traction**: Evidence-based claims
- **Vision**: Inspiring but realistic
- **Call to action**: Specific ask
