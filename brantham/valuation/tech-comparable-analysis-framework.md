# Analyse Comparables - Valorisation Tech

**Objectif** : Construire un peer group pour multiples sectoriels et benchmarking  
**Précision** : ±10% avec données récentes et peer group pertinent  
**Applications** : Due diligence, pricing strategy, market positioning

---


## 📊 Multiples de Valorisation Sectoriels

### SaaS Sector Multiples (2026)

```markdown
- <20% growth: 4-6x

- Unprofitable: 3-5x
```

```markdown
- EV/Revenue: 1-3x
```

## 🔄 Normalisation des Multiples

### Revenue Multiple Normalization
```markdown
**Adjustment Factors**:
- **Growth Rate**: ±0.5x per 10% growth difference
- **Profitability**: ±1x per 10% EBITDA difference  
- **Market Position**: ±1-2x for leader/challenger
- **Revenue Quality**: ±0.5x for recurring revenue mix
- **Technology Moat**: ±0.5-1x for IP advantage
- **Geographic Risk**: ±0.5x for international exposure

**Example Calculation**:
Base multiple: 8x
Growth adjustment: +1x (40% vs 30% average)
Profitability: +0.5x (15% vs 10% EBITDA)
Market position: +1x (leader vs average)
Normalized multiple: 11x
```

### EBITDA Multiple Normalization
```markdown
**EBITDA Multiple Adjustments**:
- **High Growth**: +2x per 20% above industry average
- **Low Growth**: -2x per 20% below industry average
- **Quality of EBITDA**: ±1x for one-time items
- **Capital Intensity**: ±1x for Capex requirements
- **Revenue Visibility**: ±1x for contract quality

**EBITDA Quality Indicators**:
- Recurring revenue >80%: +1x
- Long-term contracts >1 year: +0.5x
- Customer concentration <20%: +0.5x
- Regulatory risk: -1x
```

## 📊 Data Sources et Vérification

### Primary Sources
```markdown
**Public Markets**:
- SEC filings (10-K, 8-K)
- Earnings transcripts
- Investor presentations
- Market data providers (Bloomberg, Refinitiv)

**Private Markets**:
- PitchBook, CB Insights
- Deal databases (PitchBook, CB Insights)
- Private company disclosures
- M&A advisory reports

**Transaction Precedents**:
- SEC S-1 filings for M&A
- Press releases with terms
- Industry reports
- Broker research reports
```

### Data Quality Assessment
```markdown
**A-Rated Data**:
- Public companies with quarterly reports
- Recent transactions (within 6 months)
- Verified financial statements

**B-Rated Data**:  
- Private company estimates
- Older transactions (6-18 months)
- Broker estimates

**C-Rated Data** (use with caution):
- Old transaction data
- Unverified company metrics
- Different business models
```

```excel
# Peer Group Analysis Template
A1: "Peer Group Valuation Analysis"

A10: "Weighted Average", B10: =AVERAGEIFS(B4:B9, F4:F9, "A"), C10: =AVERAGEIFS(C4:C9, F4:F9, "A"), D10: =AVERAGEIFS(D4:D9, F4:F9, "A"), E10: =AVERAGEIFS(E4:E9, F4:F9, "A")

A12: "Normalization Factors", B12: "Factor", C12: "Our Value", D12: "Peer Value", E12: "Adjustment"
A13: "Growth Rate", B13: "Weight", C13: [our growth], D13: =AVERAGE(C4:C9), E13: =(C13-D13)*B13/10
A14: "Profitability", B14: "Weight", C14: [our ebitda], D14: =AVERAGE(D4:D9), E14: =(C14-D14)*B14/10
A15: "Market Position", B15: "Weight", C15: [our position], D15: =AVERAGE(peer positions), E15: =(C15-D15)*B15/10

A18: "Price Range", B18: "Low", C18: =B18_base*[E17], D18: "High", E18: =B18_upper*[E17]
```
